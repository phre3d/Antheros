# Clerk

**Parent Class:** [[classes/mundane/mundane|Mundane]]

Anyone that performs work that is not labour-intensive. For example a lower-level store employee or office worker.

---

# Role Features

**Extra Skills.** At level 2, 5 and 10, the Clerk can choose one of *Investigation*, *Perception*, or *Persuasion*.  A skill may be selected twice to provide expertise.
