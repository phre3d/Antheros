# Description
These deep green crystals are vibrant and richly colored, pulsing with a soft, inner light. Ranging in size from that of a pebble to as large as a melon, each Earthheart Emerald is a marvel of nature's craftsmanship. They are raw and rugged, yet hold an untamed beauty that speaks of the elemental power locked within.

The value of an Earthheart Emerald greatly depends on its size, quality, and the amount of energy it holds. Prices could vary depending on the setting, availability of the emeralds, and demand for such a power source.

# Properties
## Physical Characteristics

## Properties
**Source of Power:**  The energy within an Earthheart Emerald is drawn from the elemental heart of the world itself. Over centuries, the earth's innate elemental power is absorbed and condensed within these crystals, resulting in a stone capable of holding vast amounts of energy. This energy is similar to geothermal power on a highly concentrated scale.

## Half Life / Deterioration / Expiration
An Earthheart Emerald can hold its charge indefinitely until the energy is extracted. Once the energy has been drained, the emerald becomes a regular stone.
