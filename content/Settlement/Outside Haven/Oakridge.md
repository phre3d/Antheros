# Description
# Description 
Small town on the north side of the [[location/Carlemere River|Carlemere River]], south of the [[location/Khuz Mountains|Khuz Mountains]].

Its recent growth can be attributed to an increase in traffic from the main north/south route along the [[location/Dorian River|Dorian River]]. From [[Settlement/Outside Haven/Oakridge|Oakridge]], one can travel north into the [[location/Khuz Mountains|Khuz Mountains]], eastward towards Bredakix or southeast to Brighton.

The residents of [[Settlement/Outside Haven/Oakridge|Oakridge]] are primarily simple farmers and craftsmen. Recently, an old mine was reopened and number of miners have been hired (c.f. [[Plot/Mine Your Own Business/Mine Your Own Business|Mine Your Own Business]]). 

Notably, the village lacks its own guards, relying instead on the protection provided patrols from Haven or Brighton.

There's a small shop catering to the community's needs, offering a range of general goods and farming equipment.
