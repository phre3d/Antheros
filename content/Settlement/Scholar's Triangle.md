# Description
Technically part of [[Settlement/The Cauldron|The Cauldron]], this area is bounded by Lorien Street, The Horseshoe, and [[settlement/Magic Street|Magic Street]]. It is home to the [[organization/University of Haven|University of Haven]] and most of its related colleges:
- [[landmark/College of Common Law|College of Common Law]]
- [[landmark/College of Natural Philosophy|College of Natural Philosophy]]
- [[landmark/College of Physical Naturalism|College of Physical Naturalism]]
- [[landmark/College of Rhetoric|College of Rhetoric]]
- [[landmark/Institute of Mathematics|Institute of Mathematics]]
- [[landmark/School of Alchemy|School of Alchemy]]
- [[landmark/School of Medicine|School of Medicine]]

Only the [[landmark/College of the Mystic Arts|College of the Mystic Arts]] and [[landmark/Student Tenements|Student Tenements]] lay outside this neighborhood.
