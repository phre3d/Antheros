Eugene casts detect magic. Martin asks about the argument. The mayor says she thinks it was in the past, and was Michael Swiftsword, pointing at Harz De la Riis. The two of them aren't normally chummy, but that isn'tnexpected.

Eugene doesn't see anything, but another phantom attacks him, and misses as he had foreseen. Arada shoots it with her bow and misses. Another weird shift happens. Penelope rushesp and swaps places with Eugene. Eugene blasts it with magic missile and dissolves it. He continues his detect magic search.

Martin mentions Olivia and Constantine. The mayor thinks that Gearhart mentioned an Olivia Chronosmith.

Turin asks about the Ironwood coal, Earthheart emerald, and Haven steel.

Penelope notices a box of mirrors. The mayor says that they were going to be hungp in the council room.

The mayor gets a page to takes to the archives to meet Gearhart. The page takes to the archives and tells us to go upstairs and leaves. We go upstairs.

As we enter the halls, a wind burst strikes Turin. Martin calls out, and also hits a tornado.

We explore a bit. One of the rooms is a storeroom. He thinks the tower was built in the long past. We mention Olivia, and he recognizes the name. He thinks Olivia is the last living Chronosmith descendent. He has a book of Constantine's notes.

We explore a bit and find a stair leading to not this adventure. Penelope finds an old patent tellings how to build beep beep. Eugene's reading of the notes suggests that we need a temporal resonator.

We look around the library and find many interesting volumes, including sketches of the tower, schematics of defense systems, principles of time magic, and blueprints for a temporal matrix. Eugene leaves some of the books with Gearhart.

Gearhart thinks Olivia might be a watchmaker. There are a few streets where there are a lot of jewelry shops.

Martin suggests we talk to the guards in that area and see if they know of her shop.
