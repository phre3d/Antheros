# Session Recap

**Game Date:**

1172-6-5

## Details

Suddenly it is Earthday.

We talk to Porky who says we have been acting normal the past five days. We talk to Lyza and explains what is going on. She suggests talking to Thom. Thom says she heard from a person at theniversity that this has been experienced by a few people. Professor Gearhardt is the one to talk to of the Department of Chronology. He has beeen hanging out at the library.

Lyza says we were going to take the weekend to go investigate the lover ' s situation. We rent a cart and horse and a horse for Turin, and head east.

Towards the end of the day we get to the town we thing is Killwich. There is a rundown pub called the Wandering Griffon. Entering there are a few trestle tables and benches. The barkeep isnfriendly. We talk about the church and he says it is a bad place. It was a church to Mandos the god of Death, but it has been taken over by a group of strangers. The Innkeeper also says that his sister has gone missing, and would pay a reward if we could save her.

We head east, through fog and a rough track.

In the clearing we see a large church. On the front there are four stone statues of creatures with wings. We go around, there is a side building tucked beside the tower with a corridor connecting it to another side building. There are lights inside the church and the buildings. To the west side, there is a green glow coming from a window. We see flickering of shadows through the window, but it is hard to tell what it is.
