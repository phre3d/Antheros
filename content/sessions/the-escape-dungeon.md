# Session Recap

**Game Date:**

1172-5-20

## Details

The day before we did some shopping. We bought a pearl.

The day of the event we go to the Trademaster' s Square, where we wait for a long time. We are in with a group of other adventurers in the Hall of All Guilds, who all are waiting their turn. It is a mixed bag. Turin heard a familiar female voice, but couldn 't locate the speaker.

We had our choice of six different items, fish, stones, parchment, a d8, a broken key, and some salt. We were told to only take one each but Arada took two!

There were many puzzle rooms, gather three keys and open a door, solve a puzzle with an owl bear, pick out the right door (we picked a tree. Shouldn' t have picked the tree), and solve a logic puzzle.

We endedp third, which was the best result for a guard team in years. The winning team was called the Enigma. A red headed woman, a wizard with a yellow beard, and a heavily tattooed man.

At the celebration, Arada heard the voice of the captain from the Nightmare-it was the read head from The Enigma!Dun dun dun.
