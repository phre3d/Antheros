## Description
Gunpowder is chiefly used to propel a bullet from the barrel of a pistol or rifle, or it is formed into a bomb. Gunpowder is stored in small wooden kegs and water resistant powder horns.

## Effects
Setting fire to a container full of gunpowder can cause it to explode, dealing fire damage to creatures within 10 feet of it (3d6 for a powder horn, 7d6 for a keg). A successful DC 12 Dexterity saving throw halves the damage. 

Setting fire to an ounce of gunpowder causes it to flare for 1 round, shedding bright light in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet.
