## Description
A swirling, glowing tonic that is orange in color. It smells like garlic, but tastes like rot.

## Effects
Causes one to grow a thick covering of two-inch-long hair. The hair insulates the imbiber from even the coldest weather and winds, even magical. It has the positve side effect of increasing unarmored AC by two.

## History
Created by an explorer of the cold regions, this potion helps keep you warm.
