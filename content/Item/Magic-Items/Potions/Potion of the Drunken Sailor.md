## Description
A slimy liquid that is gray in color. It smells and tastes like butter.

## Effects
Allows one to feel the full effects of alcohol and return to full sobriety once a situation arises that calls for the imbiber's focused atiention. Once the task is complete, the pleasant drunk feeling returns. 

The potion lasts until the body has fully metabolized the alcohol.

## History
A savvy pirate captain stumbled upon the secret of this special concoction in a book collected from a wayward merchant ship. Since then, he mixed dribbles of potion into the rum barrels to keep his crew both happy and functional.
