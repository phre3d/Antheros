# Oil of Magic Stones

## Description
A oily, thin fluid that is ochre in color. It smells and tastes like fruit and apples.

This oil can coat up to three small stones. Applying the oil takes an action.

## Effects
The coated stones become magical and gain a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls made with them. They can be slung from a sling as magical ammunition, or can be thrown. 

If thrown, they have the Thrown property, a range of 20/60 feet, and deal 1d4 damage. All creatures are considered proficient when throwing the stones.
