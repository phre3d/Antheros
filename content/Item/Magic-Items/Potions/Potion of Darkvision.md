## Description
A swirling, chunky potion that is brown in color. It smells and tastes like urine.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, you gain darkvision to a distance of 60 feet for 8 hours. If you already have darkvision with a range of at least 60 feet, the range increases by 30 feet for the duration.
