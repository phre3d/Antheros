## Description
A transparent, thin liquid that is gold in color. It smells and tastes like vegetables and pears.

## Effects
For a period of 1 hour after drinking this potion, you gain resistance to all damage types other than force, fire, or radiant as long as you are in dim light or darkness. Additionally, any damage type, resistance, vulnerability, or immunity granted by an items of draconic origin is changed to necrotic.
