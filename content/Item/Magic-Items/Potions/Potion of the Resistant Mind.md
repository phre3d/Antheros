## Description
A boiling, oily substance that is green with white flashes. It smells like walnuts and milk, but tastes like oranges.

## Effects
Places the mind in a mental cage that cannot feel pain or any other effects of torture or truth-telling drugs, both mundane and magical. Once the potion wears off, the imbiber falls into a stupor (incapacitated) for ten minutes.

## History
Created for spies to keep secrets during torture.
