## Description
A boiling, swirling, thin draught that is indigo in color. It smells and tastes like cantaloupe.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, you are surrounded in a protective magical force, granting you an AC of 13 + your Dexterity modifier for the next 8 hours as long as you are not wearing any armor.
