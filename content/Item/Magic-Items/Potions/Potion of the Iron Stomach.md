## Description
A smoking, chunky elixir that is amethyst with yellow glows. It smells like vanilla and ginger, but tastes like ashes and garlic.

## Effects
Staves off the ill effects caused by any food for 24 hours.

## History
Created for the diplomatc corps by an enterprising cleric The potion allowed the diplomats to eat whatever was served as delicacies in the far-flung lands they visited without offending their hosts. It also worked as protection against poisoned dishes. 

It is still used today as a remedy for a delicate stomach or for festivals where overeating is the standard.
