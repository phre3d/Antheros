## Description
This potion is contained in a blue-green glass vial with embossed vines and leaves. A label and a tag state the potion's name in crooked handwriting.

## Effects
This potion enables you to impersonate one characteristic of another person. When you drink the potion, you can target one humanoid within 30 feet that you can see and choose one of these effects: 

&bull; You can imitate the creature's voice.

&bull; You can copy the creature's handwriting (you need to have a piece of paper with their writing to do so).

&bull; You can mimic their appearance and mannerisms.
