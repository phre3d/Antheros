## Description
A oily, chunky liquid that is pearl with orange flashes. It smells like caramel, but tastes like wax and mead.

This oil can coat one melee weapon or up to 5 pieces of ammunition. Applying the oil takes 1 minute.

## Effects
For 1 hour, the coated weapon deals an additional 1d6 fire damage. A flammable object hit by this weapon ignites if it isn't being worn or carried.
