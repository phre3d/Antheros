## Description
A translucent potion that is cyan with blue flakes. It smells and tastes like sugar.

## Effects
Fills the imbiber with a well of lightning energy. Each time an enemy succeeds in a melee attack against the imbiber with a metal weapon, a burst of lightning traces down the weapon and causes an additional 1d6 lightning damage.

## History
This poton was created by a battlemaster looking for a way for his troops to be more effectve in armed battles against enemies.
