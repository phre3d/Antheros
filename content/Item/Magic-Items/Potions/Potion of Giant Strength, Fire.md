## Description
A translucent, watery liquid that is orange in color and has a whitish, crescent shaped thing floating in it. It smells and tastes like herbs and lemon.

When you drink this potion, your Strength score changes to 25. The potion has no effect on you if your Strength is equal to or greater than that score.
