# General Info
Alfonso is one of [[person/angelina|Angelina]]'s lieutenants in [[organization/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]. Along with three other subordinates of slightly greater brawn but vastly inferior wit, directs a small longshoreman's firm and storage warehouse, known respectively as Longshoremen Inc. and [[landmark/caradellos-storage|Caradello's Storage]]. 

 He also supervises the activities of Constantine Maylino, owner of a dockside warehouse, Maylino's night watchman [[person/rufus|Rufus]], and [[person/lazar-symbocca|Lazar Symbocca]], owner of [[landmark/syms-storage-company|Sym's Storage Company]]. Under the direction of Caradello, Maylino oversees the buying and storage of illicit cargos for the small fleet of Black Hand smugglers that operates along the river. Unknown to Maylino, cargos smuggled into Haven are stored in Caradello's warehouse, or at Sym's Storage. 

 A large and burly man whose bull neck and sloping forehead belie his fierce and arrogant intelligence. He is not a man given to sparkling wit and prolonged conversation, but rather to violent repartee and organizational insight; this latter capability, and his mastery of codes and ciphers, has made him invaluable to [[person/angelina|Angelina]]. 

 Ostensibly he is the owner and chief foreman for the small longshoreman's firm called Longshoremen Inc., and part owner of [[landmark/caradellos-storage|Caradello's Storage]]; however, he also directs Black Hand smuggling operations on [[settlement/the-south-docks|The South Docks]]. He is not a thief, and his promotion within the Hand has been the result of his managerial capabilities. 

 He is a well-liked local figure who uses his influence to look after or protect the neighborhood populace; as a consequence, he has many favors to collect from the common folk of his area. 

 Although he is well past thirty, he is not too old to hold his own in dockside brawls, and his great strength and fast reflexes make him a dangerous opponent.
