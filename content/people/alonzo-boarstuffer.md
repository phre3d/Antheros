# General Info
A large, jovial man, with little hair on his head and much on his chin (his beard is waist length). He enjoys the study of the law in his spare time, and has always dreamed of sitting as judge for a trial. He got his name from his habit of procuring a large boar from one of the farms outside the city, stuffing it and roasting it whole whenever there is a spectacular trial in the [[landmark/courts|Courts]].
