# General Info
Bianca is a young newcomer at [[landmark/the-blue-lantern|The Blue Lantern]] and has made quite a splash with the customers. She is very beautiful, of medium height, slender, with blonde hair and blue eyes. 

She is in reality the runaway daughter of a noble family on [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]], the Tainleys. She has altered her name and dyed her hair to change her appearance. (Refer to scenario The Nobleman's Daughter). 

 [[person/byelaya-tainley|Byelaya Tainley]] was always known as a loner by the other maidens of [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]] and has only one close friend - [[person/cortina-de-la-riis|Cortina de la Riis]]. As a young girl she heard a very romantic story in which the heroine was a courtesan and since then has had dreams of being a hetaera (a very high class courtesan) herself. She has confided these dreams to no one, not even her closest friend. 

 Three months ago, she dyed her hair blonde, changed her name to Bianca, and ran away from home. Bianca eventually started  working at [[landmark/the-blue-lantern|The Blue Lantern]] on [[settlement/the-street-of-silk-veils|The Street of Silk Veils]]. No one at the house knows her origins and certainly no one suspects she is the daughter of a noble house. 

Bianca/Byelaya is a natural courtesan - she enjoys men and women and her clients definitely enjoy her. Bianca has that indefinable quality of innocence that they find irresistible. She has rapidly progressed, in the little time she has been at [[landmark/the-blue-lantern|The Blue Lantern]], from a very green novice to a competent intermediate.
