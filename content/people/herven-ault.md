# General Info
Ault (#217) is tall, and spare, with longish sandy hair and blue eyes. He is also a member of the Philosophical Way, and has been conducting an affair with [[person/ellian|Ellian]] for over ten years. Herven is relatively staid in all other ways, and exhibits less bigotry than any other judge. He, too, despises Cardallo, and would like nothing better than to see the lecherous scoundrel removed from office; so far, no evidence sufficient for impeachment (even in the aggregate) has been discovered.
