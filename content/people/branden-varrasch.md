# General Info
Brandon is the aged former leader of the Varrasch clan. He was renowned in his prime as a fearless horse breaker, able to calm the wildest stallions. A few years ago Branden suffered a fall from a horse that left his legs completely crippled, and he is now confined to a wheelchair. 

His brother [[person/valken-varrasch|Valken Varrasch]] is not ageing well, so younger brothers Heidel Varrash and Kronen Varrash have taken over the business. Despite them having done well, both Branden and Valken are angry that they are no longer consulted about major business decisions.
