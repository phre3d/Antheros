# General Info
Othmar is about 50 years old, with a gray, wispy beard and mustache, and a raspy voice; a virtual recluse, little has been done in the matter of his personal hygiene for a long period - his body and the disheveled blue robe he constantly wears appear to have never been washed in the last ten years, and he constantly scratches himself. Othmar tells a good story when he gives a reading, and he always puts his predictions in the most optimistic context; as a result, he has developed a faithful clientele, despite his personal habits.
