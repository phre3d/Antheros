# General Info
Young City Watch sergeant who is responsible for the [[landmark/city-of-the-dead|City of the Dead]]. He started as a regular watchman in [[settlement/the-south-docks|The South Docks]] but was later transfered to Noble's Way Station.  

He was recently promoted to sergeant and assigned to [[landmark/city-of-the-dead|City of the Dead]]. He was hoping that work in the new posting would be easier but slowly learns that guarding the park presents its own unique challenges.
