# General Info
Common thief. 

 Forming a Laurel and Hardy team with brother [[person/ernie|Ernie]], these brothers work together on armed robberies and burglaries. [[person/ernie|Ernie]] is small and wiry, and provides the brains of the operation, while slow-witted Mugs provides the muscle. Unfortunately, with only one brain between them, their success rate is not high; [[person/ernie|Ernie]]'s plans start out logically but often go awry due to later elaborate embellishments, while Mugs can remember only the simplest of instructions and is apt to falter in or bungle the job assigned to him.
