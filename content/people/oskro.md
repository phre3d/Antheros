# General Info
Oskro the innkeeper is a grouchy old fellow who suffers from rheumatism, gout, indigestion, and bad teeth, and is seldom seen to smile. He spends much of his time bedridden, leaving operation of the inn to his wife and daughter, who wait on him hand and foot.
