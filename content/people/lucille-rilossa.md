# General Info
> “I've found where the Black Hand are hiding. So, are we going to take them down together?”|Lucille Rilossa, Haven City Watch

An ambitious city watchman who runs into the players. She'll go far, but the direction will depend on how she is treated.

# Background and Motivations 
Lucille Rilossa joined the watchs to be like her uncle, a local hero from a few decades back that tracke d down a ruthless killer singlehandedly . Since joining she's been attracting the attention of the higher ups in a very positive way. She's ambitious and looking for the feather in her cap that will lead to a promotion. 

That “feather” has come in the form of the Black Hand gang. They've been smuggling weapons and the way to be initiated is to take a smuggled weapon and red it with blood. It doesn't matter who's. 

She will be easy to introduce to the party. Any time a watch ought to turn up, have it be her. If the party have done anything untoward then she can offer them clemency in return for helping track down the Black Hand. If they're blameless in any altercation then she may simply ask for their help. If watchs haven't needed to be summoned then have the party run into some of the gang or have a help wanted poster.

She will be keen to have the party's help in tracking the smugglers. She's so keen for her promotion in fact that she could easily be tempted to take risks. Risks like storming the gang's hideout with just the player party for backup. This will be her turning point.

If she ends up not getting proper credit for her work in the aftermath, if the players let the other watchs know or take the glory for themselves, or if the party abandon her in the assault and leave her for dead, then it will send her down a dark path. 

If things go well for the young, idealistic watch, then great things are in store for her.

# Watchman 
Lucille will begin any campaign as a lowly city watch with big dreams, stationed at [[landmark/market-barracks|Market Barracks]]. If she gets involved with the party then it will lead to a crux point. Either things will go well regarding the Black Hand Gang or they will go badly.

If they go well she will progress up the ranks and become a Watch Captain. If they go badly, she will become disillusioned and become the leader of a group of bandits. No matter where she ends up though, that idealistic watch's desire to do good will still be there at the core, even if it is wrapped in layers of pain and betrayal. 

### Description 
Lucille is normally in the watch standard leather armour, which she keeps cleaned to a high polish. When not on duty she will often be wearing her own set of leather armour that is far more ornate with various decorative toolings and brass buckles. Either way, she wears a ![[1348745]]|ribbon in her hair given to her by a young girl she saved from the Black Hand.

# Watch Captain 
After her successful and impressive handling of the Black Hand Gang, the watch leadership finally found the excuse to promote her, making her one of the youngest Captains in the city. She will be at the forefront of the next crisis to befall the city when the players are around. If catastrophe is averted and she is instrumental, then she will become the City Champion. If she fails in averting catastrophe or isn't able to help, she'll leave the constabulary, looking for a different path where she can have the impact she wants as a Monster Hunter.
