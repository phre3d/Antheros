# General Info
Culver is a tall middle-aged merchant with hair still jet black, and piercing gray eyes; possessed of a humble self-image, he is genuinely grateful for his modest business successes, and strives to provide rapid service for his customers (he will make urgent or private personal deliveries himself, and can be trusted to keep a confidence - except from his wife, whom he worships completely).
