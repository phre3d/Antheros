# General Info
[[landmark/the-golden-ducat|The Golden Ducat]]'s chef, Haldo Rumbutter, is an exceedingly rotund halfling who recognizes only one chef, Undo Hardcastle (at the Aerie), as his peer. He is exceptionally perturbed by all the recent uproar in [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]] about the cooking feats of Biggs Oldbuck, and the mere mention of the latter's name when Haldo is in earshot is a good way to guarantee a badly burned meal.
