# General Info
Olive-skinned, longish dark hair, tied-back in a ponytail; bearded, with dark, restless eyes. Yohan is somewhat short, and lean; he wears simple trail garb that allows him to blend into the woods easily. A bow and arrows, and a short sword are his weapons. 

 He has long experience as a highwayman and can move soundlessly through the roughest terrain. He is a skilled hunter and tracker, a deadly aim with his longbow, and speaks Elvish and Orcish fluently, in addition to the Common tongue. 

 Nominally hired to guard the troupe he is there to ensure [[person/toric-habadash|Toric Habadash]] get the chest to Mandalai. He has no personal interest in the contents of the chest - he is a mercenary hired to do a job, and is stubbornly loyal to his mysterious employers.
