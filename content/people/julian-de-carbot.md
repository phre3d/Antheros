# General Info
Carbot is in charge of the operation that supplies many of Haven's opium dens with thedrugs necessary for their continued existence. 

Julian works out of [[landmark/black-padres|Black Padre's]], the seedy dockside bar, opium den, and whorehouse located in [[settlement/the-commons|The Commons]]. 

 Carbot is short and fat (although he does not begin to approach the massive girth of Maximus), but he is also cunning, devious, and ruthless. 

 He directs the movements of some ten men (three cells), and the drugs that they handle. Padre's serves as one of the principal store places for Black Hand opium before it is sold, along with a rented storage room at Fellini's boarding house, deep in [[settlement/the-commons|The Commons]], and the back room of Andre de Montville's spice emporium in [[settlement/the-south-docks|The South Docks]], where contraband opium is stored in teakwood boxes marked as cinnamon.
