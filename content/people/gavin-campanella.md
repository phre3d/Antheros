# General Info
Keeper of the [[landmark/carillon-tower|Carillon Tower]], Gavin lives on the lower floors. He knows that there is a secret, 9th bell in the tower. The bell is magical, although Gavin is not sure what it does but he knows it must be rung if the city is ever threatened. 

 Gavin writes "adventure ballads" in his spare time  which are sometimes sung in the taverns. They are, however, totally fictional. 

 His mother, Ranna, owns [[landmark/mixolydian-mode|Mixolydian Mode]].
