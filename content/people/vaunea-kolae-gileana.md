# General Info
A (relatively) slender and rather short (only 6'8") female goliath who deals in fresh poultry and their eggs. Despite her size and apparent clumsiness, she has never been known to drop or crush an egg (people, yes - but never an egg). 

She is married to Benzantree, a mercenary leader and befriending Vaunea will provide an in with him.
