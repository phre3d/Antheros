# General Info
An irascible old dwarf with wispy grey eyebrows and beard, and stormy dark eyes.  His fingers are gnarled with age, but his hands retain much of their metalworking skill. 

Oin conducts business dealings solely in the dwarven tongue (a fact hinted at by the dwarven store name), and non-dwarves must get along as best they can. His tone is fearsomely stern, and may give the impression that he is greatly insulted when a customer attempts to bargain; in fact, he loves to haggle, and a buyer will get a much better price if he perseveres.
