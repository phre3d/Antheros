# General Info
A Seargent of the [[organization/haven-city-guard|Haven City Guard]] stationed at [[landmark/coppergate-barracks|Coppergate Barracks]] and a special friend of [[person/tikkan-maltby|Tikkan Maltby]] (Borough Commander), and the two generally spend their off-duty hours together. 

Lhor is a feisty little man who happens to be an excellent boxer - he has won Haven's equivalent of the Golden Gloves for his weight class, seven years in a row. If Llor is involved in a fight, he receives a bonus of +5 to his effective armor class, because of his small stature and speed. 

Llor is currently unmarried (his wife died about three years ago), has little to do with women, and lives at the Guard barracks.
