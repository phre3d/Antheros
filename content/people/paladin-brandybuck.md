# General Info
Paladin is a very proud young halfling fighter with political aspirations, who has recently been appointed a Leftenant in the [[organization/haven-city-guard|Haven City Guard]] company at Tower Stations despite his rather pedestrian record in that unit (rumor has it that Paladin's brother Rigaletto "coincidentally" made a large contribution to the company's upkeep a few weeks before this commission was awarded). 

He will almost always be wearing a spotlessly polished dress uniform when encountered, and will frequently (60%) be in the company of non-halflings (i.e., elves, dwarves, or humans). Considers matters in [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]] to be unworthy of his attention, particularly the current problem posed by the gang of young human toughs (see Dell, Scenarios). 

Paladin shares a spacious smial with Rigaletto; their home is the frequent site of parties with guest lists including many influential Haveners from [[settlement/the-south-corridor|The South Corridor]], and which are widely renowned as the dullest social events occurring in or around [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]]. 

Anyone wishing to make Paladin livid with rage need only persist in calling him "Lad", a derogatory nickname given to him by some of the more waggish members of the Guard.
