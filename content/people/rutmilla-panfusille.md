# General Info
Rutmilla, [[person/tybalt-de-la-riis|Tybalt de la Riis]]' daughter, is big-boned and very homely, with a broad flat nose and moles on her chin. She is married to her brother [[person/morgan-de-la-riis|Morgan de la Riis]]' best friend [[person/clemente-panfusille|Clemente Panfusille]]. She has not taken to her new sister-in-law [[person/kmaill-de-la-riis|K'maill de la Riis]], and along with niece [[person/ingrid-de-la-riis|Ingrid de la Riis]] has been spreading rumours that K'maill has evil magic powers.
