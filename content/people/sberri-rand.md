# General Info
Hardly looks like he had the same parentage as Kruegger - he is quite tall, almost 6" over 6', and very slender, with blond hair and ice-blue eyes. He works primarily on land investments, and could be considered an expert on real estate valuation. His real expertise, however, is in the field of entomology - he collects butterflies, and has a collection with few peers in the known world (he is rumored to have paid 2,000gp for one little bug!). 

 Sberri is quiet and studious, and is seldom found outside the office or the estate, unless he is taking a lepidopteran sabbatical (an annual occurrence - he is gone for a month, chasing butterflies).
