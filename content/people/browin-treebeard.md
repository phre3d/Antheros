# General Info
Browin has the physical appearance of a dwarf but is actually an alien being from another sphere which has a portal that opens onto this world on rare occasions. The shop is the only two-story aboveground structure in [[settlement/the-dwarrow|The Dwarrow]], and most of its neighbors view Browin and his employees as almost dangerously eccentric.
