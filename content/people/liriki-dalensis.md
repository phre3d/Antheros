# General Info
Of medium height, and average build, with the normal coloration for his heritage, this gypsy horse trader comes to the [[settlement/horse-market|Horse Market]] about every three months to sell off the horses he has gathered. He finds many bargains in his travels along the Long Road to Belakor and back, but seldom does he buy or trade for really bad horses. His stock is usually Average, with an occasional Good or Excellent steed as a bonus. 

 On this particular trip, Liriki has among his herd a miserable palomino with blue eyes (extremely unusual for horses) that was sold to him by an fellow he met on the road. Normally, Liriki would not have considered the horse, but the eye color (and the very low price) convinced him and he hopes to be able to sell the mount, but is not sure he can. If not sold at the Auction, he plans to sell the animal to a butcher to recover his costs.
