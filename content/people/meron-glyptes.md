# General Info
The lawyer of choice, it seems, for every half-orc, tiefling, or lowlife who gets into trouble in Haven. He is a relatively handsome tiefling, with a slightly less obvious horns than usual, rather chunky, dark hair and black eyes. Unfortunately, although he is probably the best lawyer in the city he deals at a tremendous disadvantage with all of the judges because of his racial background. Dagnir, the elf, costs him a -40% penalty; Mindal dislikes him, but tries to be relatively fair, only -20%; [[person/ellian|Ellian]] feels he should be disbarred, a -30% penalty, but Herven is a bit fairer, realizing that the law must provide at least opportunity of representation to all (and who else would take an tiefling for a client?), -10%; Cardallo feels that in any cases involving Meron's clients he should receive a bribe (-20% if none is offered, half normal buying percentages if he is bought). Many of his fellow lawyers respect him for the ability he displays, but they wish he would "just go away"; he is not allowed in either [[landmark/the-hanged-man|The Hanged Man]] or [[landmark/the-lying-lawyer|The Lying Lawyer]] (Yes, Virginia, there is bigotry in Haven).
