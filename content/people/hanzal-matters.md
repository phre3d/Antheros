# General Info
Husband of [[person/valma|Valma]] (nee [[organization/geronde|Geronde]]), Hanzal is a leatherworker from a merchant background who repairs tack and saddles in a little shop located very close to the city's immense [[settlement/horse-market|Horse Market]]. He and Valma live with the rest of the Matters family in a crowded, but nicely maintained, two-story wood structure in the residential area west of [[settlement/the-street-of-harps|The Street of Harps]].
