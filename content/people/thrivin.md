# General Info
A moneylender, who will make loans of up to 20,000gp to a dwarf from [[settlement/the-dwarrow|The Dwarrow]], up to 10,000gp to other residents of Haven, and up to 5000gp to non-residents. His interest rates range from 5-20% per week, depending on the collateral a borrower has to put up.
