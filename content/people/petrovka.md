# General Info
Young, only 17, relatively pretty, with long, thick brown hair and hazel eyes, and a trim figure, and sells flowers from a small cart. She can be found almost anywhere in the Guilders' Park area, or wandering in the Maze. She has a crush on [[person/huiel-farris|Huiel Farris]], and will usually show up at his office door each evening at the time he normally leaves and wait for him; Huiel does not know the depth of her feelings, but buys a small bouquet from her each night (he almost always gives it to old Mallana, the cook at [[landmark/the-lying-lawyer|The Lying Lawyer]] - Mallana thinks he's a sweet boy). Petrovka will seldom miss a trial in which Huiel figures. Her prices are reasonable for her flowers, and they are always fresh in the morning.
