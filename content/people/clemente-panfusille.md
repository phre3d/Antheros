# General Info
Clemente, Rutmilla de la Riis' husband, is close friends with [[person/morgan-de-la-riis|Morgan de la Riis]] and they are fellow officers in Haven's prestigious [[organization/citadel-guard|Citadel Guard]]. Both have served in the Guard for more than ten years, and are very pleased to have the chance to exert authority over the vendors and visitors of [[settlement/trade-island|Trade Island]]. 

 Clemente's face still shows the ravages of a childhood bout of smallpox. Unlike Morgan, who is almost universally liked, Clemente is viewed as a martinet by the men in his barracks, for he drills his command hard and very frequently. He is a very loyal husband uninterested in other women, and is happy with his rather pedestrian duties harboring no great urges to seek a more adventurous avocation.
