# General Info
Assistant metalworker at [[landmark/minotaur-metalworks|Minotaur Metalworks]] who is trying to get his master to becomd more involved with the other [[organization/disaffected-artisans|Disaffected Artisans]]. 

 Whenever possible, Vassig tries to rescue [[person/tacobur|Tacobur]]'s rejects from the trash pile before they can be thrown back in the smelter and re-melted.  He smuggles these pieces out of [[settlement/the-dwarrow|The Dwarrow]], and has them sold at Thieves Market. To disguise the origin, he adds "ito" to the end of the [[person/tacobur|Tacobur]]'s signature.
