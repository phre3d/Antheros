# General Info
Jelaine, merchant, a tall, lean woman with short dark hair beginning to grey is a guest at [[landmark/the-pendragon-inn|The Pendragon Inn]]. She is a dealer in pearls and semi-precious stones and her store of samples is cached in the Inn's safe. She will be looking for trustworthy guards in a few days to accompany her in a caravan going west to her home.
