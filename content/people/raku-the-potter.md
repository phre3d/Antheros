# General Info
Tall and skinny, with long auburn hair tied back in a ponytail, and a shaggy handlebar moustache; he is usually dressed in a mud-splattered tunic, with the sleeves rolled up, muddy breeches and bare feet. He smokes a pipe constantly, and is very easy-going, taking nothing in life seriously; very laid-back and mellow, 

 Raku makes various sizes of vases, pots, etc. on a treadle-powered wheel. He experiments with glazes, however, and the colors don't always come out the way he wants, so about 20% of his merchandise is half-price, due to mottled or otherwise odd colors, 

 Price Range: Fair
Quality: Generaly Good, aside from the above
