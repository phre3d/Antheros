# General Info
[[landmark/the-briarthorn-inn|The Briarthorn Inn]] is run by a tall, broad-shouldered man with a scarred face known as Calder the Whip (#1828); he is a former highwayman, and is an expert with the bullwhip he wears at his hip. Calder does little of the work of the inn, except lounge behind his desk and collect rents.
