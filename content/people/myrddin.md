# General Info
A guest at [[landmark/the-pendragon-inn|The Pendragon Inn]], Myrddin is an itinerant mage whose real talents lie in clairvoyance. His ability with Tarot cards is truly amazing, but he is a reluctant prophet, and must be persuaded. If appealed to, Myrddin will be able to give them valuable clues regarding the disappearance of Lady [[person/sigrid|Sigrid]] (see scenario The Stoned Maiden).
