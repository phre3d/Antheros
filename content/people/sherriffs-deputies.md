# General Info
A group of  halflings led by [[person/pauli-bracegirdle|Pauli Bracegirdle]], who patrol [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]] because it has been troubled by a gang of young toughs of human and half-orcish blood, who have been threatening merchants and demanding "protection money". They will stop strangers and ask them state their business. If the stranger remain open and friendly, they will have no major problem and will be readily accepted after a short period of initial reserve. These problems will largely disappear if the toughs are vanquished.
