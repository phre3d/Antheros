# General Info
Dark-haired, clean shaven, and very well-muscled, Han is the heartthrob of half the young ladies of the neighborhood. Han is also well aware of their interest, and it has gone to his head. Now he believes himself irresistible, and has become quite a ladies man. His ready smile and courtly manners have gained him many a pleasant evening, but he seems to love variety, and never stays with the same girl. Many of the local young men dislike him, not surprisingly. 

 Han sings when he works, and is charming and flirtatious with any attractive young woman he encounters.
