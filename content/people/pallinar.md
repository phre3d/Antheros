# General Info
The former owner of [[landmark/the-stone-hammer|The Stone Hammer]], Pallinar was forced to sell in order to raise money for the treatment of his invalid daughter Arabel. He still works at the inn several nights a week and lives a few buildings away. He is a grim, joyless man, whose liveliness was snuffed out on the fateful day his daughter suffered her crippling accident in a fall.  He blames her injury on his own inattentiveness, and no one (not even Tolbin, who has tried repeatedly) can comfort him. Since the accident, he cannot abide to see others engaged in frivolous horseplay, and will unleash a violent outburst at anyone involved in such actions.
