# General Info
Owner of a shop selling musical instruments, Alfio has had a running feud with [[person/luciano-clarino|Luciano Clarino]] for 30 years, and will not abide even the mention of his name. 

 He is planing to marry his beautiful daughter Zofia to one of his journeymen, Nikklas in a few months. He is however blissfully unaware that Zofia loves Derek Clarino, the son of his enemy, and Nikklas loves a different female every week. 

 The source of his feud with [[person/luciano-clarino|Luciano Clarino]] lies back 30 years ago when they both loved the same woman, Elissa, who married Alfio, and died giving birth to Zofia. Both men blame the other for causing her death.
