# General Info
Rico, the innkeeper of the [[landmark/golden-wheat-inn|Golden Wheat Inn]], is a well-known figure in [[settlement/oakridge|Oakridge]]. Having spent his entire life in [[settlement/oakridge|Oakridge]], Rico is intimately familiar with every tale and secret the village holds. In a place where days unfold with a predictable calm and the peacefulness is real, Rico has sought ways to inject a bit of excitement into the daily lives of his fellow villagers and guests.   

Standing tall and robust, Rico's presence is as comforting as the fireplace in his inn's main room. His ove for good food, hearty conversations, and especially fine ale is known far and wide. His booming and infectious laughter often fills the inn, drawing people into the warmth of his establishment. 

Rico's culinary skills are self-taught, born of experimentation and a deep appreciation for the local produce that [[settlement/oakridges|Oakridge's]] fertile lands offer. The dishes he serves reflect the village itself-simple, satisfying, and made with care.
