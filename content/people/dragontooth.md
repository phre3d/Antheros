# General Info
General alley basher/legbreakers/other muscle.

 A psychopathic killer 10% of the time, otherwise a normal thug. Uncontrollable once in a state except by magic. Dragontooth once served a mage who knew his problem and kept it under control except when it suited his purpose. 

 Dragontooth is particularly susceptible to SLEEP and CHARM spells, and saves against them at a penalty of 6.
