# General Info
A (supposedly) retired mercenary captain, a former adventurer and explorer, and still a formidable strategist and warrior. He is restless in retirement, and can frequently be found working out at one of the Sworn Sword halls in [[settlement/the-south-corridor|The South Corridor]] or at [[landmark/the-four-of-swords|The Four of Swords]] on Lorien Street. 

 There is a possibility that the player characters could interest Kiroc and some of his men in joining them for a particularly challenging adventure. 

 Kiroc is bold and imaginative, a born leader, with just a touch of recklessness; he does not take orders well from one he does not respect. Kiroc is also an elf-friend, speaks elvish, orcish and common, and is a charming ladies' man.
