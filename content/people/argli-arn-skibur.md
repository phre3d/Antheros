# General Info
Of the Iron tribe of dwarves, and it shows in his courtroom procedure; he is steely hard. Of average height for a dwarf (but built like an arena wrestler, and exceptionally strong for his size), he has bright red hair and beard (not uncommon among the Iron tribe) and bright blue eyes; his beard is always curled and beribboned (one of the few vanities he has), and reaches to his belt. He tends to accept only dwarven clients, but will take others if sufficient remuneration is offered (fee structure 5). Cardallo is afraid of this man, one of the few in the city that he respects, and gives him a +15% bonus (Argli felt Cardallo had been influenced unduly in a trial one time, and swore he would break every bone in Cardallo's body if he ever had any evidence that, at any trial at which both he and Cardallo worked, Cardallo took bribes or unduly favored either side - Cardallo believes him implicitly? None of the other judges particularly favor or dislike the dwarf, but because of his reputation for honesty and righteousness, all do give him a +5% bonus.
