# Description

Half-elf, an orphan from the Labyrinth originally, he used to steal to make a living. He joined Jackals and decided to stick with mausoleum robbery because it brings more coin. He is the second survivor after Nesan''s attack and also got trapped in the mirror. (c.f. Sins of the Past)

![[dndline.png]]

# Special Abilities

**Fey Ancestry.**  He has advantage on saving throws against being charmed, and magic can''t put him to sleep.
