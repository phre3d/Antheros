# General Info
He is of average height, with brown hair and dark eyes.

The Borough Commander of [[settlement/the-commons|The Commons]] in the [[organization/haven-city-guard|Haven City Guard]] for over 15 years and is reckoned a good leader by his men. He likes to drink good beer (almost always at [[landmark/the-snowblind-mouse|The Snowblind Mouse]] - he and the owner have known each other for years), and tell stories of the battles he fought in as a younger man when "the streets were wilder". He has never married (although he is occasionally seen on the Street of Silk Veils), and has quarters at the barracks. 

Tikkan is very outgoing, and an acquaintance can easily become a friend. Any friend who convinces him of sincerity has a good chance for a recommendation to the Guard Academy (35% bonus to chances of acceptance for training).
