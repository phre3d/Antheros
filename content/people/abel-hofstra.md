# General Info
The spitting image of his father, except for the graying temples. He does not have the uncanny business sense his sire shows, but is a capable businessman who seldom makes bad investments. He has a wife of whom he is reasonably fond, and three young daughters he positively adores. When not at the banking house, he is at home with his family.
