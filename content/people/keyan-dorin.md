# General Info
The nephew of [[person/dagnir-saurul|Dagnir Saurul]]; he is tall and leonine, with a great shock of golden hair, and black eyes. In any cases tried before his uncle, he receives a +20% bonus; with Cardallo, who hates the elf judge, he has a -25% penalty. With all others he is neutral. Seldom can he be found other than at his office during the day, but in the evenings he is usually (75%) at the Loft in [[settlement/erinhir|Erinhir]].
