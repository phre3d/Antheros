# General Info
Pelleas is a modest man without pretensions of greatness, who has a penchant for finding common sense solutions to the problems that face his small community. He firmly believes that the citizens of [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]] must keep their own house in order, and will discourage non-halflings from getting involved in the current struggle the community faces in trying to keep out the young toughs from [[settlement/the-commons|The Commons]]. 

 Though Pennywhistle is by no means an able fighter, he has recently begun the practice of walking around [[landmark/the-green|The Green]] nightly, to reassure his neighbors that everything is all right. Unbeknownst to the Mayor, Sherriff [[person/pauli-bracegirdle|Pauli Bracegirdle]] follows Pelleas on these jaunts at a safe distance to protect the latter from harm.
