# General Info
Jock is the [[organization/blackbourne-family|Blackbourne Family]]'s oldest and wisest hand and is overseeing the tutalge of [[person/marcus-blackbourne|Marcus Blackbourne]] as he learns move about the family's businesses. 

 Because of this there is only a 20% chance of he (and Marcus) being in town as the cloth trade keeps him on the road. When they are in town, the two will hit the taverns. Jock often needs to restrain Marcus who won't back down from a fight, even if the odds are against him.
