# General Info
A powerfully built man, who wears tunics that show off his impressive physique. His skull is clean shaven, but he wears a full black beard and earrings of gold, and a bronze armlet with ornamental runes that never comes off. 

 Simba manages the two lions, the bear, and the griffin that comprise the Beast show. His armlet has a Control Beasts spell embedded in it. The two lions are overweight, lazy beasts, but not tame without the armlet; the bear will do anything for a snack, but is short-tempered if it feels the snack is being withheld, The griffin is only half grown, and is actually devoted to Simba, who has raised it from a hatchling. All beasts are securely caged when not performing. 

 Simba distrusts Toric, and privately threatened to tear him limb from limb if he proves untrustworthy. Simba keeps to himself mostly, but is loyal to Fenris, and the troupe. He will fight if the troupe is attacked, releasing the griffin, and is a deadly with a whip.
