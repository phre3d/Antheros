# General Info
A morose man of middle years; he appears gray, and even projects an aura of gray. He will tell you all the terrible things that have happened to him, and the problem of his tenants, raining a veritable barrage of pessimism upon the ears of anyone willing to listen (or unwilling to be impolite and walk away).
