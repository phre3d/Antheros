# General Info
His spirit appears as an old man, dressed like a warrior in plate armor with a greatsword on his back. He is tall, has a big nose, short beard, and weepers. He curses and uses profane language a lot. 

Nesan was head of the [[organization/geronde-family|Geronde Family]] 200 years ago. He captured the dangerous hag Dread Ursula Greenteeth with her coven, tortured them, and managed to kill her sisters. Ursula flew, and cursed him and his family, turning them into ghosts after death. He decided to hide the truth about the curse, ordered placing Mirror of Life Trapping in the secret room of the mausoleum and burying all dead family members here, starting with him.
