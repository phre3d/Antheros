# General Info
Joffa the Barakite is a small, dark-skinned man with an oily complexion, and a personality to match - it is certainly safe to say that people do not patronize this establishment out of personal affection for the owner. Joffa is an unctuous, hard-hearted individual who has little regard for his fellow man; however, he has made it his business to know what people like, and to spend the cash necessary to provide such comforts.
