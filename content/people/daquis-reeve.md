# General Info
A small, dark fellow with black eyes and a thoughtful manner, Reeve is an information broker, a risky business in this part of town. He employs a number of people to keep him informed of doings around town, and will often personally check out a particularly interesting lead. He will both buy Information from reliable sources (no tieflings, please) and take "commissions" for researching a particular subject or person. Daquis is careful, methodical, and a reasonably reliable source of information; he is also a good businessman, and prices his information according to how valuable it is to the prospective buyer. He can be found in several taverns around [[settlement/the-labyrinth|The Labyrinth]] in the afternoons, such as [[landmark/the-hearth-and-hound|The Hearth and Hound]], or [[landmark/the-silver-penny|The Silver Penny]]; he does not stay in [[settlement/the-labyrinth|The Labyrinth]] after dark.
