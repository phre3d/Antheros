# General Info
An excellent healer, although he cannot heal his own condition. Disfigured, hideous to behold, bent and misshapen, he is frequently referred to as the Monster Man; the other healers, even the priests of the various temples, have reached the conclusion that Curadon is the victim of some horrific and incredibly strong curse, one that there seems to be no relieving. 

Curadon normally stays in [[landmark/the-house-of-healing|The House of Healing]], but he will venture out to help the rare person who has need of his skill but is unable to travel to him.

He is much revered by the healers in the city, and they do what they can to protect him.
