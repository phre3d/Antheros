# General Info
A tall, burly man with a thick black beard, bushy eyebrows and a scowl permanently on his face. He speaks in surly monosyllables and grunts, and walks with a painful limp. Through his puppets, however, he is a different man; cheerful, loving, and vivacious, but only through the puppets can he release his true gentle nature. He is not aware of this split of his personality - but the puppets talk about "the boss" constantly, bemoaning his hard heart!
