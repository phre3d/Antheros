# General Info
Fernlace is the top Herbalist in Haven; there is scarcely a plant she doesn't know and she can work wondrous things using them.  She can force-grow seedlings into mature plants overnight, shrink a very large tree down to its original seed in 72 hours, use the correct application of herbs to speed healing, draw up water from underground for drinking or filling moats.  She has written three Herbalism texts and 29 commentaries.

She appears ageless and in in fact a prophet of the goddess [[person/etheah|Etheah]], which is where her powers originate.
