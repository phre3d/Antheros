# General Info
Bing and his brother, [[person/bob|Bob]], seem to act as two halves of one personality. He has a ruddy-face and slightly rotund and is a bit taller than [[person/bob|Bob]]. 

 Bing fancies himself a singer and loves to sing love songs and ballads, particularly when there are ladies present. [[person/bob|Bob]] may be known for his interest in the opposite sex, but Bing always seems to get the girl. 

 He and [[person/bob|Bob]] stick together, and frequently one finishes a sentence the other had started, and they occasionally answer in unison. 

 Bing uses a sling with proficiency but will fight only to save his or [[person/bob|Bob]]'s life; content to take cover under a wagon at any hint of trouble. 

 The brothers are also the cooks for the troupe.
