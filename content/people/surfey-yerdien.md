# General Info
Surfey, or "spearchucker" is a real bow-lover, makes some of the best and largest bows around, with a high moisture tolerance and long life. She got her nickname Spearchucker because she one accepted a bet that she couldn't make a bow capable of firing short spears. The resulting "giant longbow" was only useful in the hands of a giant (or really tall/strong goliath) and required a 22 STR just to achieve a short range shot.
