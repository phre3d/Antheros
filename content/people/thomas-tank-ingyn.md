# General Info
Thomas, a farmer, is a straightforward and warm-hearted individual. He is terrified that his children might never wake up again, and he is willing to do whatever it takes to save them. In his desperation, he even considers facing the Hag alone to secure a cure for his kids. He earnestly pleads with the players to lend their assistance in this dire situation.  

Thomas is a tall, broad-shouldered man, and the constant field sunlight weathered his skin. He wears a small hat, brown trousers with a suspender and a shirt, complemented by a modest grey moustache.
