# General Info
A tall, full-bodied girl of sixteen with shoulder-length, curly auburn hair, green eyes, and a thin aquiline nose. Her grandmother, [[person/rowena-geronde|Rowena Geronde]], sees much of herself in Adrienne, and has pushed very hard to interest her daughter in the study of magic. Unfortunately, the Baroness is also a perfectionist, and soon cooled any ardor for the arcane arts that Adrienne possessed. Adrienne is very intellectually oriented, however, and seems likely to become either a scholar, or a cleric of some sort. 

Adrienne finds most social events around [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]] to be dreadfully boring, for she is expected to mingle with the other noble daughters of her age group, almost all of whom are preoccupied with men, marriage, and clothing (and not necessarily in that order). Although she has blossomed in the last two years and become very physically attractive she has a negative self-image of herself in this regard, and considers flattery of this type to be vaguely insulting. 

 She wears a [[item/pendant-of-the-sisters-of-the-unicorn|Pendant of the Sisters of the Unicorn]], which glows red whenever it is held or worn by one who is not sexually chaste. ([[person/marita-maggia|Marita Maggia]] has one also).
