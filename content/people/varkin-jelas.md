# General Info
Varkin, owner of [[landmark/the-silver-penny|The Silver Penny]], is a lean, dark fellow with a lined face, who has a rare malady affecting his vision; he has not seen a single criminal act take place in his tavern for over thirty years. The dried bloodstains on the floor and on the walls of the back corridors tell another story.
