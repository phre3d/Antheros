# General Info
The head of the Ministry of Health and Sanitation is a broad-shouldered dwarf by the name of Kithli arn Robur. Kithli, who is average in height for a dwarf, with gray-brown skin, reddish hair and dark eyes, lives in [[settlement/the-dwarrow|The Dwarrow]], as do most of his workers.
