# General Info
A talented youth, Petriko and his partner [[person/kivi|Kivi]] spend their days teaching at a small dance and acrobatic school which they own, near [[settlement/the-commons|The Commons]]. Their incredible abilities have drawn many affluent clients to their small school. 

 At night, they perform at various points along [[settlement/the-street-of-harps|The Street of Harps]], the Plaza of Troubadours, as well as the Leiderstrasse. They have several times been employed to perform at houses in [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]], and their popularity and fame is steadily increasing. 

 [[person/kivi|Kivi]] and Petriko are inseparable, yet they are not married, and do not seem to plan to do so. 

 Petriko is a stocky, but quick young man who, in his earlier fighting career, suffered a serious face wound which left him badly scarred. He decided to give up fighting and joined [[person/kivi|Kivi]] to become a truly amazing acrobatic team.
