# General Info
A vigorous entrepreneur, always looking for a new angle to increase his already substantial fortune. He dislikes competition, and will use threats or underhanded tactics to maintain his monopoly on sales of many prestigious products (see Dell, Scenarios). 

 Rigaletto Brandybuck, is one of the very few inhabitants of [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]] seeking to gain influence in overall city affairs, and he owns a shop in both the North and [[settlement/the-south-corridor|The South Corridor]] in addition to his shop and warehouse in [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]]. Rigaletto is not well liked in [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]] because of his social airs, but this bothers him little, for he is much too busy cultivating his friendships with the major merchants of the Corridor.
