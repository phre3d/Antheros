# General Info
A Noldor elf with piercing grey eyes and very pale blond hair who describes himself as "a simple traveler" and appears to be a fighter of some kind. If in his company for very long, one gets the impression that there is a great deal more to this "simple traveler" than meets the eye. 

 If players let slip the fact that they are adventurers or fighters, Silverthorne will be interested in hearing of their exploits, particularly if there are elves or half-elves in the party. This may lead to the players' involvement in Silverthorne's efforts to regain his throne (see [[plots/the-renegade-prince|The Renegade Prince]], [[article/scenarios|Scenarios]], the Cauldron).
