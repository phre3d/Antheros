# General Info
First encountered on her way to the gallows, she is a psychopath who is intensely jealous of any man she loves. If she thinks her man is being "unfaithful", she will attempt to kill him (probably by inveigling him into bed and slipping a knife into his neck while making love). She is, by the way, guilty of the murder with which she is charged. (See Street Encounter: Tumbril Girl)
