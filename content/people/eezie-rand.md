# General Info
A petite, vivacious brunette with blue eyes and a luscious figure. She is totally devoted to husband [[person/zalle-rand|Zalle Rand]], but does like to enjoy herself with a lot of people. She frequently talks Zalle into "going out for the evening? Please, I've been stuck in this stuffy old house all day." He's putty in her soft little hands.
