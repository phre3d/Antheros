# General Info
A halfling who prefers to live with the human folk in [[settlement/the-commons|The Commons]] and is married to a very lovely human girl, Roxanne, who stands only a head (not shoulders) taller than her husband. He is honest, hardworking, and easy to trade with, a totally atypical merchant for the area.
