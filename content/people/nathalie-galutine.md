# General Info
The Baroness is a no-nonsense elf who runs the inn with militaristic discipline. Her main objective is to keep her business lucrative and catering to only the most successful of upper nobility and their distinguished guests. She has neither the time nor tolerance for mistakes or half measures. Her countenance is stoically beautiful, and she speaks firmly and directly while very rarely engaging in small talk. She bears a cold and direct presence that is felt tenfold by those in her employ.

Despite the property's rich history, it did not take much for Baroness Natalie Galutine to acquire the grounds. A member of high standing nobility herself, the only thing more prominent than her social status are her achievements garnered through adventuring.  Additionally, the more recent history of the grounds is marred by treachery and darkness,  as the former lord owner was executed for treason nigh two decades prior. This was enough deterrent for potential investors to be scarce at best, leaving negotiations all but wide open for the Baroness. 

She restored the inn in due time, seeing that the aesthetics of the vast property were aligned to its clientele of high nobility, their distinguished guests from nearby or distant lands, and successful adventurers whose achievements have rendered them celebrities across multiple regions.

Inn security's influence pales in comparison to that of the Baroness, however. Natalie Galutine rules the inn with strict discipline. It is not uncommon for her to physically punish staff members in the more hidden areas of the mansion for breaking glassware, being rude to customers, or any other behavior she deems clumsy or unseemly. Her method of discipline is usually to cane an employee's hands with a bamboo rod. As a result of such enforcement, a stay at the [[landmark/rose-garden-inn|Rose Garden Inn]] is usually an unblemished experience.
