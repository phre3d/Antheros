# General Info
Branden, oldest child of Bartholomew and Amelia, is a chunky youth of 19 who he is trying to learn the business of running the inn, but lacks his father's drive for perfection when he feels his service is not appreciated. 

 He is in love with Felitia, a singer/actress who occasionally performs at the [[landmark/horn-and-hardart|Horn and Hardart]]. He has an intense dislike of [[person/paul-de-la-riis|Paul de la Riis]], for the latter's flirting with Felitia, and will be less than gracious to any other offender.
