# General Info
The Lady Byelaya went missing a little while ago and her father is looking to hire someone to ensure her safe return (refer to scenario The Nobleman's Daughter). 

 Byelaya Tainley was always known as a loner by the other maidens of [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]] and has only one close friend - [[person/cortina-de-la-riis|Cortina de la Riis]]. 
 
 As a young girl she heard a very romantic story in which the heroine was a courtesan and since then has had dreams of being a hetaera (a very high class courtesan) herself. She has confided these dreams to no one, not even her closest friend. 

 Three months ago, she dyed her hair brown, changed her name to [[person/bianca|Bianca]], and ran away from home. [[person/bianca|Bianca]] eventually started  working at [[landmark/the-blue-lantern|The Blue Lantern]] on [[settlement/the-street-of-silk-veils|The Street of Silk Veils]]. No one at the house knows her origins and certainly no one suspects she is the daughter of a noble house.
