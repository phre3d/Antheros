# General Info
A formet wildcat prospector, Ninar is both incredibly gullible and incredibly lucky, the kind of fellow who spends his life savings on an abandoned, worthless mine, and then falls through a hole in its shaft and discovers an untapped mother lode beneath. He runs [[landmark/the-grubstake|The Grubstake]] with his partner [[person/forti|Forti]].
