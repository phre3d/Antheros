# General Info
Alain, [[person/tenley-tainleys|Tenley Tainley's]] younger brother is an enigmatic religious scholar and devoted priest at the [[landmark/temple-of-vairë|Temple of Vairë]]. Like the god he worships, Alain is a creature of the night, seldom seen in public by the light of day. He has dark eyes, close-cropped brown hair, and a mottled complexion; he projects a very foreboding presence that normally discourages others from speaking with him. He occupies the tower room of the temple of Kar, and spends his nights in detailed study of the movements of the moon and stars. 

At present, Alain is abroad in the city far more often than usual as he seeks to learn the whereabouts of a meteor which recently fell to earth. He will show an interest in any traveler in Haven who has recently come from the far north (the Khuz mountains area). 

Alain will not communicate with the rest of his family, unless they are in dire need. Indeed, his brothers in the sect know nothing of his noble background, or his true identity, for he has kept these things secret.
