# General Info
Astrilde, Morgan del la Riis' wife, is happy with her lot as a Guard's wife. She has honey-blond waist length hair, high cheekbones, and delicate features. Astrilde is sweet-tempered, but more than a trifle slow upstairs. If seen in the shops of Haven, she will be carrying her 6 month old baby daughter Bismunde, whom she dotes on to make up for her feelings of failure regarding Carlo, whose inquisitive, intellectual nature is beyond her comprehension.
