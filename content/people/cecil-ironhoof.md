# General Info
Sir Cecil Ironhoof, is an ex-cavalryman in his late fifties with a rugged countenance marked by years of outdoor labor. He has piercing blue eyes that seem to miss nothing and a closely cropped beard streaked with gray. His hands are calloused from years of handling horses and working with tools, and his stance exudes an air of authority and confidence. 

He wears well-designed riding clothes and personally rides all the horses that enter his stables for training, evaluating them for suitability.   

He treats the stables and his workers as though they were his own litile unit, each in their regimented place, with a keen understanding of their tasks and expectations. He considers them all good people and trusts each implicitly to care for and protect these noble steeds, whatever the cost. He spends a few minutes chating quietly to each horse while feeding them apple slices.
