# General Info
U. N. Owen (the U. N. stands for Ulysses Nicodemus) is middle-aged, graying, and among the top five jewelers in Haven (Cellini is acknowledged number one - grudgingly in many instances). He produces most of his items in the workshop in his home, with the aid of a dozen apprentices and journeymen, and wholesales these pieces to a number of shops throughout the city.
