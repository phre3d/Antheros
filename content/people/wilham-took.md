# General Info
A greying, weathered old halfling in his nineties, and his five craftsmen design and build tables, desks, and cabinets. 

 in his prime he was a master second story burglar and safecracker. He remains the overall leader of Thieves Guild activities in [[settlement/the-dell|The Dell]], and is the halfling to contact if the services of a thief are desired.
