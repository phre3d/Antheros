# General Info
A plump, matronly woman with a cheery smile, Daisy greets each ship with a fray full of ribbons, laces, snuff, pipe weed, and candy for sailors to use for gifts to their sweethearts; she is well-known among the sailors and dock denizens. She knows almost every ship and captain better than the register of ships, and is an excellent source of trivial information about the ships and their crews.
