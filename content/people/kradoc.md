# General Info
A sullen half-orc named Kradoc who works at [[landmark/alekhines-boarding-house|Alekhine's Boarding House]], he makes a half-hearted attempt to keep the place clean. He is nosier than his boss, and will not hesitate to eavesdrop on an interesting conversation, if the opportunity arises. Nor is he above petty theft, if he thinks he can get away from it.
