# General Info
Common thief. 

 A plain-looking dwarf with hungry eyes, Yacobur is a reasonably skilled thief; he is also a compulsive kleptomaniac. His normal dwarvish lust for material wealth is far beyond his control and often leads him into some very close scrapes as he strives to possess whatever bauble has caught his eye.
