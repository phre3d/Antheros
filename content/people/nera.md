# General Info
A tiefling vagabond, a thin, bony girl of about 15, with dark shaggy hair and eyes set aslant in her homely face. Taken in by Ranna, she cleans pots and pans in the kitchen and other simple chores. 

Nera speaks common with a thick accent and is very shy around people, preferring to remain in the kitchen. 

Fiercely loyal to Ranna and Ryan, and dangerous with a thrown knife
