# General Info
Kelvar is the only dwarven artisan who chooses to work in [[settlement/the-heights|The Heights]]; he lives a solitary existence very near the city's North Gate, and it is said that his past holds some dark secret that makes him isolate himself from the rest of the dwarven community. 

 Kelvar is very gruff with his customers and has no use for idle chatter.
