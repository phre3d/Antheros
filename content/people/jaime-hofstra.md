# General Info
About 5'5”, tall, and slender, with piercing hazel eyes and [[person/raven|Raven]]'s wing hair, slightly graying at the temples. He has an almost uncanny facility for knowing which way an investment, or a client, will run. Since his wife died 10 years ago, he has devoted himself almost entirely to his business affairs. Occasionally, he will treat himself to an evening of shoptalk at his favorite tavern, [[landmark/garduccios|Garduccio's]]
