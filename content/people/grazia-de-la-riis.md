# General Info
Once a beautiful, lively queen of the Haven social circuit, [[person/tybalt-de-la-riis|Tybalt de la Riis]]' sister is now senile and feeble-minded. She rarely is taken beyond the boundaries of the manor house, for she has an unfortunate habit of mistaking young men that she meets for her dead son, Porcassio, making quite a scene in the process.
