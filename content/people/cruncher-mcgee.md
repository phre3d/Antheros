# General Info
General alley basher/legbreakers/other muscle.

 Cruncher is slow-witted even by alley basher standards, and has a tendency to forget even simple instructions if left alone for more than five minutes. His chief value is as a visible threat, and he is very good at crushing bricks to rubble with his bare hands as a demonstration.
