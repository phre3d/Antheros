# Hofstra Family

Living in [[settlements/the-glens|The Glens]] neighbourhood in the [[settlements/the-north-corridor|The North Corridor]], the Hofstra clan consists of the patriarch, [[people/jaime-hofstra|Jaime]], his four sons, [[people/abel-hofstra|Abel]], [[people/bakka-hofstra|Bakka]], [[people/charlie-hofstra|Charlie]], and [[people/dauge-hofstra|Dauge]], and their wives (who are of little consequence). Jaime's only daughter, [[people/eezie-rand|Eezie Rand]], is married to the youngest [[organizations/families/rand-family|Rand]] brother, [[people/zalle-rand|Zalle Rand]], but still keeps in close contact with her family.

The two older sons assist [[people/jaime-hofstra|Jaime]] in running the bank, determining investments, foreclosing on overdue loans, etc. [[people/charlie-hofstra|Charlie]], and [[people/dauge-hofstra|Dauge]] run the courier service which connects the main branch in Haven with the branches in the rest of the [[organizations/confederation-of-the-ten-cities|Confederation of the Ten Cities]] - they are presently engaged in trying to set up a coach and mail line to compete with the successful venture run by Gillaume Fargot and Andre Welshe.

>[!Private]
>The family fortune makes the Hofstra one of the five richest families in Haven but show few pretension of power.
