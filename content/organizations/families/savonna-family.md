# Savonna Family

[[people/margery-savonna|Lady Margery Savonna]] is the very wealthy matronly widow of Eklund Savonna, the late lord of Kinselbrock. The family retains substantial feudal land holdings in the west, now governed by Eklund's half-brother, Tolman Burlingame.

Margery herself has no interest in politics or administration, and much, prefers the active social life of the city. She shares the family's city estate with her younger daughter [[people/donia-savonna|Donia Savonna]] and a host of servants; her elder daughter, Karys, is a priestess of [[religions/etheah|Etheah]] at a temple in Kinselbrock.

The [[landmarks/savonna-family-residence|Savonna Family Residence]] in Haven is located in The [[settlements/the-heights|The Heights]]
