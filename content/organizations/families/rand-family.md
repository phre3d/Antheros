# Rand Family

The Rands are considered upstarts, having burst upon the Haven financial scene only a decade ago; their flamboyant rise to the near top of the banking pile has made them many enemies, but the three Rand brothers appear unconcerned with others' opinions of them - they are, purely and simply, out to make as much money as they can.

The head of the group is the eldest brother, [[people/kruegger-rand|Kruegger]], the most money-wise of the three and under his direction the family's fortunes have increased many fold. The other two, [[people/sberri-rand|Sberri Rand]] and [[people/zalle-rand|Zalle Rand]], are good investors, one in land, the other in commodities for export, but neither has the management skill displayed by [[people/kruegger-rand|Kruegger]].

The three share an estate in [[settlements/the-glens|The Glens]] and only [[people/zalle-rand|Zalle]] is wed, to [[people/eezie-rand|Eezie Rand]] (nee [[organizations/families/hofstra-family|Hofstra]], which has prompted rumors of a possible merger between two of the largest fortunes in Haven.
