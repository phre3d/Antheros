# Description
The guild looks after anyone who makes or works with cloth and textiles. As [[people/hamlin-blackbourne|Hamlin Blackbourne]] is its leader, this is one of the five "great guilds" that contol much of what happens in Haven.

They meet in the [[landmarks/guildhall-of-the-loom|Guildhall of the Loom]].
