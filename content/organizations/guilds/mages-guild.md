# Description
Most practitioners of magic in Haven are members of this guild, though it is not as strict on membership as the [[organizations/guilds/guild-of-entertainers|Guild of Entertainers]]. The guild also is reponsible for training through the [[landmarks/college-of-the-mystic-arts|College of the Mystic Arts]], which is part of the [[organizations/university-of-haven|University of Haven]], despite being located away from the main univerity grounds ([[settlements/scholars-triangle|Scholar's Triangle]]).

The guild is led by members of the [[organizations/guilds/circle-chamber|Circle Chamber]], whose master is [[people/karilinia-inturisdan|Karilinia Inturisdan]].

Both the guild meetings and [[organizations/guilds/circle-chamber|Circle Chamber]] meetings are held at the [[landmarks/guildhouse-of-magic|Guildhouse of Magic]] on [[settlements/magic-street|Magic Street]].

Note that while alchemy and potions might be considered magic, these practitions are part of the Guild of Apothecaries, Alchemists and Potioners.
