"Eat, drink, and be merry, for tomorrow you may diet".

The Provenders represents all who provide and sell foodstuffs: butchers, bakers, green grocers, dairy, etc. Basically everything between the farm and the kitchen. They meet at the [[landmarks/guildhall-of-provenders|Guildhall of Provenders]].

[[people/gilberto-maggia|Gilberto Maggia]] is head of the guild and one of the five "great guilders" who control much of what happens in Haven (see [[organizations/government-of-haven|Government of Haven]]).
