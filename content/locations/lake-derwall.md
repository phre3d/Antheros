# Description
Lake Derwall is a collecting point for rivers that drain much of Antheros' western continent. Because of this it has become a central training point for the  [[organizations/confederation-of-the-ten-cities|Confederation of the Ten Cities]]. The largest settlement on the lake is the city of [[settlements/outside-haven/mandalai|Mandalai]] on the eastern shore.

The lake's outlet, is the [[locations/dorian-river|Dorian River]], that flows south to the ocean.
