# Description
The Dorian has been a major trading route for centuries as it is navigable by large sailing vessels as far north as the [[settlements/free-city-of-haven|Free City of Haven]].  There, goods are transfered between river vessels that have made the trip downstream bringing goods from [[settlements/outside-haven/mandalai|Mandalai]].

Alongside the river is a major caravan route from Clermont to [[settlements/outside-haven/mandalai|Mandalai]] and beyond.

# Geography
Draining [[locations/lake-derwall|Lake Derwall]], The Dorian flows south to where it meats the  ocean in a large, wide delta.
