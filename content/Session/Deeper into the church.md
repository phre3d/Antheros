They say the cultists have taken over the church.  It has all been women that they have seen. We offer drinks. Arada examines the chest which contains a small amount of money.

They find a small office with a desk. On the desk are a silver mark, five dice and a sheet of paper and quill. The paper has two columns with a symbol at the top of each. Other than that there are old robes and nothing otherwise exciting. The door leads outside. Penelope looks where there ought to be a space. The group thinks they have seen the church and side building.
