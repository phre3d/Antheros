# Burglar's Pack

````col
```col-md
flexGrow=2.5
===
A basic backpack that can hold 1 cubic foot of gear weighing up to 30 lbs. Additional items may be strapped to the outside. It comes with:
- a bag of 1,000 ball bearings
- 10 feet of string
- a bell
- 5 candles
- a crowbar
- a hammer
- 10 pitons
- a hooded lantern
- 2 flasks of oil
- 5 days rations
- a tinderbox
- a waterskin
- 50 feet of hempen rope
````
