# Dungeoneer's Pack

````col
```col-md

A basic backpack that can hold 1 cubic foot of gear weighing up to 30 lbs. Additional items may be strapped to the outside. It comes with:
- a crowbar
- a hammer
- 10 pitons
- 10 torches
- a tinderbox
- 10 days of rations
- a waterskin
- 50 feet of hempen rope
````
