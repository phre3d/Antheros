# Potion of Lightness

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Utility
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours
>
>**Price:** 3,500gp

## Description

A swirling, viscious potion that is cyan in color. It smells like cinnamon, but tastes like nuts.

## Effects

Cuts a character's weight by 50%, making climbing activities much easier. The only drawback is that less weight in windy conditions creates a special falling hazard due to their lighter weight. Saves against wind attacks are done at disadvantage for the duration.

## History

Used by rock climbers worldwide.
