# Potion of Focus

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Skill
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours
>
>**Price:** 3,800gp

## Description

A jade liquid. It smells and tastes like cucumber and ashes.

## Effects

This potion grants the ability to learn a language by studying documents written in that language. It only takes seeing 100 words in the unknown language for the potion to grant the ability to read and write.

## History

Created by a translator who often worked with arcane documents.
