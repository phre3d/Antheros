# Potion of Alertness

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mind
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 72 hours

## Description
A chunky fluid that is red in color. It smells like wine, but tastes like beer and herbs.

## Effects
This potion allows the imbiber to stay awake for 72 hours without exhaustion effects. When the potion wears off, the imbiber must sleep a regular eight hours and wake up refreshed.
