# Potion of Gliding

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours 
>
>**Price:** 420gp


## Description
A opalescent, thin mixture that is amber with yellow flecks. It smells like salt water and candy, but tastes like wax.

## Effects
Allows one to glide over difficult terrain and water as though it were normal terrain. Obstructions, tangled vines, and more, both magical and non-magical, are ignored when one is under the effect of this potion.

## History
Created by a swamp hunter in the early days when much more of the earth was swamp infested.
