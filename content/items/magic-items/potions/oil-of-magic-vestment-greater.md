# Oil of Magic Vestment, Greater

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Coat <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours
>
>**Price:** 50,000gp

## Description

A slimy draught that is ochre in color. It smells like coconut, but tastes like coffee and chemicals.

There is enough to coat one set of armor or clothing. Applying the oil takes 10 minutes.

## Effects

The coated item is magical and grants a bonus of +2 to AC. Coated clothing is treated as armor that grants an AC of 10 plus the target's Dexterity modifier.
