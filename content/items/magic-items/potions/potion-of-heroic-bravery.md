# Potion of Heroic Bravery

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Stat
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute 
>
>**Price:** 3,100gp


## Description
A fizzing, murky draught that is black in color. It smells like oranges and nuts, but tastes like chemicals and lettuce.

## Effects
When Imbibed, the imbiber's primary stat increases by 4 points for one minute.

## History
This potion was commissioned for a great warlord intent upon taking over the world. Fortunately, the potion wasn't as powerful as he wished, but it still created havoc for a generation until the warlord was finally assassinated, and the formula was lost to time. Occasionally, a bottle is found in an ancient treasure trove.
