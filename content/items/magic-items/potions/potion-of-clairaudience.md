# Potion of Clairaudience

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Sense
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes
>
>**Price:** 4,600gp

## Description

A transparent tonic that is pink in color. It smells and tastes like pinapple and nuts.

## Effects

After imbibing, the imbiber can hear any sounds within 30' regardless of walls, doors, or other obstructions.

The one drawback is any really loud noise in that range causes temporary deafness lasting 1d4 rounds.

## History

The thief Timmy Timms needed an edge for his second-story activities, and this potion was just the edge he needed.
