# Potion of Misdirection

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Protection
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours 
>
>**Price:** 3,500gp


## Description
A bubbling, opalescent fluid that is white with scarlet flakes. It smells like peanuts and celery, but tastes like vanilla and cinnamon.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, choose another creature or object within 30 feet. For potion's duration, whenever you are subjected to any divination spell or other form of magical scrying effect, the effect instead targets the chosen creature or object as long as you remain within 1 mile of it.
