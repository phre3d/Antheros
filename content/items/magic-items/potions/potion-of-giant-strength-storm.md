# Potion of Giant Strength, Storm

>[!info]

>**Effect Category:** Stat
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour
>
>**Price:** 6,000gp

## Description

A translucent, watery liquid that is dark grey in color and has a whitish, crescent shaped thing floating in it. It smells and tastes like herbs and lemon.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, your Strength score changes to 29. The potion has no effect on you if your Strength is equal to or greater than that score.
