# Potion of Cleansing

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>

>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** Special
>
>**Price:** 660gp

## Description

A glowing elixir that is blue with crimson specks. It smells like lime and butter, but tastes like carrots and tea.

## Effects

Drinking the potion covers the imbiber in a rich carpet of bubbles that remove all dirt and grime and magically disappear seconds after finishing their cleaning task.

The only drawback is the residual soapy taste in the mouth.

## History

A priest created this potion to help people cleanse their souls of sin, but it didn't quite work out. Instead, it made the imbiber immaculate - including their clothing.
