# Potion of Recklessness

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute
>
>**Price:** 430gp

## Description

A swirling, fizzing draught that is crimson in color. It smells like rot, but tastes like urine and raspberries.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain advantage on melee weapon attack rolls using Strength for 1 minute, but during this time attack rolls against you have advantage.
