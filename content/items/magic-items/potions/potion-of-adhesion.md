# Potion of Adhesion

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours
>
>**Price:** 350gp

## Description

A bubbling fluid that is cobalt with ruby flecks. It smells like earwax, but tastes like candy.

## Effects

When Imbibed, the imbiber's hands become sticky, giving a plus-four bonus to all climbing checks.

A small side effect is that the imbiber must wear gloves when not climbing, or things will randomly stick to their bare hands.

## History

This potion was created by a thieves guild in a tropical region after observing how easily lizards climbed walls.
