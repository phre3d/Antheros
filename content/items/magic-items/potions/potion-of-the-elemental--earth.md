# Potion of the Elemental - Earth

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Legendary <br>
>**Effect Category:** Special
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute 
>
>**Price:** 314,000gp


## Description
A swirling, bubbling, viscious liquid whose color indicates the type of elemental: in this case brown. 

It has an earthy scent, and it feels as though it will clog your throat when you drink it.

## Effects
Turns the imbiber into an elemental for one minute - complete with all the elemental's abilities. If the imbiber falls unconscious, they revert to their original form.

## History
This potion was designed by a powerful sorcerer who felt that elemental creatures held secrets that he could learn if only he could assume their form.
