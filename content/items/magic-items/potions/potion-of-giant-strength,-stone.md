## Description
A translucent, watery liquid that is grey in color and has a whitish, crescent shaped thing floating in it. It smells and tastes like herbs and lemon.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, your Strength score changes to 23. The potion has no effect on you if your Strength is equal to or greater than that score.
