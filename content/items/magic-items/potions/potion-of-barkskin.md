# Potion of Barkskin

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour 
>
>**Price:** 6,000gp


## Description
A bubbling, thick substance that is yellow in color. It smells and tastes like cabbage.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, your skin feels dry and stiff as it gains a rough, bark-like appearance. Your AC can't be less than 16 for the next hour regardless of what kind of armor you are wearing.
