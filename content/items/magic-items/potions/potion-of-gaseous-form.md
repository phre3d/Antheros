# Potion of Gaseous Form

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour
>
>**Price:** 300gp

## Description

This potion's container seems to hold fog that moves and pours like water. It smells and tastes like pears and pine needles.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the gaseous form spell for 1 hour (no concentration required) or until you end the effect as a bonus action.
