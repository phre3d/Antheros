# Potion of Vitality

>[!info]

>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** Special

## Description
A potion's crimson liquid regularly pulses with dull light, calling to mind a heartbeat.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, it removes any exhaustion you are suffering and cures and disease or poison affecting you. For the next 24 hours, you regain the maximum number of hit points for any Hit Die you spend.
