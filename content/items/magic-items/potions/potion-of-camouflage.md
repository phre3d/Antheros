# Potion of Camouflage

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour

## Description

A metallic substance that is yellow in color. It smells like wine and bannanas, but tastes like peanuts.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain the effect of the pass without trace spell for 1 hour (no concentration required}
