# Potion of Hope

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Legendary <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute
>
>**Price:** 297,000gp

## Description

A boiling, oily mixture that is indigo with azure specks. It smells like lettuce and tea, but tastes like cantaloupe and strawberries.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain advantage on all attack rolls, ability checks, and saving throws, and a +2 bonus to all damage rolls, for the next minute.
