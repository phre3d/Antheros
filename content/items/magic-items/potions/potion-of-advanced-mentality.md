# Potion of Advanced Mentality

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mind
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 24 hours
>
>**Price:** 29,000gp

## Description

A bubbling, thick elixir that is cyan in color. It smells and tastes like cherries.

## Effects

This potion protects the imbiber from all psionic thought probes, offers resistance to all psionic attacks, and causes +2 damage to any psionic attacks the imbiber has available.

## History

Created by an ancient monk who sought the mythical silent mind.
