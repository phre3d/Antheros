## Description
A pulsing, thin liquid that is yellow with orange specks. It smells like ashes, but tastes like coconut.

## Effects
Grants superior hand-eye coordination. As a reaction, once per round for a minute, the imbiber can attempt to block a melee or range attack. The blocking effect boosts AC by four against that one attack.

## History
Commissioned by the warlord Oriias to boost his chances of survival in combat.
