## Description
This potion's orange liquid flickers, and smoke fills the top of the container and wafts out whenever it is opened

## Effects
After drinking this potion, you can use a bonus action to exhale fire at a target within 30 feet of you. The target must make a DC 13 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. 

The effect ends after you exhale the fire three times or when 1 hour has passed.
