# Potion of Mimicry

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Skill
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 3 hours 
>
>**Price:** 28,000gp


## Description
A frothing, oily liquid that is yellow in color. It smells like cabbage, but tastes like green pepper.

## Effects
Often used by actors, when Imbibed, one can reshape their body to exactly match the look of any creature of approximately the same weight. The imbiber retains all their normal stats and intuitively knows how to move and function in the transformed body.

## History
A famous actress created this potion to allow herself to step completely into the roles she performed.
