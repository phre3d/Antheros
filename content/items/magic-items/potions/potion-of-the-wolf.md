# Potion of the Wolf

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Legendary <br>
>**Effect Category:** Special
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1d6 minutes 
>
>**Price:** 280,000gp


## Description
A glowing liquid that is yellow in color.  It smells like coffee and vanilla, but tastes like oranges.

## Effects
Turns the imbiber into a werewolf with all its powers and abilities for 1d6 minutes. The imbiber retains awareness during the transformation and acts in the same manner as the character would choose to act. 

For a month after imbibing the potion, the imbiber's hair grows at triple the normal rate, and their diet consists of only raw meat. Otherwise, there are no ill effects from the transformation.

## History
Created by accident while trying to devise an effective rat extermination poison.
