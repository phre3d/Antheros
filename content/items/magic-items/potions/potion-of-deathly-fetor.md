## Description
A metallic fluid that is purple with gray streaks. It smells and tastes like fruit.

## Effects
Fools the undead into believing the imbiber is also undead and not worthy of the special attention they give to the living. The potion has no ill effects but leaves a lingering scent of death and decay in the imbiber's clothes that stays until washed off.

## History
Created by a necromancer to make it easier to study the undead in their natural habitat, this potion gives off the aura of undeath.
