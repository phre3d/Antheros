# Potion of Recovery

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Health
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** Special 
>
>**Price:** 43,000gp


## Description
A moving, fizzing, thick tonic that is azure with jade accretions on the bottom. It smells like melon, but tastes like garlic and beer.

## Effects
Immediately restores a character to full abilities – effectively granting a long rest. The downside is that the imbiber crashes hard four hours later and wakes up after eight hours of sleep with two levels of exhaustion.

## History
Created by the adventurers Scald to stack the deck in their favor on their toughest encounters.
