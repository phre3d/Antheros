# Potion of Fungal Immunity

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Protection
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours 
>
>**Price:** 2,700gp


## Description
A frothing, fizzing potion that is scarlet in color. It smells and tastes like pinapple and cabbage.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, you gain immunity to the poisoned condition if it would be caused by fungi or fungal creatures.
