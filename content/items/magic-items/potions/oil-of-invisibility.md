# Oil of Invisibility

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Protection
>
>**Application:**Coat <br>
>**Duration:** 24 hours
>
>**Price:** 5,800gp

## Description

A cobalt substance. It smells and tastes like vegetables.

This oil can coat a single object with a surface area of up to 10 square feet. Applying the oil takes 1 minute.

## Effects

The object becomes invisible for 24 hours.
