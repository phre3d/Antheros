## Description

A smoking, reflective draught that is pink with gold flashes. It smells like strawberries, but tastes like blood and mud.

## Effects

The imbiber can create one minor, amusing item per round for five minutes.

## History

This party potion was created by a street magician who was famous for his performances for children.
