## Description
A steaming, swirling, watery potion that is blue in color. It smells like licorice and garlic, but tastes like bread and pinapple.

## Effects
Boosts AC by 2 and attack rolls by 1.

## History
Created for a master gladiator desiring to be able to see the results of dozens of actions and counteractions per round, and thus choose the best attack and defense possible during combat.
