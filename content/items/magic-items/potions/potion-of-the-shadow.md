## Description
A watery mixture that is azure in color. It smells like fruit, but tastes like rum and ashes.

## Effects
Causes you to become a shadowy figure existing in that thin line between life and death. While in this state, the imbiber retains their full movement speed, can move through spaces as small as one inch, and occupy the same space as another, causing 1d6 necrotic damage per round while doing so. 

The imbiber cannot physically interact with objects or communicate while in this state.

## History
This potion, created by a widow looking for a way to interact with her dead husband,
