# Potion of Corrosive Spit

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Offensive
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute 
>
>**Price:** 36,000gp


## Description
A transparent, slimy tonic that is yellow in color. It smells like melon and grapes, but tastes like acid and blueberries.

## Effects
Once imbibed, for one minute the imbiber can use an attack action every round to spit at a creature up to 20 feet away as a ranged attack. If it hits, the attack causes 2d6 acid damage on contact and 1d6 additional damage per round thereafter until it is washed off with soap and water.

## History
A reclusive wizard discovered how dragons could survive with corrosive elements flowing through their bodies and developed a potion to give mortals such power.
