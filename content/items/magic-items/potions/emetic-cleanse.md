# Emetic Cleanse

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Health
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 2 rounds 
>
>**Price:** 5,300gp


## Description
A bubbling, oily draught that is yellow with gray bubbles. It smells like wax, but tastes like raspberries.

## Effects
When drunk, the potion causes the imbiber's entire body to shake uncontrollably for several seconds. It purges the poison from all bodily tissues and forms them into a three-inch-long rubbery capsule that is then vomited. 

The purge removes all effects of the poison (except any damage already taken before imbibing), and the poison can be extracted from the capsule to be reused, but its potency is reduced by half. 

The imbiber takes no damage from the potion but incapacitates them for two rounds while it does its work.

## History
An alchemist created this potion to help keep subjects alive a bit longer while developing and testing new poisons.
