## Description
A swirling, watery liquid that is amethyst in color. It smells like walnuts and grass, but tastes like beer.

## Effects
This potion shrinks the imbiber to the size of a large ant while retaining enhanced strength - enough to easily move mechanisms weighing up to five pounds. The potion is popular with thieves and adventurers, granting them easy access to challenging areas. 

The body starts tingling two minutes before it wears off as a warning signal to get somewhere safe before explosively growing back to full size. No damage is incurred as long as the imbiber is in a safe place before expanding back to full size. However, if the space is not big enough, it could result in tremendous damage and even death (at the DMs discretion.)

## History
Created by a horologist to allow his assistants to work on the delicate repair of clock and clockwork mechanisms.
