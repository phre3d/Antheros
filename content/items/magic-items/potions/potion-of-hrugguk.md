## Description

A chunky draught that is cyan with cerulean bubbles. It smells like cream, but tastes like candy.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain advantage on saving throws against being charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, stunned, or put to sleep for 1d4 hours.
