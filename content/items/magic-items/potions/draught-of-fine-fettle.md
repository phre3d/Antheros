# Draught of Fine Fettle

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Protection
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 4 hours
>
>**Price:** 35,000gp

## Description

A bubbling, translucent, thick draught that is violet with amber flecks. It smells like salt, but tastes like blueberries.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain immunity to being charmed, frightened, paralyzed, poisoned, and blinded for 4 hours.
