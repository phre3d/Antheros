# Potion of Loquacity

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mind
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes
>
>**Price:** 3,100gp

## Description

A ochre draught with copper flakes. It smells like rum, but tastes like peppermint.

## Effects

The imbiber must answer any question asked truthfully. It works but has a significant drawback - the imbiber just keeps talking and talking. Deciphering the answer to the question from the gibberish is a difficult task - so difficult that the king abandoned this approach and went back to torture to gather the information he needed.

The potion only affects those who fail a DC 16 Wisdom check.

## History

In ancient times, this potion was commissioned by a king who hated the idea of torture to extract information.
