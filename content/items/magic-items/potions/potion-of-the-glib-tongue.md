# Potion of the Glib Tongue

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Skill
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 2 hours 
>
>**Price:** 2,700gp


## Description
A swirling, watery potion that is emerald in color. It smells like candy, but tastes like lemon and peppermint.

## Effects
Gives confidence in social situations, loosening the tongue and forcing the imbiber to unleash a constant stream of small talk on various subjects. Those the imbiber talks with will come away with a good impression of the person but no clue what was discussed.

## History
A minor noble with social anxiety had the keep's alchemist devise a potion to let him function more effectively in society.
