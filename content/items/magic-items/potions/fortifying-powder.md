# Fortifying Powder

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Common <br>
>**Effect Category:** Health
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 24 hours or loss of granted temp HP 
>
>**Price:** 135gp


## Description
A single packet of this chalky white powder is mixed with a beverage. Loses efficacy if not consumed within an hour of mixing.

## Effects
Drinking this potion provides 2d4 temporary hit points. A creature cannot benefit from more than one dose of this powder before finishing a long rest.
