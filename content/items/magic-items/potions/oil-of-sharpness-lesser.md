# Oil of Sharpness, Lesser

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**coat <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour
>
>**Price:** 300gp

## Description

A glowing, oily mixture that is white in color. It smells like cherries and celery, but tastes like garlic.

The oil can coat one slashing or piercing weapon or up to 5 pieces of slashing or piercing ammunition. Applying the oil takes 1 minute.

## Effects

The coated item is magical and has a a bonus to attack and damage rolls of +1.
