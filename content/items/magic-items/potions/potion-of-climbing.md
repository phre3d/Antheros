# Potion of Climbing

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Common <br>
>**Effect Category:** Skill
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour 
>
>**Price:** 180gp


## Description
The potion is a smoking purple liquid

## Effects
When you drink this potion, you gain a climbing speed equal to your walking speed. During this time, you have advantage on Strength (Athletics) checks you make to climb.
