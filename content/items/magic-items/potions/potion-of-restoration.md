# Potion of Restoration

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Health
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** Permanent
>
>**Price:** 5,000gp

## Description

A thick fluid that is ebony with violet accretions on the bottom. It smells like walnuts, but tastes like pinapple.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, your exhaustion level is reduced by one. You cannot benefit from the effects of this potion again before finishing a long rest.
