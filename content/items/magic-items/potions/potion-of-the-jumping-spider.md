# Potion of the Jumping Spider

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Special
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 3 minutes
>
>**Price:** 27,000gp

## Description

A smoking, reflective, thick mixture that is gray in color. It smells like cabbage, but tastes like cream.

## Effects

The potion causes the imbiber to jump 20' in a random direction every time they fail a DC 14 Constitution save.

If the jump puts them in danger, such as hiting a wall, falling through a window, or other life-threatening situations, the potion mitigates all but 1 hp of damage.

## History

Created as a party prank by the mirthful wizard, Roos Drobane.
