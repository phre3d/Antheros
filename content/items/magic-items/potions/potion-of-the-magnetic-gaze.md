# Potion of the Magnetic Gaze

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 action
>
>**Price:** 37,000gp

## Description

A watery draught that is amethyst with blue flashes. It smells like pine needles and grass, but tastes like herbs and lettuce.

## Effects

As an attack action, you can focus on an iron item, even a warrior in a set of plate armor, and hurl it up to 30'. If a creature is tossed, it takes 3d6 bludgeoning damage when it lands.

## History

The wizard Silvornious specialized in iron golem magic and needed a way to move pieces of iron, large and small, by himself and finely manipulate them. In the wild, the potion has been adopted by adventurers due to its potential in battle.
