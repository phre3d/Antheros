# Potion of Recall

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mind
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours 
>
>**Price:** 5,000gp


## Description
A smoking liquid that is ivory in color. It smells and tastes like spinach.

## Effects
While under the effects of this potion, you gain the ability to perfectly recall any path they have traveled, and automatically succeed on saving throws to escape the maze spell and similar areas.
