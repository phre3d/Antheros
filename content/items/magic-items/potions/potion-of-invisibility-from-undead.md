## Description
A potion is a deep, transparent green and it glows faintly in dim or dark conditions.

## Effects
When you drink this potion, you become invisible to undead creatures. Anything you wear or carry is invisible with you. Other creatures perceive you normally. The effect ends early if you attack, cast a spell, or turn undead
