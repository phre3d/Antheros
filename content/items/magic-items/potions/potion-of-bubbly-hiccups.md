# Potion of Bubbly Hiccups

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Health
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1d4 rounds
>
>**Price:** 4,800gp

## Description

A steaming, viscious elixir that is red in color. It smells like strawberries and caramel, but tastes like cucumber and melon.

## Effects

When imbibed, it causes a strong case of hiccups, each generating a floating bubble filled with a bit of the poison being purged.

The potion takes 1d4 rounds to complete its task and generates 1d4 bubbles per round. Each bubble causes 1d2 poison damage on any creature it touches.

The hiccups do not impact the imbiber's ability to function, and any bubble that lands on the imbiber has no effect.

## History

An assassin created this potion as a tool to remove poisons from his body.
