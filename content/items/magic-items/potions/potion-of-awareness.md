>**Duration:** 1 hour

## Description

A glowing, pulsing, viscious tonic that is sapphire with purple bubbles. It smells like nuts and honey, but tastes like rum.

## Effects

This potion expands the imbiber's awareness to 200'. Focusing on a target allows the imbiber's vision to penetrate any cover, even buildings and walls.
