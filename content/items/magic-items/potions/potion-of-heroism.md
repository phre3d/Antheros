# Potion of Heroism

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour or loss of the temp HP
>
>**Price:** 180gp

## Description

This blue potion bubbles and steams as if boiling.

## Effects

For 1 hour after drinking it, you gain 10 temporary hit points that last. For the same duration, you are under the effect of the bless spell (no concentration required}
