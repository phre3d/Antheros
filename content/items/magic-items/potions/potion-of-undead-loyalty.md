# Potion of Undead Loyalty

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mind
>
>**Application:**Pour <br>
>**Duration:** 24 hours
>
>**Price:** 27,000gp

## Description

A gray mixture. It smells like nuts, but tastes like cherries and salt.

## Effects

When poured over an undead creature, the potion absorbs into its body, making it a loyal servant of the person who doused it. The loyalty can be renewed by another dousing.

If not renewed, the undead develops enmity for the one who controlled it and is driven to revenge.

## History

This odd potion was created by a necromancer needing many undead for a massive secret construction project.
