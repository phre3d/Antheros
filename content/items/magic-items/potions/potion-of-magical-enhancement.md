## Description
A fizzing, ichorous liquid that is jade in color. It smells like coffee, but tastes like pears.

## Effects
All attack class magic is amplified by 50%, rounded down, for one minute. For example, if an attack spell causes seven points of damage, it will cause an additional three points.

## History
Always seeking ways to grow powerful, the mage Kormac created a potion to act as a force multiplier.
