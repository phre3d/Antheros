## Description
A cerulean tonic with blue specks. It smells like lime and wine, but tastes like cream and ashes.

## Effects
This potion puts the imbiber into a rage (per the Barbarian class) for one minute granting resistance to bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage as well as other standard first level rage benefits. If their hit points drop to zero, the potion stops conferring benefits.

## History
Created by a tribal lord to make his warriors more fierce in battle
