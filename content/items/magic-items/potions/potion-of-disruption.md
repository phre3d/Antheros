# Potion of Disruption

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute
>
>**Price:** 4,900gp

## Description

A thin elixir that is yellow in color. It smells like peach, but tastes like pears and tea.

## Effects

Archers are given arcane insight and once per turn they can use their reaction to fire a single arrow at a spell caster. If the arrow hits, the spell is disrupted if the spell caster fails a DC 15 save against their primary spellcasting stat.

## History

A baron commissioned a potion from the tinkerer Smith to protect his fortress.
