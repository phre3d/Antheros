# Oil of Levitation

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Coat <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes 
>
>**Price:** 670gp


## Description
A ebony elixir with scarlet oddiments. It smells like acid and grapes, but tastes like melon and mead.

This oil can coat a single object with a surface area of up to 10 square feet that weighs 500 pounds or less. Applying the oil takes 1 minute.

## Effects
The object rises 5 feet into the air and floats there for 10 minutes. The object can be moved freely by physical labor, and continues to float in place for the duration.
