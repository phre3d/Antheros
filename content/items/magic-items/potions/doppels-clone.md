# Doppel's Clone

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Spell
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes or clone death 
>
>**Price:** 49,000gp


## Description
A steaming, fizzing, thin liquid that is yellow with brown accretions on the bottom. It smells and tastes like cinnamon.

## Effects
When Imbibed, the imbiber falls into a comatose trance, and an exact duplicate forms - complete with all abilities and memories of the original and with the accepted knowledge that it has a life span of just ten minutes. The duplicate can then perform the dangerous task without risk to the imbiber. 

When ten minutes pass or the clone is destroyed, whichever comes first, the clone vanishes, and the imbiber wakes with full knowledge of the clone's actions. 

The potion is imperfect, and there is a 10% chance the duplicate will go on a murderous rampage during its ten-minute lifespan.

## History
An explosives expert created this special potion to assist during particular dangerous experiments or preparation methods for her various explosive creations.
