# Potion of Doubling

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Utility
>
>**Application:**Varies <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes
>
>**Price:** 48,000gp

## Description

A white substance with scarlet globules. It smells like candy and cooked meat, but tastes like caramel.

## Effects

When mixed with another potion, it doubles the dose count of the other potion while maintaining its full strength. The potion's doubled portions must be consumed within ten minutes, or the effect disappears.

## History

The artificer Kane was looking for a cheap way to expand her potion inventory when she had a flash of inspiration - the potion of doubling.
