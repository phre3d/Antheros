## Description
A thick tonic that is ivory in color. It smells and tastes like bread and honey.

## Effects
Causes the imbiber to go berserk, attack the nearest creature, friend or foe, within range, and keep attacking until the creature or the imbiber is dead. The imbiber gains advantage on all attack rolls and rolls a crit on 18-20.

## History
Created by a mild-mannered tinkerer seeking to leave a legacy.
