# Potion of Faith Shielding

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes
>
>**Price:** 30,000gp

## Description

A boiling, metallic, viscious potion that is white with azure flakes. It smells like vomit, but tastes like salt water and caramel.

## Effects

When you drink this potion, you gain a +2 bonus to AC and are surrounded by a shimmering field, shedding bright light in a 20-foot radius and dim light for an additional 20 feet.
