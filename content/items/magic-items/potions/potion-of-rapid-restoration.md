## Description
A frothing, pulsing, thick tonic that is azure with copper flakes. It smells like pine needles and salt water, but tastes like raspberries.

## Effects
Puts the imbiber into a deep sleep that lasts for thirty minutes, whereupon the imbiber wakes up fresh and alert as though a full night of quality sleep has passed.  

However, after five consecutive doses (over three-and-a-half days), the imbiber falls into a deep sleep that lasts twenty-four hours to recover from the magical enhanced energy of the potion-driven burst of productivity.

## History
This potion was created by a ruthless businessperson who put profit above everything else.
