## Description
A reflective, watery potion that is purple in color. It smells and tastes like tea.

## Effects
Your mind reaches out and touches all insect life within 100'. It builds a composite of everything they see, including hidden structures, in the imbiber's visual cortex, providing a three-dimensional image of the region.

## History
A brave mapmaker created this unique visual-oriented potion to help her survey and map remote corners of the world.
