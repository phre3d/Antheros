## Description

A steaming, thin elixir that is yellow in color. It smells like blood and dirt, but tastes like nuts.

## Effects

The imbiber is transported back in time by 30 seconds. The imbiber is positioned exactly where they were 30 seconds ago and is disoriented for several seconds - which could be problematic in a dynamic situation.

No one else is aware of the time shift, which only affects a 30-foot radius area. Anything outside that area is not affected by the time shift.

## History

A clumsy alchemist created this time manipulative potion to 'correct' mistakes.
