## Description
A oily, thin liquid that is blue in color. It smells like pinapple, but tastes like fish.

There is enough to coat one set of armor or clothing. Applying the oil takes 10 minutes.

## Effects
The coated item is magical and grants a bonus of +1 to AC. Coated clothing is treated as armor that grants an AC of 10 plus the target's Dexterity modifier.
