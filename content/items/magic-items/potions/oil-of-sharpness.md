# Oil of Sharpness

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Very Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Coat <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour 
>
>**Price:** 3,200gp


## Description
A frothing, reflective, thin mixture that is orange with scarlet accretions on the bottom. It smells like rot, but tastes like peppermint and wax.

The oil can coat one slashing or piercing weapon or up to 5 pieces of slashing or piercing ammunition. Applying the oil takes 1 minute.

## Effects
For 1 hour, the coated item is magical and has a a bonus to attack and damage rolls of +3.
