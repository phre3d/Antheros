# Potion of Homesickness

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Legendary <br>
>**Effect Category:** Health
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours 
>
>**Price:** 288,000gp


## Description
A crimson potion. It smells like green pepper and melon, but tastes like pine needles.

## Effects
Constantly monitors the imbiber's hit-point level. If it goes below 10%, the imbiber is immediately teleported back to their childhood home.

## History
A solo adventurer with no friends discovered adventuring solo is extremely risky. He had a flash of insight that if he could find a way to make or have made a potion that could teleport him back home if he got into too much trouble, it would help him survive.
