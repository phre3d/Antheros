# Potion of the Hyena

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mind
>
>**Application:**Ingest <br>
>**Duration:** 10 minutes
>
>**Price:** 5,100gp

## Description

A yellow elixir. It smells like bread, but tastes like cinnamon.

## Effects

Causes fits of uncontrollable, highly contagious, laughter. Every round the imbiber fails a DC 14 Wisdom check, everyone within 20' of that person must also make a save or burst out laughing for 1d3 rounds. No other actions can be taken when afflicted with laughter.

## History

Created by a bard to enhance his comedic performances
