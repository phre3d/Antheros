# Oil of Sharpness, Minor

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Potion <br>
>**Rarity:** Rare <br>
>**Effect Category:** Mechanic
>
>**Application:**Coat <br>
>**Duration:** 1 hour
>
>**Price:** 3,900gp

## Description

A glowing, pulsing draught that is indigo in color. It smells like blood, but tastes like apples and cherries.

The oil can coat one slashing or piercing weapon or up to 5 pieces of slashing or piercing ammunition. Applying the oil takes 1 minute.

## Effects

The coated item is magical and has a a bonus to attack and damage rolls of +2.
