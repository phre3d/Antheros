# Dragon Egg

>

>**Duration:** Special 
>
>**Price:** 6,500gp


## Description
This small iron-bound wooden cask is filled with an alchemical mix of strange powders and liquids. It smells very strongly of sulfur. A fuse protrudes from the top of the cask. The cask weighs 20 lbs.

## Effects
Once lit, the fuse burns quickly. 1d4 + 1 rounds after you ignite the fuse, the cask explodes. Each creature in a 20-foot radius must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw. A target takes 8d6 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one. 

If the dragon's egg cask suffers any fire or lightning damage, it explodes at the end of the next round.
