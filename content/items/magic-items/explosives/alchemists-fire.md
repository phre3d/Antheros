# Alchemist's Fire

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Explosive <br>
>**Rarity:** Common <br>
>**Effect Category:** Offensive
>
>**Application:**Thrown <br>
>**Duration:** Special 
>
>**Price:** 75gp


## Description
This sticky, adhesive fluid ignites when exposed to air.

## Effects
As an action, you can throw this flask up to 20 feet, shattering it on impact. 

Make a ranged attack against a creature or object, treating the alchemist's fire as an improvised weapon. On a hit, the target takes 1d4 fire damage at the start of each of its turns. 

A creature can end this damage by using its action to make a DC 10 Dexterity check to extinguish the flames.
