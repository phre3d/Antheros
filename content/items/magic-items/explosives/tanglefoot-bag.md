# Tanglefoot Bag

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Explosive <br>

>
>**Application:**Thrown <br>
>**Duration:** 1 minute 
>
>**Price:** 120gp


## Description
A sealed canvas bag that appears to be about 1' in diameter. When jostled, it appears to contain some form of goo.

## Effects
This fragile sack contains coiled strands of fibrous alchemical goo that expand and become sticky when exposed to air. 

As an action, you can throw the bag up to 30 feet, causing an effect identical to the web spell (DC 12), filling a 5- foot cube, for 1 minute. 

Large creatures have advantage on saving throws against the effects of the goo. Huge or larger creatures are unaffected. The bag does not function underwater.
