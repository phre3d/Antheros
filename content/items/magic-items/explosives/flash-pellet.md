# Flash Pellet

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item / Explosive <br>
>**Rarity:** Common <br>
>**Effect Category:** Offensive
>
>**Application:**Thrown <br>
>**Duration:** Special
>
>**Price:** 100gp

## Description

brown, walnut-sized pellet of what appears to be packed sawdust.

## Effects

When thrown against a hard surface, this pellet explodes in a blinding flash of light.  Each creature in a 5-foot radius must succeed at a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw to avert their gaze or are blinded for 1 round and suffer disadvantage on any attack rolls or Wisdom (Perception) checks based on sight 1 round additional round after that. Creatures with a vulnerability to bright light have disadvantage on this save.
