# Pomander of Warding

>[!info]
>**Item Type/Category:** Magic Item <br>
>**Rarity:** Uncommon <br>
>**Effect Category:** Protection
>
>**Application:**Inhale <br>
>**Duration:** 8 hours 
>
>**Price:** 300gp


## Description
A ball of aromatic spices and perfumes contained in a loose-weave cloth bag.  Normally wrapped tightly in parchment to keep the contents from seeping out until ready for use.

## Effects
Once unwrapped,  it fills an area in a 15-foot radius with a strong aroma for 8 hours.

Undead creatures of CR 2 or lower that enter this area must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC 15) or be turned as detailed in the Turn Undead cleric feature.
