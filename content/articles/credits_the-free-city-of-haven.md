The city of Haven and it's contents are based on two modules originally published by Gamelords Ltd. in the early 1980s:  *The Free City of Haven* , and  *The Secrets of the Labyrinth* . Sadly, the planned third module was never published, or at least I can find no record of it. 

In order to adapt the original work to my own purposes I have made a number of changes mainly consisting of the following:
- updating statblocks to 5e
- replacing orc NPCs with tieflings
- filling in some missing content on the North Corridor
- specific placement of individual shops, inns, etc.
- adjusting for some inconsistencies
- replacing the deities and religions


Due credit goes to the original authors of Haven...
- Kevin Hardwick, Kerry Lloyd,Richard Meyer, Larry Richardson, Janet Trautvetter, Michael Watkins, and Michael Welker;



...and to the art and graphics creators:
- Wallace Miller, Dave Scalzi, Hannah M. G. Shapero, Larry Shade, Janet Trautvetter, Michael Welker, and V. M. Wyman.
