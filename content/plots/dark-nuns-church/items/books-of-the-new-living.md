# Properties
Magic books that hold the secrets to reanimating the dead. All three volumes are needed to ensure that the spells are cast correctly.

### Contained Spells
When you attack with any spells learned from the “Books of the New Living”, you deal extra 1d6 necrotic damage.  If however the attack missed,  you **take** 1d8 necrotic damage.

---

# Description
These three books are old and moldy with puss oozing out of the pages. There is only one reason these books exist: to reanimate the dead!
