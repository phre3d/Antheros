# General Info
# Description 
Ellyn Cloudfang, the Dark Mother Superior, is a 50-year-old female half-elf.
- She has short, straight, blond hair and green eyes.
- She has rugged red skin.
- She stands 187cm (6'2") tall and has a lean build.
- She has an oval, awful face.
- She wears leather armor beneath her habit.


# Personality 
- She proudly worships [[person/azriel|Azriel]]: Goddess of Disease & Suffering, Mistress of Pain
- She is very pessimistic. She uses a beautiful walking cane.


# Other 
Her blood is the cure to any terrible and deadly sickness. Ironic, given who she worships.
