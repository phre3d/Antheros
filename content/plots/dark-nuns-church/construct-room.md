# Outline
The Construct Room is where the Dark Nuns cast their reanimation spells to bring back the dead. There is a private altar in the room with a partially mummified body. Gore-encrusted chains on the walls contain the remains of dead animals that are also used in the reanimation process. Two sets of stocks at the back of the room hold Lena and Tatum.

> [!read-aloud] **Read Aloud** 
 >  
 > This appears to be a room directly from hell. A small altar has a mummified body wrapped on it. Chains, where people have been kept, line the walls. There is a large work table down the middle of the room - on the table are a verious knives, a potted plant, various other surgical tools and clamps. Three stained glass windows allow some light in. On the floor by the northeast door is a small chest.  At the south end of the room there are stocks, imprisoning a human male and a tiefling woman.


The mummy will <u>animate if the players touch it or the potted plant</u>.
