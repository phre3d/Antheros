# Special Abilities
### Blood Frenzy 
 The bear has an advantage on melee atack rolls against any creature that doesn't have all its hit points.  
### Keen Smell 
 The bear has an advantage in Wisdom (Perception)  checks that rely on smell.

# Actions
### Multiattack
###  The bear makes two atacks: one with its bite and one with its claws.  
### Bite 
  **Melee Weapon Attack:**  +7 to hit , reach 5 ft., one target. 
  **Hit:**  12 (1d8 + 5) piercing damage.  
### Claws 
  **Melee Weapon Attack:**  +7 to hit , reach 5 ft., one target. 
  **Hit:**  14 (2d6 + 5) slashing damage.  
### Frightful Presence 
 Each creature of the bear's choice that is within 60 feet of it and can see it must succeed on a <u>DC 14 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened</u> for 2 rounds. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each turn, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a creature's saving  throw succeeds or the effect ends, the creature is immune to the bear's Frightful Presence for the next 24 hours.  
### Trample 
 Great Black can make a 'Trample' atack as an action. Each creature in a <u>10-foot-wide and 20-foot-long line</u> in Great Black's path must make a <u>DC 15 Strength saving throw</u>. A target takes 10  bludgeoning damage on a failed save and is knocked prone. On a successful save, the target takes half as much damage and isn't knocked prone. A creature that <u>fails its saving throw by 5 or more</u> is also stunned until the end of its next turn.
