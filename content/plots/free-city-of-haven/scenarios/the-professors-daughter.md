# Outline
## Players Information 
Nimbel Harrock, a renowned alchemist of the [[landmark/school-of-alchemy|School of Alchemy]], has posted several signs around the [[settlement/magic-street|Magic Street]]/Genius Park areas, which offers a reward for information concerning his daughter Jewl, a voluptuous young lady who has disappeared a little over a week ago from her father's home. He fears that she has been taken by bandits or kidnappers.

## GM's Notes 
Jewl was last seen in Adrielle's shop, [[landmark/fine-silks-and-fabrics|Fine Silks and Fabrics]], on Lorien Street - she was not, however,spirited out of the city by the slaving operation. She simply moved out of her father's house, taking a newly bought wardrobe, and has moved in with a master thief, Tryvart. He is a tall, handsome man that she met in the [[landmark/professors-challenge|Professor's Challenge]] (he likes to play chess), and subsequently fell in love with. 

She is certain her father would totally disapprove of her choice, so she has neither told him of Tryvart or her wish to marry him. They are accumulating the funds necessary to leave the city and purchase a small farm (Tryvart's boyhood dream). 

Jewl's best friend, Lalie Bulin, knows where she is staying and visits her occasionally, but will not inform Jewl's father until the lovers have left the city.
