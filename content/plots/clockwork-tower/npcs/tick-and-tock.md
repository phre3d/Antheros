# General Info
Tick and Tock were among [[person/constantines|Constantine's]] most cherished creations. The clockwork birds served not only as clockwork marvels but also as his companions. He imbued them with a fraction of his genius, enabling them to understand and navigate the labyrinthine complexity of the Clockwork Tower.

- **Tick** , the slightly larger of the pair, has a more assertive personality. He often takes the lead, making decisions and guiding the way through the tower's twisting passages.
- **Tock** , on the other hand, is more cautious and methodical. He carefully analyzes each situation, often catching details that Tick may miss in his haste.


Despite their different personalities, the two work together seamlessly, with their combined intellect and abilities allowing them to overcome challenges that would confound individual constructs. They flutter through the Clockwork Tower, fixing minor malfunctions, rerouting misaligned cogs, and oiling squeaky gears, but their primary task is to guide visitors through the treacherous tower, making them invaluable allies for any who seek to explore Constantine's masterpiece.

## Plot: Clockwork Tower
Tick and Tock are designed to serve as guides and helpers, not combatants. They are best used to provide clues, assist in overcoming obstacles, or repair damaged mechanisms within the Clockwork Tower. Their ability to fly also allows them to access places that would be unreachable for the adventurers.
