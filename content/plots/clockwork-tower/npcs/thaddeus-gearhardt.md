# General Info
A lifelong scholar, Professor Thaddeus Gearhardt has devoted his existence to the pursuit of knowledge, specializing in the intertwining domains of artifice and arcane engineering. Known for his eccentric personality and disheveled appearance, Professor Gearhardt's cluttered office is a testament to his untamed intellect, filled with blueprints, arcane tomes, and half-finished contraptions.

An enthusiastic historian, Gearhardt has always been fascinated by the figure of [[person/constantine-chronosmith|Constantine Chronosmith]]. He has studied Chronosmith's life and work intensively, viewing him as a maverick genius of his time. When the Clockwork Tower materialized, Gearhardt was thrilled at the opportunity to delve into Chronosmith's magnum opus. While he's cautious of the dangers that the tower might pose, he can't help but be exhilarated at the prospect of learning more about the man who single-handedly revolutionized the field of temporal artifice over a century ago.

Gearhardt is always eager to share his knowledge with anyone who shares his curiosity, and despite his absent-minded professor persona, he's quite perceptive and quick to notice details others might overlook. His expertise on Chronosmith and the Clockwork Tower could prove invaluable to those seeking to understand and navigate the tower's intricate mechanisms and temporal enigmas.

## [[plots/plot:-clockwork-tower|Plot: Clockwork Tower]] 
Professor Gearhardt is far more useful in an advisory role than in combat. His vast knowledge of history, arcane engineering, and his specialization in the work of [[person/constantine-chronosmith|Constantine Chronosmith]] make him a valuable asset to any party attempting to navigate the Clockwork Tower. Utilize his abilities as a font of knowledge rather than a combatant.
