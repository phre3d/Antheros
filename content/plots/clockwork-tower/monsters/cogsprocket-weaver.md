# Special Abilities

### Immutable Form

 The Cogsprocket Weaver is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

### Fabricate Spider

 As a bonus action, the Cogsprocket Weaver can begin crafting a Cogsprocket Spider. After two rounds, the spider becomes operational and acts on the Weaver's initiative count. A maximum of 3 spiders can be active at a time via this method.

### Repair Aura

 Constructs within 10 feet of the Weaver at the start of its turn regain 5 hit points.

# Description

The Cogsprocket Weaver resembles a blend of a spider and a craftsman's workbench. Its body is a collection of intricate gears, arms, and tools, all meant to craft and repair its smaller Cogsprocket Spider counterparts quickly. While not as combative as the Cogsprocket Sentinel, it plays a vital role in maintaining the swarm's numbers during confrontations.  The Weaver has a set of retractable tendrils that it uses for both crafting and defense. Its underbelly houses a mini-factory, capable of churning out the smaller spiders with impressive speed. This mini-factory glows  with a soft blue light, indicating its continuous operation. When not in combat, the Weaver works tirelessly, producing, repairing, and modifying constructs, ensuring the Clockwork Tower remains in impeccable condition. If threatened, the Weaver prioritizes the creation and repair of its spider allies, leveraging numbers and maintaining operational integrity above all else.
