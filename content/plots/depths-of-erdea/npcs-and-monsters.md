# NPCs / Named Monsters 
## Jubal 
A dark and twisted  **ogre**  with some spellcasting ability (although no Ogre Mage by any stretch of the imagination). He can cast  *Bane*  (the opposite of Bless),  *Protection from Good* , and  *Fear* , each once per day as a level 4 cleric. His massive staff with dangling skulls and feathers makes the targets of his fear spell save at a -2 penalty. 

The magical charms on the staff can be transferred to a smaller staff or similar weapon, allowing the magical benefit to be passed on to smaller spellcasters.

## CueOne & CueTwo 
A year ago Jubal split his dark and twisted soul into three pieces – one that he keeps himself, and two that have split off into smaller little “essences of Jubal” that he calles CueOne and CueTwo. While looking like  **tiny ogres** ,  they are <u>re-skinned Quasits</u> (and thus size "small"). 

CueOne will likely be first encountered in [room 17](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/antheros-phre3d/a/doe3A-level-1-plot#17) while CueTwo is stationed in [area 16](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/antheros-phre3d/a/doe3A-level-1-plot#16).

## Orthur 
An  **ogre** , Orthur is a lieutenant to Vera / Jubal and based out of [room 19](https://www.worldanvil.com/w/antheros-phre3d/a/doe3A-level-1-plot#19). He wishes that his bosses would get along.
