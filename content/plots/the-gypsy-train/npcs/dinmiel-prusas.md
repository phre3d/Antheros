# Description
Guard. [[plots/the-gypsy-train/npcs/bronkar-prusas|Bronkar Prusas']] son

Dinmiel is really too young for the job of guard, being only age 10, but the group needed someone to fill the position for this wagon, and since his father is the driver, he was picked for the task. This has given him a slight ego problem, which most of the band try to ignore.

Dinmiel is a 1st-level human fighter, chaotic neutral, with Str 9, Int 9, Wis 7, Dex 11. Con 11, Cha 7, AC 8, and 7 hp. He wears leather armor and uses a _short sword_, a _footman’s pick_, and a _dagger_.
