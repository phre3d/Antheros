# Description

Driver, [[plots/the-gypsy-train/npcs/bronkar-prusas|Bronkar's]] wife

Trynkara is a patient woman in her late 20s who is very happy that her husband has found a job he can hold. She is friendly and can get along with most anyone (as evi­denced by the fact that she shares a wagon with [[plots/the-gypsy-train/npcs/ezerina-galinys|Ezerina Galinys]]).

Trynkara is a 1st-level human thief, neu­tral good, with Str 6, Int 9, Wis 13, Dex 15, Con 14, Cha 13, AC 7, and 6 hp. She wears leather armor and uses a *sling* with 20 bullets and a *dagger*.
