# Collar of Communication

**Item Type:** Wondrous Item<br>
**Rarity:** Unique<br>
**Price:** <br>
**Weight:** .5 lb<br>
**Attunement:** No

---

# Properties

 It is enchanted such that any animal wearing it is granted intelligence and the ability to speak aloud and communicate (using Common) with other sentient creatures as per the *Awaken* spell.

# Description

An adjustable collar is made of black tooled leather.
