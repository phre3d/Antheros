# General Info
In life, Artemis Nahanna was a handsome male high-elf noble (LN). He had long silvery hair, violet-blue eyes, and high cheekbones. At the time of his death, he wore a Winterhold Guards' uniform with a cornflower-blue armband worn only by high-ranking soldiers. Artemis came from a family of aristocrats in Winterhold. As the youngest sibling, he became a soldier as per tradition and later joined the Winterhold Guards.

 **Armour and weapon:**  Artemis carried a longsword, a light crossbow, and breastplate armour.

 **Trait:**  The common people are beneath me, and I treat them accordingly.

 **Ideal:**  Aspiration. I hope to one day become captain of the Winterhold Guards.

 **Bond:**  I would do anything to protect my sister, Thaneli'a.

 **Flaw:**  I firmly believe in law and order, but if I can bend the rules without breaking them to profit, I will.
