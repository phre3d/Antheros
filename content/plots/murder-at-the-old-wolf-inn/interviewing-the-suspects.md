# Interviewing the Suspects

A key part of figuring out who murdered [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/artemis-nahanna|Artemis Nahanna]] is gathering useful information by talking to the NPCs. Almost everyone at the Old Wolf Inn has something to hide – for one reason or another. 

The statistics of all the NPCs are listed in the core rulebooks, but the DM can modify these as they wish. 

The suspects are:
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/cael-thraelive|Cael Thraelive]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/enoch-the-birdwhisperer|Enoch the Birdwhisperer]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/hugo-von-silverton|Hugo von Silverton]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/inga-broadhammer|Inga Broadhammer]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/jon-callion|Jon Callion]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/kara-tanner|Kara Tanner]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/vittra-northdaughter|Vittra Northdaughter]]
- [[plots/murder-at-the-old-wolf-inn/npcs/william-fastfinger|William Fastfinger]]
