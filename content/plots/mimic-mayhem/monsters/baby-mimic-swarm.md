# Special Abilities
**Swarm.** The swarm can occupy another creature’s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny rat. The swarm can’t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

**Ankle-Biters.** If a creature begins their turn sharing a space with a swarm, the creature suffers 1d6 piercing damage from bites. 

**Magic Resistance.** The mimic swarm has advantage on all saving throws against spells and magic.
