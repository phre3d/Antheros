# Special Abilities

**Shapechanger.** The mimic can use its action to polymorph into an object or back into its true, amorphous form. Its statistics are the same in each form. Equipment is not transformed. It reverts to its true form if it dies.

**False Appearance** (Object Form Only). While motionless, the mimic is indistinguishable from an ordinary object.

<mark style="background:#d3f8b6">**Adhesive Pseudopods.** The pseudopods adhere to anything they touch. A creature adhered to one is also grappled by it (escape DC 13). Ability checks made to escape this grapple have disadvantage.</mark>

<mark style="background:#d3f8b6">**Grappler.** The mimic has advantage on attack rolls against any creature grappled by it.</mark>

<mark style="background:#ff4d4f">**Magic Resistance.** The mimic has advantage on all saving throws against spells and magic.</mark>

<mark style="background:#ff4d4f">**Regeneration.** As one of their attacks, the mimic can swallow a pseudopod to instantly heal 30 hp. It regenerates a new pseudopod in 1d4 days.</mark>

# Actions

<mark style="background:#fff88f">**Multi-Attack.** The mimic can make three attacks. One with each of its two pseudopods and one with its bite.</mark>

**Pseudopod.** <br>
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, <u>reach 10 ft.</u>, one target. <br>
*Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) bludgeoning damage <mark style="background:#d3f8b6">and grappled. <br></mark>

<mark style="background:#d3f8b6">A Pseudopod that is already grappling someone does not attack again but instead tries to pull their victim ten feet closer by winning an opposed Athletics check.</mark>

**Bite.** <br>
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +8 to hit, <u>reach 5 ft.</u>, one target. <br>
*Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage plus 9 (2d8) acid damage.

- If the target is Small, it must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity saving throw or be swallowed by the mimic. A swallowed creature is blinded and restrained, it has total cover against  attacks and other effects outside the mimic, and it takes 7 (2d6) acid damage at the start of each of the mimic’s turns.
- If the mimic takes 10 damage or more on a single turn from a creature inside it, the mimic must succeed on a DC 21 Constitution saving throw at the end of that turn or regurgitate all swallowed creatures, which fall prone in a space within 10 feet of the mimic. If the mimic dies, a swallowed creature is no longer restrained by it and can escape from the corpse by using 20 feet of movement, exiting prone.

**Spit.** <br>
*Ranged Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, <u>range 30 ft.</u>, one target. <br>
*Hit:* 7 (2d6) acid damage.
