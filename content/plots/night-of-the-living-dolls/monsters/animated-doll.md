# Special Abilities

**False Appearance:**  While the doll remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a regular toy.

  **Magic Resistance:**  The animated doll has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

  **Keen Hearing:**  The doll has advantage on Wisdom (Perception) checks that rely on hearing.

  **Multiattack:**  The animated doll makes two slam attacks.

### Creepy Lullaby

  **Recharge 5–6**

 The doll lets out a haunting melody, forcing all creatures within 20 feet that can hear it to make a DC 12 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. The frightened target can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

# Description

This animated doll is about two feet tall, with porcelain skin that is chipped and cracked in places. Its round, glassy eyes are wide open, staring unblinkingly as it moves with jerky, unnatural motions. Dressed in a tattered, old-fashioned dress, it clutches a small wooden toy or a child's accessory in one hand. Its once-rosy cheeks are now pale, and a faint grin is permanently etched into its painted face, making its expression both innocent and menacing at the same time.   These dolls were originally crafted as gifts for children by an eccentric toy maker who poured his heart and soul into each creation. However, when his beloved daughter tragically passed away, the grief-stricken toy maker turned to dark magic, binding malevolent spirits to his once-wholesome creations. Now, these animated dolls roam freely, filled with twisted, childlike malice. They often remain motionless, pretending to be harmless toys until the moment is right to strike, their eternal mission to seek vengeance on those who wronged their creator.
