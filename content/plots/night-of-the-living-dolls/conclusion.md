# Outline
The players successfully defeat the Cursed Doll and use the Cursed Mirror to trap and release her soul. With the 
doll's destruction, the dark magic that haunted the toy shop dissipates, and the eerie presence within the shop fades. 
The souls of the animated toys are freed, and the shop returns to its lifeless state. However, the emotional weight of 
the toy maker's tragedy lingers. The players have freed the town from the curse, but the remnants of the toy maker's 
sorrow are still felt. 

As the final blow strikes the Cursed Doll, her porcelain body shatters, and a haunting, sorrowful wail echoes 
through the room. The pieces of the doll crumble to dust, and for a brief moment, the air feels lighter. The dark 
magic that once filled the room fades, and the strange, oppressive energy dissipates. The toys that had been 
moving moments before now lie still, their glassy eyes no longer filled with malevolence, but simply empty once 
more. 

You hold up the Cursed Mirror, and as you gaze into its cracked surface, you see the faint image of a young girl-
her face no longer twisted in rage, but instead peaceful, calm. With a final whisper, she thanks you, her form 
growing faint until it disappears entirely. The mirror goes cold in your hands, the magic within it spent. All around 
you, the dolls collapse to the ground, lifeless once more, and the shop falls into an eerie, heavy silence. 
The curse has been broken. The town is safe. Yet, as you step out of the basement workshop and into the dark 
streets once more, a chill runs down your spine. The toy maker's sorrow may have been silenced, but the legacy of 
his grief lingers, a quiet reminder that some tragedies can never fully be erased. 

# Reward for Completing the Adventure 
### Gold & Trinkets 
 Upon searching the toy shop after the curse is lifted, the players find a hidden stash of gold coins 
beneath the floorboards (200 gp), along with a collection of rare trinkets and mechanical toys, worth an additional 
150 gp. 

### Experience Points (XP) 
 Award the players 1,800 XP each for defeating the Cursed Doll, breaking the curse, and 
solving the mystery of the toy shop. 

### Reputation in the Town 
 The players become local heroes for saving the town from the haunting curse. Merchants 
and townsfolk offer them discounts and hospitality whenever they return, and they are forever remembered as the 
ones who lifted the curse of the toy shop.
