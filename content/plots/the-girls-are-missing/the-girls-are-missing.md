# Outline
This isn't part of a module but rather a series of events that may or may not be related to each other.  They interweave in time with the all disappearances taking place between 10th of Wealsun and the 12th.  Unless you want to include [[person/byelaya-tainley|Byelaya Tainley]] , in which case it goes back further - to the 24th of Coldeven.  (c.f. # Olivia and Seraphina 
[[person/olivia-chronosmith|Olivia Chronosmith]] and [[person/seraphina-steamspark|Seraphina Steamspark]] were kidnapped on 10th of Wealsun by [[organization/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]] as a way to gain the tower's secrets.

They are being held in a cave used for Hand slave smuggling. Marita is also there. The cave can be accessed via [[landmark/fine-silks-and-fabrics|Fine Silks and Fabrics]].

# Marita 
[[person/marita-maggia|Marita Maggia]] was taken by [[organization/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]] on 10th of Wealsun at the request of [[person/mikyl-swiftsword|Mikyl Swiftsword]]. 

This is an attempt to discredit [[person/lyza|Lyza's]] new command - once it comes out the Irreguars were working to get Marita married it will look like a kidnapping by them.

She is being held in a cave used for Hand slave smuggling. Olivia and Seraphina are also there. The cave can be accessed via [[landmark/fine-silks-and-fabrics]].

# Byelaya (Bianca) Tainley 
[[person/byelaya-tainley|Byelaya Tainley]] left home on 24th of Coldeven and becomes the courtesan [[person/bianca|Bianca]], working at [[landmark/the-blue-lantern|The Blue Lantern]] on [[settlements/the-street-of-silk-veils|The Street of Silk Veils]].

She is quite happy in her new role and doesn't want to leave it. She has sent letters home saying she is safe and happy and until recently, her parents have resisted searching for her. They were of the belief that she should be allowed to "sow her wild oats" but are now regretting the decision to not send her for fostering like her brother.

Her mother Stella  is of the opinion that the family let Byelaya be, but her father wants to at least know where she is and the life she now leads. He might approach the party asking for help on this.

A couple days ago (9th of Wealsun) she was spotted by [[person/cortina-de-la-riis|Cortina de la Riis]].

**Related plot(s):**  [[plots/free-city-of-haven/scenarios/the-noblemans-daughter|The Nobleman's Daughter]]

# Jillie 
[[person/martin-plowhearth|Martin Plowhearth's]] girlfriend [[person/jillie-ferndew|Jillie Ferndew]] has disappeared but the reason is not sinister. After getting off work on the 11th of Wealsun she went home where she found her mother quite ill. She was distracted by looking after her and forgot to let anyone know.

**Related plot(s):**  None
