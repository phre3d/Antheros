# Outline
## What the Players Believe 
- MS isn't happy that [[person/lyzas|Lyza's]] command exists 
	- **True** , he doesn't want her to succeed as it might rival his power base.

- He argued against it at the city council 
	- **True** . It was seen in a flashback when the party visited the [[plots/ct:-council-chambers|CT: Council Chambers]] as part of [[plots/key:-clockwork-tower|Key: Clockwork Tower]] .

- He is behind the kidnapping of [[person/marita-maggia|Marita Maggia]] 
	- **True** .  He did it to discredit [[person/lyza|Lyza]] (e.g. her team's involvement got her taken by [[organization/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]) - he paid the Hand to do it.

- He is responsible for the kidnapping of Oliva Chronosmith and [[person/seraphina-steamspark|Seraphina Steamspark]] 
    - **False** . [[organization/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]] did it for their own reasons.

- MS is the leader of [[organization/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]
    - **False** . To MS the Hand is a useful tool.  To the Hand, MS is just a different kind of tool.


## Support for Swiftsword 
 **Supporters** 
- The  [[organization/government-of-haven#council-of-boroughs|Council of Boroughs]]  generally backs him.  They aren't the most imaginative people and think he's doing a fine job with the Watch as it is.
- Of the major merchant houses,  only the [[organization/varrasch-family|Varrasch Family]] and [[organization/ilorim-family|Ilorim Family]] support Swiftsword. The latter, due to the party's raid on Adrielle's shop ( [[landmark/fine-silks-and-fabrics|Fine Silks and Fabrics]] )
