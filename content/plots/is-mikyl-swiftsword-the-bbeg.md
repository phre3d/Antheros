# Is Mikyl Swiftsword the BBEG
As the plot progressed, [[people/mikyl-swiftsword|Mikyl Swiftsword]] was looking like a bad guy, maybe even the head of [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]. I had to start writing things down.

# What the Players Believe 
- MS isn't happy that [[people/lyza|Lyza's]] command exists 
	- **True** , he doesn't want her to succeed as it might rival his power base.

- He argued against it at the city council 
	- **True** . It was seen in a flashback when the party visited the [[plots/clockwork-tower/council-chambers|Council Chambers]] as part of [[plots/clockwork-tower|Clockwork Tower]] .

- He is behind the kidnapping of [[people/marita-maggia|Marita Maggia]] 
	- **True** .  He did it to discredit [[people/lyza|Lyza]] (e.g. her team's involvement got her taken by [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]) - he paid the Hand to do it.

- He is responsible for the kidnapping of Oliva Chronosmith and [[people/seraphina-steamspark|Seraphina Steamspark]] 
    - **False** . [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]] did it for their own reasons.

- MS is the leader of [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]
    - **False** . To Swiftsword the Hand is a useful tool.  To the Hand, Swiftsword is just a different kind of tool.
