# Description
## Piper upscale clothing for the masses…that are rich 

## Description 
An upscale clothier that is part of the Blackbourne empire. Piper is a well educated half-elf who knows her regular clientele and greets them by name. Piper and her staff of three can provide recommendations, fittings and adjustment services. If necessary, Piper will work with local tailors and seamstresses for a custom look.

## NPC Encounters 
Hamlin Blackbourne
