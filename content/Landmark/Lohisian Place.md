[[Antheros/Religion/Lohi|Lohi]], Goddess of Healing isn't worshipped in the traditional manner but rather through acts of succor for the ill and injured and healing. This site at the east end of [[settlement/Believer's Lane|Believer's Lane]] is therefore not a temple per se but rather three buildings that house varrying specialties.
