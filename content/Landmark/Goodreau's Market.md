# Description
## Everything you want in a shop and a little bit more 

## Description 
[[person/Goodreau|Goodreau]] carries a varied selection of fresh meats (kept in a magically constructed Cold storage box), fruits and dairy products, all of good quality. Most of the patrons of the store are the kitchen servants of the estates. Consequently, there is a great deal of gossip passed back and forth over the counter here (only about 40% of it true).
