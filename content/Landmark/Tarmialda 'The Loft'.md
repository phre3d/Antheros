# Description
## Elven cuisine - par excellence 

## Description 
[[person/Skorindir Elengul|Skorindir Elengul]] - is the only elf in Haven with white hair.

## Exterior 
A 3,000 year old tree near Calenarth (the Meadow) has a wooden staircase winding around its trunk. Visible from the ground are wooden tables, chairs, and planked flooring about 30 feet up.

## Interior 
The public "room" of The Loft is a labyrinth of branches and secluded eating areas, lit by elven magic (the inn seats up to 300). The center of the area is a circular bar and staircase set into the hollow area of the trunk, where food is prepared and drinks are mixed. 

Any non-elf in the tree must be very careful to avoid losing balance while making sudden movements, a saving roll against DEX must be made to prevent falling, and, if missed, a saving roll against DEX must be made to clutch on to a branch or limb. If drunk, a character's saving roll against falling are at discretionary penalties.

## Hours 
2:00pm to 4:00am (the elves enjoy stargazing).

## Price Range 
The cuisine at The Loft is renowned throughout Haven. The prices are high, but worth every penny.

## Quality 
Superb, what else did thee expecteth?

## Cash Box 
In the trunk of the tree is a large pouch with [roll:1d20*1d20]gp in mixed coinage.

## Staff 
Along with Skorindir, who supervises the kitchen area most of the time, is his son Taubardur, called Taub by his friends, who works many jobs in the business. There will be one serving person (usually a half-elf, 80% female, 20% male) per 20 customers in attendance. The kitchen is manned by 4 master cooks, and a number of journeymen and apprentices.

## Customers 
An average of 60 customers can be found in the Loft in the afternoon, and at least 200 will be in attendance during the early evening hours.

[[person/Bakka Hofstra|Bakka Hofstra]] | [[person/Kruegger Rand|Kruegger Rand]] | [[person/Keyan Dorin|Keyan Dorin]]

## Outstanding Items 
None. When all is superb, how can anyone be outstanding?
