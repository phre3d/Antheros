# Description
## House of Seaman's Delights 

## Description 
A brothel modeled loosely on the more elaborate establishments on [[Settlement/The Street of Silk Veils|The Street of Silk Veils]], it is, however, generally referred to by the local permanent residents as the "Scales of the Fish". The owner, [[person/Madame Dorilla|Madame Dorilla]], was once a very popular courtesan on [[Settlement/The Street of Silk Veils|The Street of Silk Veils]]. Now she is tired, middle-aged, and disillusioned, and was easy prey for [[Organization/Criminal/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]]'s extortionists, who collect a sizable portion of the money brought in by the Rose's dozen or so girls as "insurance".

## NPC Encounters 
[[person/Moonlight|Moonlight]]

## Special Options 
One of the girls, a sharp-witted half-elf named [[person/Moonlight|Moonlight]], has managed to discover unusual information (see [[Plot/Free City of Haven/Haven Scenarios|Haven Scenarios]]: Rescue by Moonlight). She has not yet acted on this information, since she has no way to escape [[Organization/Criminal/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]]'s certain and deadly revenge.
