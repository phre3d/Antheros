# Description
## Tools and Services 

## Description 
This shop, run by [[person/Amsterdam Craddock|Amsterdam Craddock]], sells both house and garden tools, as well as small pieces of agricultural equipment. They also do simple repairs and provide a variety of small miscellaneous services (e.g., blade sharpening). Like [[person/Goodreau|Goodreau]]'s, most of the clientele is comprised of servants and hired hands from the estates. There is less idle talk in the air here overall, but one has a much better chance of learning which estates (if any) are in the market to hire on new help.

## Price Range 
High.

## Quality 
Fair.
