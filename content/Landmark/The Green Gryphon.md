# Description
## A quiet pub 

## Description 
The Gryphon is in a little better shape than neighboring buildings. It has a stone first floor and stucco and half-timber on the overhanging second floor with green shuttered windows. A gryphon is painted on the door in green paint. 

 The proprietor of the Gryphon is [[person/Marco|Marco]] d'Emil, a lean, sinister man garbed in black, dark haired and bearded, who has never been known to smile except after killing someone (rumor has it that killing is something that [[person/Marco|Marco]] is very good at). Violence is very rare at the Gryphon, however, as [[person/Marco|Marco]] has been known to have brawlers quieted - permanently. 

 [[person/Marco|Marco]] does no serving of customers, leaving that task to three attractive "barmaids", who also entertain customers upstairs - after clearing it with [[person/Marco|Marco]] first, of course.
