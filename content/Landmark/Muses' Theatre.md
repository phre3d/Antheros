# Description
## Not really Mount Helicon 

## Description 
[[person/Phineous Tarkin|Phineous Tarkin]] (see NPC's The South Corridor)

## Exterior 
A lovely Greek-styled building of marble and granite, with heavy bronze doors, fluted columns and an elaborate frieze on the eaves depicting nine young women in various poses and costume. A sign out front has a poster for the new play, "The Sorcerer's Secret", but the date has been scratched out and "coming soon" is written in over it.

## Interior 
Theater has recently renovated and refurbished, at great expense. The floors are marble tile, the walls have niches with lovely statuary in them, there is new upholstery on the seats in Theater itself, and new curtains and drapes for the stage and boxes. The effect is of elegant opulence. The stage is set as a mage's workshop with books, a stuffed owl, a globe, humorous scrolls, a huge tome chained to a stand, wands, jars of mysterious substances, an ornate iron candelabra, a skull, a tall desk with parchment, pens and ink bottles, and a black cloak on a wall peg, to name a few items.  

 There are dressing rooms and costume storage rooms backstage, and various other scenery and props.

It isn't open
