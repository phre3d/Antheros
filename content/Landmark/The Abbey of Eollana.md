## In the service of the Goddess 

## Description 
The elven Abbey of Eollana, was founded to commemerate an elven prophet of the goddess [[Religion/Serindë|Serindë]].  Eollana focused on [[Religion/Serindë|Serindë's]] role as Mother of the Gods and interpreted that as reflecting a desire to provide assistance to all women and mothers.  Accordingly, the abbey  consists of a shrine to the Goddess, a hospice for women seeking shelter or the aid of healers and midwives, and facilities for the sisters and novices of the Abbey.

The Abbey plays an important role in the day-to-day life of the elves of [[Settlement/The Cauldron|The Cauldron]]. Its sisters and novices maintain [[landmark/Dancing Green|Dancing Green]], and organize many of the special celebrations held there. 

The Abbess, a graceful Elven woman of middle years named [[person/Galeana|Galeana]], is also frequently called in as an impartial arbitrator in neighborhood disputes based on [[Religion/Serindë|Serindë]]'s role as seeker of truth.  [[person/Galeana|Galeana]] takes this responsibility very seriously.  [[person/Galeana|Galeana]] is also an outspoken opponent of the growing materialism of the city's elves. Her relations with the members of the [[landmark/House of Flanderin|House of Flanderin]] are frosty at best. 

The Shrine, open to the public, contains a beautiful marble and gold leaf statue of the Goddess (in elven form), and is visited frequently by worshippers. 

Abbess [[Person/Galeana|Galeana]] oversees 30 sisters (including the healer [[Person/T'Sonia|T'Sonia]] ) and 12 novices. The retired Abbess, Mother [[Person/Kai|Kai]], spends much of her time on [[landmark/The Green|The Green]], and is a favorite among [[Settlement/The Cauldron|The Cauldron]]'s children for her storytelling.
