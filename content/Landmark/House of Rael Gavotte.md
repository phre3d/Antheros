# Description
## Dwelling of the Guilder 

## Description 
A luxurious three floor house of fine stone, enclosed in a high stone wall, with an ornate bronze gate. The interior is well furnished and the gardens out back are well-tended, with beautiful statuary.  

 The studio (and living quarters) of [[person/Gustav Morris|Gustav Morris]], a young energetic composer working under the patronage of the [[Organization/Families/Maggia Family|Maggia Family]]. Tall and thin, with curly blond hair (Gustav works odd hours, plunking on a harpsichord far into the night). Gustav has a well-deserved reputation for eccentricity, both in his person and his music.
