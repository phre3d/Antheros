# Description
## The name says it all 

## Description 
In this quaint-e little-e tavern-e can be found the chief hangout for members of the [[Organization/Criminal/Thieves Guild|Thieves Guild]] in this area. There is a 35% chance that any given person met in the common room is a member in good standing of Haven's largest criminal organization. By dropping the proper word or sign (or an appropriate amount of cash), one may make contact with fairly important people in the organization, possibly even one of the Guild's lieutenants. From there, events take their own courses.
