# Description
## A stockman's paradise 

## Description 
Another watering hole frequented by wranglers and stockmen, this establishment is neither as popular nor as quiet as [[landmark/The Iron Horse|The Iron Horse]]. There is a 15% chance for a brawl to break out every half-hour, as various herdsmen warm to the rebuttal in their debates about animal quality and worth. The taverner, [[person/Bakkin da Sadulagin|Bakkin da Sadulagin]], a large man with ham-like fists will usually wade in to settle disputes before too much furniture gets destroyed in the refutations. He is usually fairly successful in these endeavors.
