## Description 
This is a very simple inn and tavern with four large rooms, each with a number of dwarf-sized beds. [[person/Oskro|Oskro]] the innkeeper is a grouchy old fellow who suffers from rheumatism, gout, indigestion, and bad teeth, and is seldom seen to smile. He spends much of his time bedridden, leaving operation of the inn to his wife and daughter, who wait on him hand and foot.  

 [[person/Oskro|Oskro]] has never permitted the girl, Dallarie, to seek a suitor nor allowed any to court her, and would be livid indeed if he knew of the reason that [[person/Corvin arn Boltar|Corvin arn Boltar]] and his brothers are spending so much time at the inn (it certainly isn't the hospitality!). The girl is devoted to her father despite his temper, and is extremely shy around strangers (see [[Plot/Free City of Haven/Haven Scenarios|Haven Scenarios]]: The Bartered Brides).  

 The family lives in a private apartment in the inn.
