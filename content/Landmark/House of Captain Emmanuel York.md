# Description
## Just a residence 

## Description 
York maintains a small home here and will occasionally (25% chance) be in. He is the owner of a small merchantman, and hauls grain and fish for the Haven Market. [[Organization/Criminal/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]] once did him a favor, and he is willing to hire Hand operatives seeking a short vacation from Haven, even if they know nothing about sailing. York's aged servant, [[person/Howie|Howie]], a former sailor, lives at his house when York is not home.
