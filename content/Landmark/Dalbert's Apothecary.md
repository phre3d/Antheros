## Description 
[[person/Dalbert|Dalbert]] runs a small, low budget Apothecary on Datashi Street, in [[Settlement/The Commons|The Commons]]. The two halfling poisoners from [[landmark/Silvante's|Silvante's]] (Angelina's headquarters) buy the supplies necessary for their trade here. [[person/Dalbert|Dalbert]] is affiliated with [[Organization/Criminal/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]], and his shop also provides a convenient short term storage place for contraband opium and hashish.
