# Description
## [[person/Ninar Goldrush|Ninar Goldrush]] and [[person/Forti|Forti]] , Props. 

## Description 
Ninar and [[person/Forti|Forti]] are former wildcat prospectors who met years ago in the foothills of the Khuz and have survived as rather [[person/Rocky|Rocky]] business partners ever since. Ninar is both incredibly gullible and incredibly lucky, the kind of fellow who spends his life savings on an abandoned, worthless mine, and then falls through a hole in its shaft and discovers an untapped mother lode beneath. After years of feast and famine, [[person/Forti|Forti]] convinced his partner to abandon the miner's life for the "greater security" of owning this shop, which sells all manner of prospecting supplies.  

 Ninar and [[person/Forti|Forti]] used to have a third partner, T'mothee who recently died in a mine collapse. He had been demonstrating a "Special Revolutionary New, One-Size Fits All, Pick and Shovel Set" with to couple of local miners (Joe and Buoy, a pair of half-orcs), both of whom were rescued after several days in good health, but there was no sign of T'im (Timothy, The Buoys).  Needless to say, [[person/Forti|Forti]] and Ninar are still mourning and not on speaking terms at the moment with any half-orc.
