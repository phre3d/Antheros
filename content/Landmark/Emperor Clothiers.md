# Description
## Outfits with Style 

## Description 
Fandril is known around [[Settlement/The Heights|The Heights]] as a quality tailor who produces good, well-fitting clothing, whose styles are conservative rather than trendy high fashion. This reputation is justifiable where his steady customers are concerned, for Fandril and his assistants are very competent craftsmen. They will cheat on the quality of a garment (sometimes shamefully), however, if they believe the customer will not know the difference - by using a poorer grade of fabric, skimping on the amount of stitching done, etc. Such a garment will wear out in 30-80% (20 + 1d6 X 10) of its normal lifetime.

## Price Range 
High.

## Quality 
Shoddy/Excellent.
