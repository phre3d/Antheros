# Description

Technically part of [[settlements/the-cauldron|The Cauldron]], this area is bounded by Lorien Street, The Horseshoe, and [[settlements/magic-street|Magic Street]]. It is home to the [[organizations/university-of-haven|University of Haven]] and most of its related colleges:

- [[landmarks/college-of-common-law|College of Common Law]]
- [[landmarks/college-of-natural-philosophy|College of Natural Philosophy]]
- [[landmarks/college-of-physical-naturalism|College of Physical Naturalism]]
- [[landmarks/college-of-rhetoric|College of Rhetoric]]
- [[landmarks/institute-of-mathematics|Institute of Mathematics]]
- [[landmarks/school-of-alchemy|School of Alchemy]]
- [[landmarks/school-of-medicine|School of Medicine]]

Only the [[landmarks/college-of-the-mystic-arts|College of the Mystic Arts]] and [[landmarks/student-tenements|Student Tenements]] lay outside this neighborhood.
