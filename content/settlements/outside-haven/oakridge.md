# Description

# Description

Small town on the north side of the [[locations/carlemere-river|Carlemere River]], south of the [[locations/khuz-mountains|Khuz Mountains]].

Its recent growth can be attributed to an increase in traffic from the main north/south route along the [[locations/dorian-river|Dorian River]]. From [[settlements/outside-haven/oakridge|Oakridge]], one can travel north into the [[locations/khuz-mountains|Khuz Mountains]], eastward towards Bredakix or southeast to Brighton.

The residents of [[settlements/outside-haven/oakridge|Oakridge]] are primarily simple farmers and craftsmen. Recently, an old mine was reopened and number of miners have been hired (c.f. [[plots/mine-your-own-business/mine-your-own-business|Mine Your Own Business]]).

Notably, the village lacks its own guards, relying instead on the protection provided patrols from Haven or Brighton.

There's a small shop catering to the community's needs, offering a range of general goods and farming equipment.
