# Analgesic Veil

*4th-level conjuration*

````col
```col-md
flexGrow=2.5
===
An aurora of warding energy pours out from you, suffusing a 15 ft. radius area around you. You and all allied creatures within that area *roll three of their hit dice and gain that amount in temporary hit points.* These hit points last until they are lost, or until one minute passes. These* hit dice are only used for calculation and are not spent*.

Creatures affected by the spell become covered in a shimmering layer of magical energy that represents this spell.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, add one additional hit die to the roll for each slot level above 4th.
````
