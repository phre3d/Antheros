# Recolour

*1st level transmutation*

````col
```col-md
flexGrow=2.5
===
You touch an object or creature and its color changes to a new color or pattern of your choice. 

If you maintain concentration for the entire duration of the spell, the new color becomes permanent and no traces of magic remain. An unwilling target may make a Wisdom saving throw to avoid this effect.
```

```col-md
**Cast Time:** 1 action<br>
**Range:** touch<br>
**Area of Effect:** 1 creature <br>
**Components:** V, M (piece of coloured chalk) <br>
**Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes<br>
**Type:** Utility   <br>
**Attack / Save:** Wisdom<br>
**Ritual?** No  <br>
**Classes:** Wizard, Sorcerer<br>

```
````
