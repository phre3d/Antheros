# Description
This plant has a gnarled, erect and cyclindrical stem. It has wavy leavees, dark green on top and blusih underneath. It has pink flowers that cluseter in a spike at the top of the stem.

# Natural Range
Coastal

---

# Common Use(s)
## Unaltered Form
The root of this herb should be boiled over a low heat for three days, and the infusion drunk by people with the plauge, in order to cure some symptoms or the plague as a whole (60% chnace to cure completely). Because of this, the value of this herb increases greatly in times of plague.

## As an Ingredient
- Can be processed to produce  [[material/essence-of-life|Essence of Life]]
- Can be used in potion making

---

# Distribution
## Trade & Market

Can be found for sale by herbalists or in magic/alchemical supply shops. Occasionally, a potion maker may be persuaded to part with some but it will be with a 20%-50% markup.
