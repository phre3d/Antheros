# Description
A sponge-like surface plant that produces and stores air.

# Natural Range
Coastal

---

# Common Use(s)
## Unaltered Form
A fist sized piece can be held in the mouth to provide a medium creature with 5 minutes of air before the plant wilts and the recipient is back to normal breathing conditions for whatever the environment determines. This effect works underwater, in clouds of poison or dangerous spores, sandstorms, and other similar conditions.

## As an Ingredient
- Can be processed to produce  [[Material/Essence of Air|Essence of Air]]
- Can be used in potion making

---

# Distribution
## Trade & Market

Can be found for sale by herbalists or in magic/alchemical supply shops. Occasionally, a potion maker may be persuaded to part with some but it will be with a 20%-50% markup.
