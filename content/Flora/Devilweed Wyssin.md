# Description
A dull-coloreed grass, with sharp awns,

# Natural Range
Grassland

---

# Common Use(s)
## Unaltered Form
Can be rolled and smoked like tobacco. Doing so provides +2 to Strength-based ability checks and -1 to all other ability checks for 1d6 hours

## As an Ingredient
- Can be processed to produce  [[Material/Essence of Fire|Essence of Fire]]
- Can be used in potion making

---

# Distribution
## Trade & Market

Can be found for sale by herbalists or in magic/alchemical supply shops. Occasionally, a potion maker may be persuaded to part with some but it will be with a 20%-50% markup.
