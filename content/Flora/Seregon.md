# Description
Blood-red flowers that bloom directly from their roots in rocky soil

# Natural Range
Mountains

---

# Common Use(s)
## Unaltered Form
Ingesting the flower's nectar grants a creature 2d6 temporary hit points that last for 1 hour. This effect cannot be combined with any other effect that grants temporary hit points.

## As an Ingredient
- Can be processed to produce  [[material/essence-of-air|Essence of Air]]
- Can be used in potion making

---

# Distribution
## Trade & Market

Can be found for sale by herbalists or in magic/alchemical supply shops. Occasionally, a potion maker may be persuaded to part with some but it will be with a 20%-50% markup.
