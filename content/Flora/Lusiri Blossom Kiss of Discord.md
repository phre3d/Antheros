# Description
A herb with dull red leaves that resemble lips.

# Natural Range
Grassland

---

# Common Use(s)
## Unaltered Form
Ingestion causes 1d4 hours of hallucinations and the feeling that you must now go on a spirit quest. You feel pulled toward a far off destination.

## As an Ingredient
- Can be processed to produce  [[Material/Essence of Water|Essence of Water]]
- Can be used in potion making

---

# Distribution
## Trade & Market

Can be found for sale by herbalists or in magic/alchemical supply shops. Occasionally, a potion maker may be persuaded to part with some but it will be with a 20%-50% markup.
