# Description
Cat's Tail is a small plant with long, thin, spear-shaped leaves rising from its base, and a single long and thin flower atop the stalk.

# Natural Range
Coastal

---

# Common Use(s)
## Unaltered Form
When ground into a salve, it will remedy all pain in a person's feet over the course of a short rest.

## As an Ingredient
- Can be processed to produce  [[Material/Essence of Water|Essence of Water]]
- Can be used in potion making

---

# Distribution
## Trade & Market

Can be found for sale by herbalists or in magic/alchemical supply shops. Occasionally, a potion maker may be persuaded to part with some but it will be with a 20%-50% markup.
