# Courtesan

Being a courtesan is not a "lesser" profession and its existence is taken for granted. While sex is part of the role (and some courtesans never grow beyond this), being a higher ranking courtesan is much more and involves the overall enjoyment of one's company.

One might acquire the services of a courtesan for several reasons, a single sex act, an evening of entertainment, an escort for a night out or to take to a party. None of these is frowned upon in per se in Haven; the specifics however may cause people to judge.

Courtesans, both male and female, are performing artists and entertainers trained in various  styles, such as dance, music and singing, as well as being proficient conversationalists and hosts. A skilled courtesan knows how to relate to their customer and a deeper level, knowing when to speak and when to listen. They are part entertainer, part companion, and part confidant/therapist. 

Because of the closeness to a customer that a courtesan may achieve they are sometimes used as spies. The more honourable ones whoever look down upon the mere idea of betraying a confidence.
