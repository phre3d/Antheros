# On Deities and Religion
Antheros is guarded by several gods and goddesses. The general attitude towards religion is that most worshippers don't restrict themselves to a sinlge deity. Rather, anyone who feels the need may make a prayer or sacrifice to whichever deity they feel appropriate though most people have one or two favourites among the pantheon. 

Clerics are the exception, and are restricted to worshipping their own deity (though one may appeal to [[religions/lohi|Lohi]] when healing). In smaller towns where not all deities are represented, this rule is relaxed somewhat and clerics will conduct services to any deity one wants (while keeping their personal devotions to a single deity).

While all the various religions are respected by all races, competition between the religions does exists. For the most part this competition is not violent (the exception being those worshipping the goddess Azriel). Any attack on the pantheon from outside is not to be tolerated, and is  one time that all religions will band together.

Within any given religion there may be several orders of varying stature (e.g. you worship [[religions/ariel|Ariel]], one of the more important goddesses, but are part of a minor order). 

Within the pantheon there are four generations:
- <u>The Elders:</u>  [[religions/serindë|Serindë]], [[religions/ladros|Ladros]] and [[religions/laydi|Laydi]], who have been around the longest
- <u>Firstborn:</u> These are the children of two Elders
- <u>Secondborn:</u> Those where only one parent is an Elder
- <u>Latterborn:</u> Children of Secondborn, or other Latterborn

## Elders 

 **[[religions/serindë|Serindë]]<sup>*</sup>:**  Goddess of Truth, Mother of the Gods<br>
 **[[religions/ladros|Ladros]]<sup>*</sup>:**  God of Knowledge, Father of the Gods<br>
 **[[religions/laydi|Laydi]]:**  Goddess of Luck, Patron of Gamblers<br>

## Firstborn 
 **[[religions/istara|Istara]]:**  Goddess of Magic<br>
 **[[religions/narconal|Narconal]]<sup>*</sup>:**  God of the Earth and Agriculture<br>
 **[[religions/nenya|Nenya]]:**  Goddess of the Sea, Mother of Storms, Patron of Sailors<br>
 **[[religions/maedhros|Maedhros]]:**  God of Fire, Patron of Metalworkers<br>
 **[[religions/ea|Ea]]:**  Goddess of the Air, Patron of Travellers

## Secondborn 
 **[[religions/strathos|Strathos]]<sup>*</sup>:**  God of War, Strength & Courage, Patron of Warriors<br>
 **[[religions/ariel|Ariel]]<sup>*</sup>:**  Goddess of Love & Beauty, Patron of the Arts<br>
 **[[religions/mandos|Mandos]] :**  God of Death<br>
 **[[religions/etheah|Etheah]]:**  Goddess of Nature<br>
 **[[religions/lohi|Lohi]]:**  Goddess of Healing

## Latterborn 
 **[[religions/azriel|Azriel]]:**  Goddess of Disease & Suffering, Mistress of Pain<br>
 **[[religions/vairë|Vairë]]:**  Goddess of the Underworld<br>
 **[[religions/thalos|Thalos]]:**  God of the Night, Patron of Thieves, Lord of Mischief

 **<sup>*</sup>**  denotes a major religion.

---

**Usually only clerics are taught the following information. It's not a secret, but also not generally known by the common folk.**

Like many other pantheons, the genealogy is a bit, shall we say, complicated. [[religions/serindë|Serindë]] and [[religions/ladros|Ladros]] are considered Mother and Father of the Gods since most of the rest a descended from them. An interesting side note is that [[religions/ariel|Ariel]]'s full parentage is not clear. Some say that [[religions/serindë|Serindë]] brought [[religions/ariel|Ariel]] into the world alone since love and beauty are unknowable thus [[religions/ladros|Ladros]] can't be the father.

Ladi is somewhat of a special case since nobody is sure where she came from and may be from "elsewhere". Others say that [[religions/serindë|Serindë]] and [[religions/ladros|Ladros]] were created by [[religions/laydi|Laydi]].
