# Celestial Objects

## Hasp

Hasp, the larger of Antheros' two moons has a pale yellow colour and a 24-day cycle.

## Celene

Celene, the small moon has a light bluish tinge and a 32-day cycle.

## Ebla & Tabit

Ebla and Tabit appear as a bright star in the sky that don't twinkle. they are in fact other planets circling Antheros' sun but that distinction isn't fully understood.
