# Description
Most practitioners of magic in Haven are members of this guild, though it is not as strict on membership as the [[Organization/Guilds/Guild of Entertainers|Guild of Entertainers]]. The guild also is reponsible for training through the [[landmark/College of the Mystic Arts|College of the Mystic Arts]], which is part of the [[organization/University of Haven|University of Haven]], despite being located away from the main univerity grounds ([[settlement/Scholar's Triangle|Scholar's Triangle]]).

The guild is led by members of the [[Organization/Guilds/Circle Chamber|Circle Chamber]], whose master is [[person/Karilinia Inturisdan|Karilinia Inturisdan]].

Both the guild meetings and [[Organization/Guilds/Circle Chamber|Circle Chamber]] meetings are held at the [[landmark/Guildhouse of Magic|Guildhouse of Magic]] on [[settlement/Magic Street|Magic Street]].

Note that while alchemy and potions might be considered magic, these practitions are part of the Guild of Apothecaries, Alchemists and Potioners.
