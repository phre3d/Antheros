# Conindir Family

The Conindir are relatively new to Haven, having resided in the city for only 40 years. The family numbers seven, the patriarch (an outstanding architect), [[Person/Teleril Conindir|Teleril]], his wife [[Person/Khelekien Conindir|Khelekien Conindir]],  two sons [[Person/Mirthule Conindir|Mirthule Conindir]] and [[Person/Celeblinde Conindir|Celeblinde Conindir]], and three young daughters, [[Person/Tehtaavilya Conindir|Tehtaavilya Conindir]], [[Person/Tariluine Conindir|Tariluine Conindir]], and [[Person/Finsilivren Conindir|Finsilivren Conindir]]. Other than [[Person/Teleril Conindir|Teleril]], the family is without exceptional note.

>[!Private]
>The family isn't very powerful in either the political or business structures of the city, since elves do not usually concern themselves with the idiosyncrasies of other races, but they do play a great role in some of the city's major institutions for recreation, construction, and music. They are legitimate equals of the non-elven higher classes, but, beyond their residences in [[settlement/Erinhir|Erinhir]], they are not considered as nobles in the classical sense. Overall, elves in general and those of [[settlement/Erinhir|Erinhir]] in particular, are respected more than many of the nobles of the city so they do not force issues with them, after all, what is the point?.
