# Hofstra Family

Living in [[Settlement/The Glens|The Glens]] neighbourhood in the [[Settlement/The North Corridor|The North Corridor]], the Hofstra clan consists of the patriarch, [[Person/Jaime Hofstra|Jaime]], his four sons, [[Person/Abel Hofstra|Abel]], [[Person/Bakka Hofstra|Bakka]], [[Person/Charlie Hofstra|Charlie]], and [[Person/Dauge Hofstra|Dauge]], and their wives (who are of little consequence). Jaime's only daughter, [[person/Eezie Rand|Eezie Rand]], is married to the youngest [[Organization/Families/Rand Family|Rand]] brother, [[Person/Zalle Rand|Zalle Rand]], but still keeps in close contact with her family. 

The two older sons assist [[Person/Jaime Hofstra|Jaime]] in running the bank, determining investments, foreclosing on overdue loans, etc. [[Person/Charlie Hofstra|Charlie]], and [[Person/Dauge Hofstra|Dauge]] run the courier service which connects the main branch in Haven with the branches in the rest of the [[Organization/Confederation of the Ten Cities|Confederation of the Ten Cities]] - they are presently engaged in trying to set up a coach and mail line to compete with the successful venture run by Gillaume Fargot and Andre Welshe. 

>[!Private]
>The family fortune makes the Hofstra one of the five richest families in Haven but show few pretension of power.
