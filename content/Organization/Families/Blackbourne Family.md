# Blackbourne Family

The Blackbourne family first made its fortune through the importation of fine fabrics but have since expanded their sights, and are involved in all aspects of the clothing trade (dyeing, tailoring, and even retail sales). The Blackbournes own several fine clothing shops, and three large sailing vessels. 

>[!Private]
>The rapid growth of the Blackbourne business interests is due to foresight of its current patriarch, Hamlin, an incredibly vigorous man of sixty who has built up an extensive trade network that brings him the latest fashion concepts from abroad, and has the good sense to commission the best clothiers in the city to produce these innovative new designs. Hamlin's election as master of the [[Organization/Guilds/Guild of the Loom|Guild of the Loom]] was virtually unanimous, and this Guild is not characterized by the petty internal politics that dominate the activities of some of the other professional Brotherhoods. 

Hamlin was recently widowed, and has three adult sons: [[Person/Anson Blackbourne|Anson]], [[Person/Marcus Blackbourne|Marcus]], and [[Person/Gladiolus Blackbourne|Gladiolus]]. Only Anson is married, to [[Person/Talia Blackbourne|Talia Corio]], whose Uncle is master of Haven's [[Organization/Guilds/Guild of Metalsmiths|Guild of Metalsmiths]]; they have one young daughter, Amathea.

The family's home in Haven is located in The [[Settlement/The Heights|The Heights]]
