# The Jackals

- A gang of 5 grave robbers
- Jackals always work(ed) together
- They usually work outside the city but occasionally try to break into mausoleums in [[landmark/City of the Dead|City of the Dead]]
- At the start of [[Plot/Sins of the Past/Sins of the Past|Sins of the Past]] 3 are dead (Tornus Icebringer, Indra Glide, Kin Simorsk) and 2 remain, trapped in the mirror ([[person/Rani Dathret|Rani Dathret]], [[person/Haral Sask|Haral Sask]])
