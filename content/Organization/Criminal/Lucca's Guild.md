# Lucca's Guild

[[person/Paraggio Lucca|Paraggio Lucca]], the master of one of [[Organization/Criminal/The Black Hand|The Black Hand's]] two traditional thieving guilds is a slender, boyish man with dark hair and an angular face. His guild consists of some three cells of highly competent thieves who operate mostly in [[Settlement/The Commons|The Commons]] and North Corridor.
