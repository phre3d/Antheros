# General Info
Stella, a tall, rather rounded blonde who wears her hair in braids adorned by thongs of rich cowhide, is an outdoorswoman who shares her husband's (Tenley Tainley) interest. She herself is quite a competent swordswoman, and during the feasting which follows the hunt, she may impulsively challenge a male newcomer to their circle to a mock combat (a battle not to the death, using weapons that have been blunted or padded to reduce their damage potential). Such events are often accompanied by wagering among the crowd, and the results (when Stella is victorious) are usually known all over [[settlement/The Heights|The Heights]] within hours (woe to the chauvinist loser with a thin skin). Stella wears no jewelry except a gold chain that matches the one worn by Tenley.
