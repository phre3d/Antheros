# General Info
er-Keng loved both Branden and Valken at different times during her youth, and the relationship between the three after her marriage to Brandon has always seemed ambiguous to those outside the immediate family (and served as the source of many a juicy rumor over the years). 

 er-Keng has tried to subtly inform the younger brothers of their elder's discontentment, but has so far been unsuccessful (an unsurprising result, given that Heidel and Kronen seldom notice anything more subtle than a 2 by 4, "upside the head”).
