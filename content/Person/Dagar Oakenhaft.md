# General Info
Although rather slight for a smith this red-bearded dwarf is no less powerful for his lack of girth (his secret is tremendous upper arm strength). He works at a forge mounted on a large, rectangular wooden platform, under a partially open wood canopy. 

Dagar is newlywed, and totally and completely infatuated with his new bride, Merian (found for him by Meydal).  He may be convinced to trade his services for a traveler's small trinket if he thinks it will brighten Merian's eye.
