# General Info
General alley basher/legbreakers/other muscle.

 Uses slow-wittedness as a ruse, since anyone so obviously brawny is rarely expected to be bright. Merrick is actually a Black Hand informant, very cagey and observant, who seldom makes a slip out of his dull-thinking character.
