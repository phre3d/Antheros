# General Info
A renegade healer, Mauray got into trouble with the Haven authorities because of his interest in communicable diseases. After an outbreak of typhus in [[settlement/The North Corridor|The North Corridor]], Mauray changed his name and moved to [[settlement/The Commons|The Commons]]. He is an accomplished surgeon, and, in exchange for protection from his neighbors (he has continued his research in the Commons), he performs facial operations on Black Hand operatives, permanently altering their features.
