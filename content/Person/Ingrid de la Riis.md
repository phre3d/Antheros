# General Info
Ingrid, [[person/Bruning de la Riis|Bruning de la Riis]]' daughter from his first marriage, is a well-proportioned young lady with auburn hair and green eyes, but her facial appearance is marred by poorly-spaced teeth.  She has not taken to her new step-mother, [[person/K'maill de la Riis|K'maill de la Riis]], and along with aunt Rutmilla de la Riis has been spreading rumours that K'maill has evil magic powers.
