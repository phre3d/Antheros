# General Info
Dog-end (Jethro Tull reference) is an old man who lives on the streets. He can be seen either wandering lonely taking time the only way he knows or sitting near parks or other places where children play. He has long, grey, filthy hair, snot running down his nose and greasy fingers smearing shabby clothes. Essentially harmless, Dog-end is just one of those people who just fell through the cracks of society and gets by somehow. For the most part people ignore him and this constant muttering but from time to time his appearance causes alarm in the more upstanding Haven residents. Normally found in [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]], he will from time to time be found wandering the streets and parks in [[settlement/The South Corridor|The South Corridor]].  Around meal times he if often found at one of the Crotofiran Missions
