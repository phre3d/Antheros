# General Info
Millar is a chubby, extremely red-faced dwarf who samples his wares perhaps a bit more than would be politic. Although [[person/Bethli|Bethli]]'s iron and copper smelter lies downstream from the brewery, many who have sampled Starkerb beer swear that Millar must somehow get his water directly from [[person/Bethli|Bethli]]'s drain pipe.
