# General Info
This young maiden is truly a breathtaking beauty, with chestnut brown hair flowing 'round her slender, sinuous frame, full pouting lips and seductive almond eyes that few can resist. She is not bright by intellectual standards, but is very aware of her beauty and is wily about using it to get what she wants. Of course, as might be expected of a girl of 18, what she wants changes from day to day for she becomes bored with a particular pastime or fashion style very quickly. 

Sui was brought to Haven three years ago by her now husband [[person/Neberhard Ohlfaardt|Neberhard Ohlfaardt]] who met her on one of his jouneys, in this case to Herudana.

She has not yet tried to cuckold old Neberhard, a fact which has greatly surprised several of Haven's young dandies.  This seems due more to her awareness of the four bully boys ([[person/Skiff Borden|Skiff Borden]], [[person/Wilverado|Wilverado]], [[person/Cambol Tartyn|Cambol Tartyn]], [[person/"Brick"|"Brick"]]) that Neberhard has hired to protect her than to any great affection for her spouse. 

It is likely that a man of discretion and taste would probably find Sui too shallow to satisfy him in the long run (but, oh, lord, what a surface she has to explore until then!).
