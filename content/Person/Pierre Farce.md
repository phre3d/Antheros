# General Info
Pierre is a man of very small stature, elaborately perfumed and always dressed in the latest styles. He speaks with a pronounced foreign accent. Though the men of [[settlement/The Heights|The Heights]] often snicker derisively at the mention of his name, Pierre has "had his way" with a fair portion of his exclusive clientele, and knows many highly sensitive personal secrets.
