# General Info
Gladshaw is egotistical and conceited and has never hired a helper due to this very fact.  Due to his very "noble" manner he will not deal with characters that do not show him proper respect. 

Gladshaw dresses in a dandified fashion when appearing socially, and often wears a gaudy cloak made of hundreds of different colored bird feathers and plumes.
