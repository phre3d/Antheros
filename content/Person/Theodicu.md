# General Info
The Bhas of the local temple of Denarius, is renowned in the city as an excellent trader (the priests of Denarius are expected to engage in trade and support themselves; their position in the afterlife is just as dependent on the money they have earned as is that of a member of the laity). He has accumulated a considerable fortune and since he has no children, that fortune will pass to the temple on his demise. 

 Theodicu is a short, fat, and very jolly man, with only a fringe of hair about his ears and sparkling brown eyes with many laugh wrinkles about them. He enjoys bargaining, and has a sharp eye for value.
