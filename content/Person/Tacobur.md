# General Info
Unquestionably the best dwarven metalworker in Haven, and arguably the peer of Salvatore Cellini, the upstart Master of the Artisan's Guild. Unlike his discontented colleagues of that Guild, however, Tacobur is unconcerned about the political struggle for Guild leadership, and largely minds his own affairs. He is an incredible perfectionist, who often consigns finished works that other artisans would give their eyeteeth to produce to the scrap pile
