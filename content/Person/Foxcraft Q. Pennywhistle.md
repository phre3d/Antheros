# General Info
General alley basher/legbreakers/other muscle.

 Sometimes, alley bashers are made, not born. Foxy's mom wanted him to be something dignified, like an artisan or barrister - she had no idea that her son would grow up to look like the entire Green Bay Packer front four. The taunts of his childhood, and the fights that followed hardened him into a sullen man. Foxcraft never trusts anybody, and seemingly small disagreements will set him off with a vengeance. If you like working with a time bomb, here's your boy.
