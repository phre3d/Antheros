# General Info
Malikor the Eye, so called for the patch he wears over his right eye, runs a pawnshop and fence out of the lower floor of this modest two-story wooden house. He specializes in precious metals, gems and jewelry, but will not look askance at almost anything of value, even if it is quite obviously "hot". Malikor is a trained in Jeweler, Glassblower, and Silversmith, and knows a fake gem 80% of the time.
