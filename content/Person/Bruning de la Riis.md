# General Info
A stocky man with dirty blond hair, and a robust appearance that belies his age. Well-known in Haven in his youth for his several victories in city jousting tournaments, he has spent the last twenty years as a knight in the military service of the late Viscount of Kveklan. With the coronation of the Viscount's son, Bruning was dismissed; the old warhorse is now chafing at the bit from inactivity and would be receptive to joining an expedition if it were satisfactorily funded and led by a fellow noble. 

Bruning returned to the city after an absence of many years with a new bride, a commoner woman almost half his age named K'maill.

He can be contacted through the mercenaries guild in Haven, the Sworn Swords (OE) in [[settlement/The South Corridor|The South Corridor]].
