# General Info
An older blood bay centaur, about 16 hands, with dark hair, slightly greying at the temples. He is an expert horse trainer, as are most of the centaurs who work in the Market, but he seems to have a special touch for accommodating war horses and their new masters. He works for [[person/Numirolakos|Numirolakos]] (VI-S092), but spends much of his free time at the auction, looking for prime stock to train as chargers.
