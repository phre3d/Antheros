# General Info
North Bank's town drunk; if he has not been encountered elsewhere, passing any tavern gives a 35% chance he will come either reeling out (and probably into you) or flying out (propelled by the boot of the innkeeper planted firmly in his fundament). He will pick himself up (he falls frequently, even when not helped), dust himself off, and, mustering what dignity he can, stagger off in the direction of the next nearest tavern; for Targoon, the shortest distance between two points is never a straight line. His appearance is usually mussed and slightly liquid, and the feature most remarked on is the enormous red nose in the middle of his face; if it is noticed, he has black hair (thinning) and dark eyes.
