Half-elf, an orphan from [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]] originally, he used to steal to make a living. He survived after [[person/Nesan Geronde's|Nesan Geronde's]] attack in [[plot/Sins of the Past|Sins of the Past]] but got trapped in the mirror - whether he gets out depends on the party's actions.
