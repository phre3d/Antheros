# General Info
Daughter of Otto Chub, Bundy has gotten herself in the "family way", and is desperately searching for a male halfling willing to be her husband. To that end she hangs around outside her parents' butcher shop checking out male halflings that wander by. 

 She is quite comely of face and form (though looking at her parents, one could justifiably have doubts about how long her svelteness will last); and would make a good homemaker.
