# General Info
The daughter of a baronet of Seki, she is much younger than the two male wards in [[person/Tenley Tainley|Tenley Tainley]]'s household. Her figure is still quite boyish, though she has gorgeous long flowing hair the color of wheat and green eyes that belie her immaturity. Taramilla has become hopelessly infatuated with [[person/Antonio Berensten|Antonio Berensten]], who is completely oblivious to her attentions. She spends virtually all her time daydreaming about possible ways to attract his interest; consequently, she often receives verbal abuse from her instructors for being inattentive and unprepared. 

Taramilla is aware of Kaspar Bundar's attentions but views him only as a friend.
