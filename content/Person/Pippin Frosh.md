# General Info
Round as a dumpling, with rosy cheeks and bright eyes, and perpetually wearing a floured apron. Pippin sells hot pies and pastries from his pushcart on [[settlement/Trade Island|Trade Island]] (where the prices are higher) baked fresh every night in [[settlement/The Dell|The Dell]]. The cart has an enchanted [[person/Brick|Brick]] in it to keep the goodies just-out-of-the-oven hot, 

 Price Range: High 

 Quality: Good
