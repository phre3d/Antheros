# General Info
Lady Penelope Sutherland (but call her "Lady" and you might get punched) is the youngest daughter from a minor noble family, from a far-off land. After travelling for a year or so, she found herself in Haven, and after a few odd jobs joined the guard in order to pay rent.

Penelope is quick to laughter and quick to anger - loyal to her friends, but will hold a grudge forever.
