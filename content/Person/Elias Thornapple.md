# General Info
Also known as "Roundbottom", he is a middle-aged halfling of considerable girth; a jester by trade. Makes his living by playing the country simpleton; he is sharper of wit than ha appears, and acts as an agent for the [[organization/Thieves' Guild|Thieves' Guild]]. 

 A good-natured fellow who loves a good pipe and a good joke (Shaggy dog variety). Has not been a practicing thief for some time (he doesn't fit into windows very well any more). He knows Haven very well, and would tag along for an "adventure", playing the bumpkin until he can take the loot and disappear, unless one of the party is also in the [[organization/Thieves' Guild|Thieves' Guild]]. 

 Most folk consider Elias a silly old halfling, and he likes it that way. Well known and respected by the elder generation on [[settlement/The Street of Harps|The Street of Harps]] for his talent and drive, but the younger bards believe he is too subservient to the whims of his patron, Duke Fernando di Cottilion.
