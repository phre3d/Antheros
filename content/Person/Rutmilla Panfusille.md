# General Info
Rutmilla, [[person/Tybalt de la Riis|Tybalt de la Riis]]' daughter, is big-boned and very homely, with a broad flat nose and moles on her chin. She is married to her brother [[person/Morgan de la Riis|Morgan de la Riis]]' best friend [[person/Clemente Panfusille|Clemente Panfusille]]. She has not taken to her new sister-in-law [[person/K'maill de la Riis|K'maill de la Riis]], and along with niece [[person/Ingrid de la Riis|Ingrid de la Riis]] has been spreading rumours that K'maill has evil magic powers.
