# General Info
Meriwether, a small man hunched from years of leaning over his work, usually has time to talk unless his mouth is full of tacks. He does know a good deal of gossip about the area, and the comings and goings of its residents, and can be an invaluable source of information (both the [[organization/Thieves' Guild|Thieves' Guild]] and [[organization/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]] use him for news of the North Corridor).
