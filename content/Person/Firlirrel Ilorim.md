# General Info
Firlirrel, who is presently accounted as one of the five finest voices in Haven, arrived only some 120 years ago. After making a great impression on the Entertainers' Guild with his superb voice (as well as on the elves), he wooed, won, and married Lachoriel, a beautiful elfin maid of Londhin, a minor family in [[settlement/Erinhir|Erinhir]], and produced a daughter, Adrielle. 

 Firlirrel is still a power in the various ramifications of [[settlement/The Street of Harps|The Street of Harps]], but seldom performs now, other than on special occasions. He is engaged in writing a history of the elvish peoples, and devotes most of his time to that project.
