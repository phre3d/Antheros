# General Info
A guest at the [[landmark/Rose Garden Inn|Rose Garden Inn]], Reliddack is a jovial, older gnome with an engaging personality and sharp wit. His congenial nature and small stature make him well-liked, especially amongst children. 

Reliddack is known for growing thriving medicinal herbs, such as can cure even diseases heretofore believed incurable. Additionally, he has found cures for other, more aesthetic, afflictions, such as the appearance of old age. His access to such properties has gained him significant ground in the highest of social circles.
