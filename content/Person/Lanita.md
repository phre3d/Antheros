# General Info
Lanita, a priestess of [[person/Ariel|Ariel]], is a warm and smiling woman in her thirties. Her skin tone suggests she comes from the south, perhaps the Endless Dunes. Sold as a slave in her youth, she grew up in a noble's house. There, she encountered a priestess of Ariel who noticed Lanita's ever-bright nature. Recognizing her potential, the priestess took Lanita to the temple, where she devoted herself to spreading joy and love to everyone she meets.   

Standing at around 5 feet 5 inches tall, Lanita has darkish skin and long black hair with streaks of grey. She willingly heals the party for free if they ask, using her powers as a priestess.
