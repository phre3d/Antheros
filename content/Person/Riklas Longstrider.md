# General Info
More of an investigative specialist than a trial lawyer; he is generally employed by the other lawyers of [[settlement/Barristers' Maze|Barristers' Maze]] to check out information and witnesses for their various cases. Few judges wish to allow him in their [[landmark/Courts|Courts]], but if he does have a trial before them, they bear him no malice. He is of normal centaur build, with reddish hair and blue eyes. He lives outside the city walls from the Horse Gate, and, like [[person/Meron Glyptes|Meron Glyptes]], is not allowed in either the Hanged Wan or [[landmark/The Lying Lawyer|The Lying Lawyer]].
