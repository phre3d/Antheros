# General Info
Bob and his brother, [[person/Bing|Bing]], seem to act as two halves of one personality. He is ruddy-faced, slightly rotund and losing his hair. 

 Bob tells jokes and funny stories, and has a quick wit, and an incredible arsenal of bad puns. He is also known for his interest in the opposite sex, but [[person/Bing|Bing]] always seems to get the girl. 

 He and [[person/Bing|Bing]] stick together, and frequently one finishes a sentence the other had started, and they occasionally answer in unison. 

 Bob uses a sling with proficiency but will fight only to save his or [[person/Bing|Bing]]'s life; content to take cover under a wagon at any hint of trouble. 

 The brothers are also the cooks for the troupe.
