# General Info
A tall, heavy set horse trainer with a limp, who works for Hrubilasz (????????). One of his duties is to scout the horse pens each morning to check on likely stock for training; he has noticed the odd palomino belonging to Liriki, and is wondering why anyone would try to sell such a miserable piece of horseflesh at [[landmark/The Auction Block|The Auction Block]]. If engaged in conversation, he is friendly and talkative, and there is a 35% chance he will make a reference to the "awkward looking stallion with the weird blue eyes".
