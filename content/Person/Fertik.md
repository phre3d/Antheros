# General Info
Fertik has worked with [[person/Valli|Valli]] for 22 years, and is very loyal to his employer. Knowing the seriousness of [[person/Valli|Valli]]'s plight, Fertik has thought about the situation and concluded that the only way to save [[person/Valli|Valli]]'s shop would be to kidnap [[person/Konig|Konig]] and somehow convince him of the error of his ways. Fertik has talked to the Thieves Guild about this, but they categorically refused to take on such a job and he is now discreetly looking for an independent band of rogues that might be willing to perform the deed.
