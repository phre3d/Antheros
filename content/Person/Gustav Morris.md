# General Info
A young energetic composer working under the patronage of the [[organization/Maggia Family|Maggia Family]]. Tall and thin, with curly blond hair Gustav works odd hours, plunking on a harpsichord far into the night. Gustav has a well-deserved reputation for eccentricity, both in his person and his music.
