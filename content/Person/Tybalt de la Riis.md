# General Info
The Count's hair is now snow-white, but he is still hale and keen-eyed. Tybalt is rarely (10%) present at the city-estate, for he only comes to town when his presence is required at ceremonial events. He has a dour, foul temperament and judges everyone in his family harshly except son [[person/Paul de la Riis|Paul de la Riis]], who is the apple of his eye and can do no wrong. 

Tybalt loves to hunt in the woods surrounding their country manor, and is still a good marksman with a short bow.
