# General Info
A beautiful girl of 18, with wide, violet eyes, fair skin, and waist-length hair of jet black, with a sweet soprano voice. Clairone always plays the heroine in the troupe's various plays, a part she is admirably suited for, due to her looks and the fact that to play the princess-in-distress is not a difficult role. 

 Clairone's chief joy is beautiful clothes, and will spend a great deal of time on trying on costumes and putting on cosmetics. She is a bit of an airhead at best, and only the loving supervision of her father keeps her from doing incredibly foolish things. She, alone of all the company, believes Toric's stories, and will actually be upset when he is kicked out of the troupe but this will only last until the next morning, when she will have forgotten his name.
