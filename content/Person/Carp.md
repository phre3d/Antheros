# General Info
A small, brown fellow, who hawks his odiferous wares in a surprisingly powerful basso profundo, wheeling his little pushcart throughout the dock area. There is little that escapes his notice as he patrols his turf, and he is perfectly willing to pass on interesting information for a little cash. 

 Although he claims to be loyal to none save himself, there beats beneath his smelly exterior a heart of purest mush, and he has been known to surreptitiously aid others who were down on their luck.
