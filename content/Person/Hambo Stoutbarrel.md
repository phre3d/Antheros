# General Info
A middle-aged halfling of tremendous girth, who is always dressed in a tent-like brown tunic and a grease-stained leather apron; he dislikes working hard, and gets angry when patrons become impatient for service. Hambo is extremely chauvinistic (one may note the absence of female employees in this establishment), and an accomplished spinner of tall tales; he knows a smattering of lore (just enough to be dangerous) on about a thousand different subjects.
