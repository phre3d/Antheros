# General Info
Bardol retired from the practice of magic about ten years ago but keeps his hand in by entertaining his customers in the evenings with small displays of prestidigitation and minor illusions. The [[landmark/Mystic Valley Inn|Mystic Valley Inn]] has been part of the Haven cityscape for nearly two centuries, and Bardol inherited it from his father. He has no children of his own, and plans to will the establishment to the Mages Guild with the proviso that they keep it open to the public. He is tall and round (he likes good food and drink), with a wreath of white hair which flies wildly in all directions.
