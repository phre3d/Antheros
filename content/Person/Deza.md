# General Info
An 18-year old half-orc girl who has been taken in by Master [[person/Nimoyin|Nimoyin]]. She has been a source of consternation among the elves of [[settlement/The Cauldron|The Cauldron]] and despite her obviously talented mind, she is less than welcome even at the multi-racial University. There are even dark rumors (false) among the students that her position with Master [[person/Nimoyin|Nimoyin]] has been obtained through intimate favors.
