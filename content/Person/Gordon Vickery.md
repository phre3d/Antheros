# General Info
A good average lawyer; he wins the share of cases his ability entitles him to, and draws neither bonuses nor penalties from any of the judges. His reputation comes from winning a very messy murder case in which his client (actually innocent) was totally implicated by circumstantial evidence. Vickery is tall, stocky, with brown hair and blue eyes. He is frequently (90%) found at [[landmark/The Lying Lawyer|The Lying Lawyer]]; he might be a better lawyer, but he has an incredible taste for alcoholic beverages, and is not uncommonly poured into a bed in one of rooms upstairs at the inn at the close of business.
