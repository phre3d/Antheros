# General Info
Under the direction of Alphonso Caradello, Maylino oversees the buying and storage of illicit cargos for the small fleet of Black Hand smugglers that operates along the river. Unknown to Maylino, cargos smuggled into Haven are stored in Caradello's warehouse, or at Sym's Storage.
