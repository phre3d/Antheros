# General Info
Dingo is a traveler and scholar of the Tabled cultures far to the east of the city, who makes his living by dealing in spices and rare volumes that he brings back from his travels. [[landmark/Bucklander's Import Emporium|Bucklander's Import Emporium]] is a major customer for these products. 

He is very lean for a halfling, and tends to dress in exotic, bright-colored silken robes. Dingo is extremely pacifistic in nature, and will not take life even in self-defense; he is protected on his travels by - 1) a silver bracelet that contains a permanent 12 point Missile Repulsor spell, and
- 2) his training in judo (in close combat, he has a 25% + (5 x (18 - opponent's CO rating)) chance of throwing any human or smaller sized foe in his grasp to the ground - if successful, the opponent must make a CON saving roll or be stunned for 1d6 melee round).
 

Dingo is only in the city about 25% of the time. When he is gone, his housekeeper Sobelia lives in and maintains his home in [[settlement/The Dell|The Dell]] which is a cornucopia filled with artifacts, books and maps from his wanderings. The biggest thing protecting his smial from robbers is the fact that most of his neighbors consider his collection to be junk.
