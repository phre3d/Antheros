# Description
Kethend is a 23-year-old female Dragonborn armorer. - She has a bald head and red eyes. - She has smooth, ghostly, white scales. - She stands 182cm (5'11\") tall and has a fat build. - She has a diamond-shaped, bland face. - She is albino.   **Personality**  - She discretely claims to worship [[person/Thalos|Thalos]]: God of the Night, Patron of Thieves, Lord of Mischief - In truth, she secretly worships [[person/Ladros|Ladros]]: God of Knowledge, Father of the Gods.  - She is incredibly conceited.  - She works hard to play hard afterward.
