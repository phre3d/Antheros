# General Info
A thin lad of twenty with dirty-blond hair and a perpetual sneer, Gunter eats, sleeps and breathes the lifestyle of the [[organization/Young Stallions|Young Stallions]]. The group is his source of identity and without it, Gunter would probably be a faceless recruit in [[landmark/The Citadel|The Citadel]] Guard. He always wears [[landmark/The Green|The Green]] swordsman's cloak, white brooch, and black boots that constitute the Stallion's uniform, and seldom travels outside the presence of one or more of his compatriots. 

 A decent but not overpowering fighter, Gunter is nonetheless unafraid to take on very unpromising odds, if he believes that fighting will further the nobility's cause. Gunter, while not stupid, did not inherit his parents' intellectual prowess; he was an extremely lackadaisical student, and consequently cannot read or write very well (a fact he desperately tries to hide from the others). 

He would like to be [[person/Paul de la Riis|Paul de la Riis]]' or [[person/Antonio Berensten|Antonio Berensten]]'s best friend, and is somewhat hurt by the unspoken closeness between the two that he cannot share.
