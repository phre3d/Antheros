# General Info
The proprietor of [[landmark/The Crystal Ship|The Crystal Ship]] is an immense, ham-fisted fellow known as Tiny who is capable of keeping order even among seamen. Although he is not a formal member of [[organization/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]], he is connected with them through the opium trade, and is not foolish enough to risk everything in any plot against them.
