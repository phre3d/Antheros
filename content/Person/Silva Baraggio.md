# General Info
The best known caravan master in Haven, Silva is a husky and robust man of middle years with a booming voice and a gruff but usually pleasant demeanor. It is well known that Baraggio has an eye for the ladies and that he seldom travels alone, though he professes love only for one woman, a songstress from [[settlement/The Street of Harps|The Street of Harps]], [[person/Lisa Duncan|Lisa Duncan]], with whom he has carried on a very passionate affair for almost five years; when in Haven he stays at her home. 

Most of the caravans Baraggio organizes these days are small to medium and wagon drawn groups, running fairly short routes normally (2-3 months round trip, the better to tend his many business interests in Haven). 

If a young woman of 15+ APP applies as a passenger for a caravan trip, there is a 65% chance Baraggio will suggest a liaison with her if she is unaccompanied by a husband or lover. Naturally, passage on the caravan will not be charged if she accepts.
