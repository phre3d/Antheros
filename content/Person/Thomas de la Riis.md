# General Info
Thomas is the junior of [[person/Paul de la Riis|Paul de la Riis]] by three years with sandy hair, brown eyes and his father's broad features. He is tall and gangly, and somewhat uncoordinated and all his clothes (a carbon copy of his brother) seem to fit him awkwardly. Thomas idolizes his older brother, who in turn barely tolerates him. The [[organization/Young Stallions|Young Stallions]] make him the frequent butt of practical jokes, but this does not reduce his ardor to be a member of their group. Thomas is very anxious to figure out some noble deed that will gain him favor. 

 He knows how to read and write several languages. Excessive physical exertion will frequently drive him to an asthmatic coughing spell.
