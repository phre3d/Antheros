# General Info
A small, bookish individual, with thin graying hair and a wispy beard. He has practiced law in Haven for over thirty years, and is fairly tight with the current set of judges. He is friends with Cardallo, and will receive a +15% preference from him; neither of the two Judges of the Ethical Way like him and he receives -5% from each; with the others, he is neutral. He is nearing his 40th wedding anniversary, and is definitely a homebody, seldom (3%) visiting either of the legal hangouts.
