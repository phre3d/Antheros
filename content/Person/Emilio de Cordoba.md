# General Info
Captain Emilio di Cordoba of the Pavanne is an enterprising fellow who never overlooks a possibly profitable deal, despite its legality. He is rumored to have been a smuggler and privateer in former days, and will do almost anything to ensure his own interest in an affair. He is also one of the few captains that keeps a ship's mage (Cimeron) though he doesn't really trust him.
