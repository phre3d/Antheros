# General Info
Fagin, a wiry old half-elf with wild eyes, has set up a thriving business in child labor, capturing "street rats" in the twisting alleyways and dark corners of [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]]. The ragged children, along with the occasional careless tiefling, are starved and beaten into submission, and sold for slave (indenture) labor in the workhouses of [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]] or [[settlement/The Commons|The Commons]]
