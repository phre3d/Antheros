# General Info
Dagnir is the only non-human judge in the Haven [[landmark/Courts|Courts]]; he is a handsome elf, of middle years (only 193 last birthday), with flowing brown hair and brown eyes, average in height and weight. As can be seen from his decision tables, he favors elves in any court action, and is fairly strict with non-elves. He hates Cardallo, and would eliminate him from the [[landmark/Courts|Courts]] with little provocation, and from the city, and from the world.
