# General Info
Mayor Gingar Terion is a figure of solid, reassuring presence in [[settlement/Oakridge|Oakridge]], representing the village's traditional values and its residents' trust. He is a man of average height and build, with a behaviour that oscillates between approachable warmth and stern authority, depending on the moment's needs. 

His hair, peppered with grey, and the lines etched into his face speak of years dedicated to the service of his community.   Gingar's attire is modest. His eyes, thoughtful and observant, reflect a mind constantly at work, considering the best paths forward for [[settlement/Oakridge|Oakridge]]. A genuine smile often plays at the corners of his mouth, revealing his kind-hearted nature, though it can quickly turn into a firm line when the situation demands seriousness.   

Despite the pressures of his role, he remains deeply committed to his wife, Theresia, and the village's wellbeing, often finding himself caught between his duties to [[settlement/Oakridge|Oakridge]] and his loyalty to his ambitious spouse.
