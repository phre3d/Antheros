# General Info
Bartholomew's wife is a plump and matronly woman, is a lover of music and theater, having grown up on [[settlement/The Street of Harps|The Street of Harps]]. She is the daughter of [[person/Artemis Guisto|Artemis Guisto]], the Drum maker and has five children - Branden, Ophelia, Andrio, Carmen, and Petrio. 

 She is involved more in running the inn than in direct contact with the guests. She spends a good deal of her time in the kitchen gossiping with [[person/Brisla Stoneaxe|Brisla Stoneaxe]] the cook and knows everyone on [[settlement/The Street of Harps|The Street of Harps]].
