# General Info
[[person/Silverthorne|Silverthorne]] has a small band of loyal followers, who have sworn to defend him and his interests, and to help him regain his throne. Their chief is Mistwood, an experienced warrior. All are staying at [[landmark/The Mallorn|The Mallorn]], and may be encountered at various places throughout the city. Mistwood has been getting very close to [[person/Melaina|Melaina]], one of the staff at [[landmark/The Mallorn|The Mallorn]].
