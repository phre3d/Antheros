# General Info
A tall, dashing gentleman with long, flowing dark hair and a diamond earring in his left ear, his tastes in clothing run toward rich tunics of silk and velvet, adorned with elaborate lace cuffs and collars. 

Swayze is married to [[person/Gabrielle de Kammeron|Gabrielle de Kammeron]], the beautiful daughter of the perfumer, Broutier. The two share a love for the outdoor life, and have journeyed together to many exotic climes. 

Swayze is an accomplished fencer, and has been forced to demonstrate his skill twice previously in duels. It is said that even the fiery Stallion [[person/Paul de la Riis|Paul de la Riis]] respects Swayze's prowess with the blade, and thus dares not challenge either Swayze or bother [[person/Boccaccio de Kammeron|Boccaccio de Kammeron]], the man he accuses of "selling his birthright as if he were some cheap whore on [[settlement/The Street of Silk Veils|The Street of Silk Veils]]." 

Swayze always wears 2-3 items of expensive jewelry.
