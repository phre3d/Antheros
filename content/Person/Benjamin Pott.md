# General Info
Despite being quite meek, Benjamin dreams of being a great mage or warrior as he tends the Museum, and eagerly listens to tales told on [[landmark/Dancing Green|Dancing Green]] or in the pubs. Unfortunately, Benjamin is nearsighted, clumsy as a puppy, and afraid of his own shadow - poor material for a hero.
