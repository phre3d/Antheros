# General Info
Elias is the part-owner of one of [[settlement/The Dell|The Dell]]'s fresh produce stands. A fervent Royalist, he uniformly blames the Guild government for all the city's current problems. Possesses excellent hearing, and a very good sense of smell, and is an astute observer of passersby.
