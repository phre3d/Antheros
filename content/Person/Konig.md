# General Info
An embittered man, seeking to wreak vengeance on the cause of his unhappiness. He and [[person/Valli|Valli]], the armorer located just up Ard Street and his former friend, wooed the same dwarven woman, who finally chose [[person/Valli|Valli]]. Konig is certain that [[person/Valli|Valli]] used some trickery or enchantment to secure Soranal's love, and has sworn to drive his now enemy to bankruptcy. Accordingly, he has drastically lowered his prices and hired large numbers of additional help to do so.
