# General Info
Ephi has lived among the patrons at The [[landmark/Rose Garden Inn|Rose Garden Inn]] longer than she can remember. After turning herself invisible one day, she followed a carriage through the main gate, and its company through the main doors. From that point forward, she has magically disguised herself by altering her appearance into a near-carbon copy of the inn's guests-or more specifically, their children. Occasionally she will take on the appearance of a chaste human female in search of courtship and dazzle in the ballroom; at others that of a distinguished gentleman so as to lounge in the smoking room. 

Hers is a genuine, insatiable thirst for innocent mischief. She particularly enjoys confounding guests to the point of utter bewilderment.
