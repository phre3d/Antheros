# General Info
Common thief. 

 A cutpurse and pickpocket, Custer's favorite trick is the use of a gaudy red hat to attract attention away from his face. To affect his disguise, he discards the hat, and fades into the crowds. His plain features and drab clothing also serve to make him less memorable. The ruse is so successful that Custer has had the chance to hone his pickpocketing skills until he rarely has to rely on the hat to save his skin.
