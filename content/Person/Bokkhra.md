# General Info
An exceptional Goliath, Bokkhra stands about 7'8" and weighs near 600 pounds; his skin is a slatey-gray and his eyes are red. Bokkhra's exceptional status is from his mental abilities - he is an esper, capable of reading emotions and, occasionally, sensing the meaning of the thoughts of the people around him. 

Presently, he is working in a saloon called [[landmark/The Snowblind Mouse|The Snowblind Mouse]], as the bouncer; the tavern has become known as a great place to go for a quiet drink since he began work there, since anyone who attempts to cause trouble is likely to leave rapidly, crashing through the swinging doors and rolling down the street. Bokkhra dislikes "troublemakers" because of the pain in the head they give him and he tends to remove the irritating presence as quickly as possible - usually by chucking the person bodily out of the tavern (or wherever else he happens to be). People swiftly learn to "think pretty" around him. 

He rents a room 'in a rambling pile of a building referred to as [[landmark/Duhm's Rooms|Duhm's Rooms]], but usually eats at the tavern. Bokkhra does like the city, for its variety and color, but wishes more people would "think pretty", so he could stop having "hurt in head".
