# General Info
Kuber lives and works in the deepest wilds of [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]]. His house includes a clinic in which he treats anyone who walks in the door (he has even been known to make house calls!), regardless of financial status (there ain't none in the Labyrinth). Kuber doesn't practice healing for the love of mankind (or any other race, for that matter). 

 He is totally interested in the spectrum of disease available in [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]]. At times he has been heard to comment that he has "seen diseases I would not have believed it possible for a person to contract. With what this one man had, he should have been dead 5 years ago!" 

 Kuber's house is given an unobtrusive guard by the [[organization/Thieves' Guild|Thieves' Guild]], who appreciate the work he is doing, whatever his motivation. 

 Anyone in [[settlement/The Labyrinth|The Labyrinth]] can give directions to Kuber's House.
