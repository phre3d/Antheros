# General Info
Spends most of his time sitting around the bar, drinking from his private stock. He leaves the serving of the regular ale and a peppery stew to the cook, [[person/Maggie|Maggie]].  He is a careful man, and will only deal with people he knows, or those who have some sort of good references. He often sells his merchandise to Alphonso Caradello, a member of the smuggling arm of [[organization/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]], although Nick himself is not a member of the Hand.
