# General Info
Conrad is a small ferret faced man and is one of the three day-shift assistant customs inspectors employed by the ministry of Ports and Shipping to collect customs duties along [[settlement/The South Docks|The South Docks]], under the direction of Inspector [[person/Valyri Pipper|Valyri Pipper]]. Alphonso Caradello attempts to arrange for Black Hand cargos to be inspected by Bissell  who places the proper seals and stamps upon the appropriate items. Nonetheless, there is only a 65% chance that Bissell will be the inspector on duty at any given time. Illegal Black Hand cargos, therefore, are usually well concealed, even when Caradello has arranged for Bissell to inspect the smuggler. 

 In exchange for his duplicity, Caradello pays Bissell an annual gratuity, and arranged for Bissell's sister to receive training and employment as a courtesan from [[landmark/The Black Whip|The Black Whip]] (small government officials, like beggars, cannot afford to be choosers).
