# General Info
A husky dwarf of middle age, he is so ugly that he keeps his face hidden from the world behind a bejeweled golden mask. He is quite an experienced adventurer, and still is interested in an occasional venture, if it is lucrative enough (i.e., if there is the promise of potent magical knowledge to be had at the end of the line).
