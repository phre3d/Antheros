# General Info
Theadus, an old elf, works in the store on weekends and makes a mean cup of coffee. During the week Theadus is a teacher at the College of Alchemy, the bungling professor type with spectacles and a nose-tuned voice box. He is the only surviving member of the Strathspey Regiment.
