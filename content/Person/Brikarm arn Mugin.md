# General Info
Brikarm is a dwarven accountant who works for Adrielle in the office from 9:00am to 11:00am every day. Among other duties, he forges the indenturing papers that she uses for her illegal slave trade.  He is also able to sell his work to [[organization/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]], since it is of such high quality.
