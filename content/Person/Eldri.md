# General Info
A talented, expert fighter who is qualified to teach any axe or hammer weapon, as well as the bastard and broadsword. Eldri can also train fighters in the use of battle-axe or war hammer up to expert level in these weapons after three months of extensive sessions, these sessions are only available to dwarves.
