# General Info
Skorindir is getting elderly for an elf, being the only on in HAven with white hair. He has lived all his long 430 years in Haven, and realizes that, if he wishes to live much longer, it is getting close to the time when he should escape to the Wold, returning to Nature, and retuning himself to the rhythms that enable the elves to endure for millennia. He is white-haired, an unusual sight in [[settlement/Erinhir|Erinhir]], but is straight as a sapling, and still strong.
