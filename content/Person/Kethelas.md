# General Info
Kethelas is a bull of a man with sufficient physical power to toss an unruly customer lightly over the garden wall into the alley. He has been the bouncer at [[landmark/The Blue Lantern|The Blue Lantern]] as long as Kinalla (the current owner), and has been her best friend and lover nearly that long.
