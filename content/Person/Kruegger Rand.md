# General Info
Brown-haired, brown eyed, and of medium height; he's a bit roly-poly, since he enjoys good food and fine wines. His appearance is a bit misleading, since few would credit him with as needle-sharp a mind as he has. In the evenings, Kruegger may be found at any of a number of different taverns, visiting friends and clients. He particularly favors [[landmark/Garduccio's|Garduccio's]], where many of the other financiers gather.
