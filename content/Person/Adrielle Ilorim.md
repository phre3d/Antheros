# General Info
Daughter of [[person/Firlirrel Ilorim|Firlirrel Ilorim]] and [[person/Lachoriel Ilorim|Lachoriel Ilorim]], Adrielle is a lovely young elven woman of middle height, with long black hair. She appears frequently to be totally absorbed in her own thoughts, even when dealing with customers. 

 Adrielle disappeared for a number of years when still a very young girl of 30, but eventually returned, and now runs a shop (Fine Silks and Fabrics, Erinhir) on Lorien Street specializing in silks and other fine fabrics. 

 She somewhat secretly funds the informal watch group calling themselves [[organization/Adrielle's Watch|Adrielle's Watch]]. How she comes by the money is a mystery.

Adrielle's shop has been under the scrutiny of the [[organization/Thieves' Guild|Thieves' Guild]] (as the object of a probable robbery) for some time. As a result of this, the guild has noticed that Adrielle has a second "business" - trade in human flesh.  Refer to scenario [[plot/Silks and Slavery|Silks and Slavery]].
