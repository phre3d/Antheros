# General Info
A greying, older, halfling who Lives with his daughter's family, Mardo can almost always be found either sitting on [[landmark/The Green|The Green]], holding forth at the Leaf at [[landmark/The Leaf and Jar|The Leaf and Jar]]. He is an outrageous [[person/Storyteller|Storyteller]], and loves to hear new jokes and riddles (preferably obscene). Hot-tempered, Mardo is frequently at odds with his daughter's husband over the latter's inability to hold a job; their bitter arguments often take place in public.
