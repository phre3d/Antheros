# General Info
Lucius, owner of [[landmark/The Dreamseller's|The Dreamseller's]], is a tall, cadaverously thin man, with sallow skin, a totally bald head, and deep, sunken eyes. His long robes are worn, and bear faded mystical runes. Lucius' fingers are gnarled and claw-like from some disease of the joints, and he walks with effort, leaning on an oaken staff.
