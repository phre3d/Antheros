# General Info
Common thief. 

 An Easterner, dark of skin and hair, speaks with a soft lilting accent and dresses in loose trousers, shirts, and a turban. More of a con-man than a thief, he is clever, a glib talker, and thinks fast on his feet. His thieving skills are good, but he considers burglary to be too much like work, and so makes his living running various scams. Always on the move, with no fixed abode, he is extremely difficult to find once the scam is revealed.
