# General Info
A stout man in a herald-style tabard and a staff, who struts about the Island, occasionally making announcements in a booming, clear voice. These range from bans of marriage, advertisements for one merchant or another, to help wanted and lost dogs. Bonyodel charges 5 CP for a given announcement of a limited duration, given twice, at opposite ends of the Island. No Insults, Profanity, or Foreign Tongues.
