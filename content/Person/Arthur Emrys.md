# General Info
The owner of [[landmark/The Pendragon Inn|The Pendragon Inn]] is a tall, brawny man and a former caravan drover. He retains good relations with old friends who travel the Long Road, and is an excellent source of the most recent news (8 weeks old at most) from other cities and towns along the trade routes.
