# General Info
Pauli and his four deputies are charged with keeping the civil peace within [[settlement/The Dell|The Dell]], and handle those disturbances that are too petty to warrant the Tower Guard's involvement. He is normally a pleasant, easygoing man, conscientious while on duty and not averse to a bit of drink and womanizing (he is a bachelor and considered quite a catch by the young halfling woman) when off the job. 

 The current situation has made him much more edgy and irritable than usual, and greatly increased the watchfulness of himself and his men - Pauli and/or his deputies are likely (80%) to approach any human traveling in [[settlement/The Dell|The Dell]] who is not either well-dressed, or accompanied by a halfling, with their weapons drawn, and harshly question him about his business in the area.
