# General Info
Paraggio, the master of one of [[organization/The Black Hand|The Black Hand]]'s two traditional thieving guilds is a slender, boyish man with dark hair and an angular face. His guild consists of some three cells of highly competent thieves who operate mostly in [[settlement/The Commons|The Commons]] and North Corridor.
