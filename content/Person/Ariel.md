# General Info
Followers are often artists and entertainers.

It should be noted that she is not a sex goddess; sex is a beautiful aspect of love, but isn't part of any specific rituals.

Also of note is the fact that her full parentage is not clear. Some say that [[person/Serindë|Serindë]] brought Ariel into the world alone since love and beauty are unknowable thus [[person/Ladros|Ladros]] can't be her father.
