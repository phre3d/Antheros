# General Info
Theresia, the mayor's wife, carries herself with a grace and poise that commands attention the moment she enters a room. Her appearance is carefully cultivated, showcasing her keen sense of style and the importance she places on her public image.   

Theresia's hair is always arranged elegantly, complementing her striking features and sharp, observant eyes that miss nothing. Despite her beauty, there's a calculative and ambitious gleam in her gaze, hinting at a keen intellect and a determination to ascend the social ladder at any cost.   

Her attire is of the finest quality, reflecting her aspirations and the status she believes she deserves. Theresia often wears dresses of rich fabrics adorned with subtle but expensive jewellery, indicating her taste for luxury and her desire to be influential and affluent.   

Her voice is smooth and persuasive, capable of persuading opinions and charming those around her, a skill she wields like a finely honed weapon in her social and political manoeuvrings.
