# General Info
Dunstan Herlihea is of average height, slender, red-haired, and black eyed. He makes most of the pipes available in his shop, carving them from seasoned briar, blackthorn, or cherry, sandstone, soapstone, or corn-cobs. His wife is names Colleen.
