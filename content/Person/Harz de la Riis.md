# General Info
A husky, well-muscled man with grey-streaked dark hair, and a deeply furrowed brow. Harz has managed to keep the de la Riis property intact while many of the other noble families have had to sell off their hereditary lands. To do so, however, he was forced to adapt to the changing economic system resulting from the rise of the Guilds, and diversify the family's activities from farming into related businesses like grain milling. 

He has been rewarded for these efforts by the hostility of his two sons, who accuse him of “selling out" to the Guildsmen and their lack of affection has robbed him of his zest for living.     

On formal occasions, Harz wears a large emerald ring engraved with the family crest; at all times, he wears a bronze medallion inlaid with a sunburst design of orange and yellow zircons (a present from his wife Claudia de la Riis). 

Harz is one of the 24 members of the Council of Boroughs, and spends about 50% of his time in the city. He is also a supporter of Watch Captain [[person/Lyza|Lyza]] and it was his support on council that got her command created, despite what [[person/Mikyl Swiftsword|Mikyl Swiftsword]] thought.
