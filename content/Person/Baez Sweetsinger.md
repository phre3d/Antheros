# General Info
Baez Sweetsinger drayed for some thirty years before he bought the [[landmark/Wagoneer's Delight|Wagoneer's Delight]] caravanserai he now runs and rather prefers catering to drayers than drovers but will not turn away any business. The tall, spare owner is in his sixties, but still hale and healthy, with hair only slightly grayed at the temples. He is often found in the compound talking with the waggoneers who drive the Long Road, reminiscing about old times.
