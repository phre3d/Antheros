# General Info
Waiter, cook and clean-up crew at [[landmark/The Leaf and Jar|The Leaf and Jar]], Furry is Laurel to his bosses Hardy. He is extrordinarily thin and tall for a halfling.  He is somewhat slow-witted, and has almost no hair on his feet or hands (resulting in his nickname).  He is occasionally the butt of practical jokes, but most of the patrons like the lad because of his eagerness to please. His two loves in life are whittling and his vegetable garden, which he tends with great care.
