## Alignment 
Since you are all members of the City Watch, the following alignments are fine.

  
  	- Lawful Good
- Lawful Neutral

  
  
  	- Neutral Good
- Neutral

  
  
  


If you can make a case for another alignment that doesn't involve somehow shafting the party or another player I'll consider it.

## Spells 
I'll be reviewing the spells to see if there's anything I foresee as world wrecking but don't expect to find any.  Also note that the [[Class/Physician/Physician|Physician]] has two class-specific spells.

## Leveling Up 
I expect to be using the milestone method of leveling, at least initially. This means that you will gain a level at set point in the plot. However, I don't know yet how this will work out if you choose to ignore my plotting and follow your own ;-)
