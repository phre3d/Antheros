# Special Abilities
- **Legendary Resistance (3/Day):**  When Roc Lobster fails a saving throw, it can choose to shout \"Roc Lobster!\" and succeed instead.
- **Siege Monster:**  Roc Lobster deals double damage to objects and structures.
- **Rock Camouflage:**  Roc Lobster can magically alter its appearance to resemble an ordinary rock. While in this form, it has advantage on Dexterity (Stealth) checks to hide.

---

# Actions
### Multiattack 
 Roc Lobster can make four attacks (claw x 4) or one Wing Attack  
### Lobster Claws 
  **Melee Attack:**  +13 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.
   **Hit:**  Damage scales with size (age)- **Immature (age <2):**   or 12 slashing damage.
- **Young Adult (age 2-25):**   or 18 slashing damage.
- **Middle Age (age 26-50):**   or 25 slashing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 10).
- **Old (age 50+):**   or 29 slashing damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it is grappled (escape DC 18).
 Roc Lobster can grapple up to two creatures simultaneously.  
### Wing Attack 
 Roc Lobster beats its wings, creating a blast of wind. Each creature within 15 feet of Roc Lobster must succeed on a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw or take 21  bludgeoning damage (half that if Immature)  and be knocked prone. Roc Lobster can then fly up to half its flying speed.

## Legendary Actions
Roc Lobster can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. It can use only one legendary action at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Roc Lobster regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn. - **Motion of the Ocean:**  Roc Lobster slams its lobster claw hands into the ground, causing a tremor. Each creature within 20 feet of Roc Lobster must succeed on a DC 18 Dexterity saving throw or take 21 (6d6) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone.
- **Red Snappers Snapping:**  Roc Lobster targets a creature it has grappled with one of its lobster claw hands. The grappled creature must make a DC 18 Strength saving throw or take 28 (4d8 + 7) slashing damage and be restrained until the grapple ends.
- **Dance Craze:**  Roc Lobster starts a mesmerizing dance, forcing creatures within 30 feet of it to make a DC 18 Wisdom saving throw or become charmed until the end of their next turn. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, hoping to break free from Roc Lobster's groovy influence.

# Description
Picture if you will a massive lobster with wings.  100' wings. Each. Of course that's only on older ones that have lived at least 100 years. Upon reaching maturity at the age to 2 they are about 6' long with 12' wingspan but their rate of growth slows greatly, to about 1' wingspan / 4\" length per year. Coloring tends to mottled green/red when younger to mostly red after about age 50. Similar to a normal lobster, they molt as they grow with a new, soft, chiton shell emerging as the old shell splits and falls away.  Inspired by [this Reddit thread](https://www.reddit.com/r/dndmemes/comments/13j53x2/its_the_roc_lobster/) and the artwork I created using [Night Cafe](https://creator.nightcafe.studio/).

## Suggested Environments
High cliffs near oceans or very large lakes.<br>
