# Description

## Fine Stones Traded and Sold

## Description

[[people/golbur|Golbur]] deals in both cut and uncut stones, purchasing them fresh from a dungeon haul or auctions, and selling them to carry their assets in a light, easily convertible form. [[people/golbur|Golbur]] is very good at discerning his customer's knowledgeability about gems and gem lore (90% chance he can tell if the customer is trained in jewelry or not). He will deal with those who know jewelry on a completely honorable basis. But will try to cheat a novice, and may even try to switch a worthless gem for a valuable one of the same type if circumstances seem propitious.  

 [[people/golbur|Golbur]] dresses rather foppishly, in brightly colored clothing of golds, reds and oranges. He has a personal weakness for gems of the same shade, and will pay 10-20% more for these stones than he will for others of the same quality (his preference should be noticeable to an attentive customer, who could exploit this knowledge in future visits).
