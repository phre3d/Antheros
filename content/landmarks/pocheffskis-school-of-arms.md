## Description 
[[people/pocheffski|Pocheffski]], a sallow-faced, squint-eyed man in his late fifties whose reflexes are still cat-quick despite the toll of advancing years on his vision is the Thieves Guild's Weapons Master. Of all the Guild's former inner circle (save [[people/azimuth|Azimuth]], of course), he has perhaps lost the most in the fire's aftermath. 

His "school" on Porcupine Alley is little more than an empty warehouse outfitted with a few ropes, tumbling mats, and sandbags, and he deeply mourns the loss of all the high-priced training equipment (the rough terrain and nighttime simulation areas, and the immense mechanized climbing apparatus) that populated his domain in Rogues' Roost. More significantly, [[people/pocheffski|Pocheffski]] is no longer permitted direct access to the Guild's armory of specialized and enhanced weaponry, and may only request such items through the auspices of [[settlements/the-labyrinth|The Labyrinth]] chapter's Master [[people/jurgan-bland|Jurgan Bland]] (an old crony of Duur's). 

Although these slights have made [[people/pocheffski|Pocheffski]] somewhat desultory about his teaching duties, he remains one of the truly expert authorities on the use of exotic weaponry (including garrotes, blowguns, and really unusual items like nunchaku) in the [[organizations/confederation-of-the-ten-cities|Confederation of the Ten Cities]] region. He maintains a small arsenal of such devices in the one-story flat he keeps as his residence.
