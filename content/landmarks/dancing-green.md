## Description

An open grassy park with scattered trees, Dancing Green is a fairground, market place, public garden and dancing meadow all in one. Tended by members of [[landmarks/the-abbey-of-eollana|The Abbey of Eollana]], it serves as a social gathering place for the Sindar elves and those of non-elvish blood who enjoy elvish revels. There is dancing and music on almost every clear evening during the warmer season, with special celebrations (sponsored by the various inns and businesses, and organized by the Abbey) on nights of the spring and fall equinox, the winter and summer solstices, and nights of the full moon. Sindar merchants frequently gather on [[landmarks/the-green|The Green]] to discuss business and women and children enjoy the fresh air, the trees, and perhaps the talents of a street musician, juggler, poet or [[people/storyteller|Storyteller]].
