# Description

## The raciest tavern in Haven

## Description

One can find members of almost all the world's races as customers of the Iron Horse at one time or another. The owner, a short and stocky, greenish hued, wolf faced individual with stumpy unusable wings, named [[people/gyr-windbreaker|Gyr Windbreaker]], is a multi-breed (dwarf-goblin- orc-avanthar) who is pleasant to all and finds animosity for none. The clientele tends to horse wranglers and stockmen, since it is so closely placed to the pens of [[landmarks/the-auction-block|The Auction Block]], but many potential purchasers of steeds and other animals drop in here for a pleasant flagon as well. Even [[people/heidel-varrasch|Heidel Varrasch]] comes in for a tipple now and then (especially when he's organizing an expedition and is looking for wranglers).
