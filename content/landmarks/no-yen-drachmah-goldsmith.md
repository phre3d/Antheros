# Description
## Money lent, collateral accepted 

## Description 
No-Yen lends money to major businessmen in the city, and to very well-known out-of-towners. No-Yen's office is on the first level of his home in [[landmarks/gold-alley|Gold Alley]], and he is generally available from 1:00pm 'til 8:00pm daily. His interest rates are reasonable (only 5% per month, compounded). He will loan up to 1,000gp to known borrowers with no collateral, and make maximum loans of 10,000gp with proper collateral. He has two Goliath "enforcers" who go out to collect overdue loans. They seldom have problems in collecting, either money or collateral. The two Goliaths are Kuori "Longleaper" Anakalathia and Vimak "Steadyhand" Anakalathia. They are brothers who were exiled from Krindor for attempted robbery.

## NPC Encounters 
[[people/kuori-anakalathia|Kuori Anakalathia]] | [[people/vimak-anakalathia|Vimak Anakalathia]]

## Special Options 
No-Yen may decide to make loans to player characters that have some sort of collateral to offer (magic items, property rights, etc.). This requires a saving roll against CHA in addition to collateral. There is a limit of 5,000gp on a first loan to an unknown, regardless of the amount of collateral.
