## Description 
The warehouse is used primarily for storing sacks of grain and other foodstuffs. It is a large building, of two floors, with an enclosed yard in which two wagons are often stored. The warehouse is also a stronghold of [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]. There is a secret entrance from the back alley into a staircase leading to secret rooms on the warehouse's second level. These rooms are used as meeting places and hideouts by [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]]. The owner, [[people/lazar-symbocca|Lazar Symbocca]], is a member of the Hand, and heavily involved in the smuggling trade. 

 Lazar is rarely at the warehouse. He is always said to be "returning shortly". [[people/esgar|Esgar]], the foreman, and his helpers, [[people/rimer|Rimer]], [[people/div|Div]], and [[people/jim-bostry|Jim Bostry]], are all Hand supporters, but do not know the extent of their boss' involvement. They would, however, be quick to report any suspicious strangers, particularly if the strangers were asking too many questions.
