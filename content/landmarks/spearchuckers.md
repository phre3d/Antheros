# Description
## Spearchucker's Bow 

## Description 
Surfey, or "spearchucker", is a real bow-lover who makes some of the best and largest bows around, with a high moisture tolerance and long life. Her specialty is composite longbows. These beautiful bows are so finely made that an excellent archer can send a shaft 800 yards. Functional, aimed fire is considerably less, but is still 50 yards greater than the average longbow. They also cost 500 Gold Pieces, with a waiting time of 2 months. 

She stocks a good number of lesser quality bows as well, with plenty of equipment. Her stock of arrows comes from Tedius and Sprig, who she views as the best fletchers in Haven.

If asked about the shop's name (her nickname) she will explain about the bet she won several years ago (see her character description).

## Hours 
9:00am to 5:00pm, after hours by appointment only

## Price Range 
Expensive

## Quality 
Excellent.

## Cash Box 
She has 10 GP on hand, hidden in an old bow case under the counter. 

## Staff 
She has two apprentices working with her, Nimble and Hasty, 40-year-old wood-elf twins that she occasionally sends on on long journeys to obtain the specialty wood she requires.

## Customers 
25% chance of another customer browsing her bows

## Outstanding Items 
10% chance there is a composite longbow in the shop that someone ordered but hasn't picked up yet.
