## Hospice for mercenaries

## Description

The sign hanging outside this large, sprawling 3- story complex of stone and half-timber is that of a knight lying on his tomb, with one sword beside him, and three more hanging over him. Run by [[people/marcus-ambrosius|Marcus Ambrosius]], a retired mercenary, the Four of Swords is primarily a hostel for mercenaries, providing rooms, simple meals, hospital care if required, and a bath-house. Most of the residents are members of one of the several [[organizations/mercenary-companies|Mercenary Companies]] that have become common sights in Haven of late, although such membership is not required for admittance.

 The hostel is a good place to hire additional muscle for dangerous expeditions, to make contact with some of the [[organizations/mercenary-companies|Mercenary Companies]], or to hire guards for caravans. The GM should note, however, that quality fighting men do not come cheap, and the more experienced the fighter, the more his services will cost (see [[settlements/the-south-corridor|The South Corridor]] for details on hiring mercenaries). The mercenaries are also a good source of information on the safety of roads, and the growing orc menace in the mountains.
