# Description

## [[people/florina|Florina]] of Lorinwood

## Description

[[people/florina|Florina]] is an Elven woman with violet eyes and hair of jet. She is one of the most beautiful women in the city, but her snobbish, and childish behavior creates numerous problems for her. She plays, but does not make, her flutes.

## Exterior

[[people/florina|Florina]]'s shop is a small, delicately made wooden structure with gracious carvings and frescos, with several large windows spaced evenly around the building. It is two storied, and the carvings on the second floor are even more beautiful and complex than those on the first floor. The windows are enchanted to make them very strong, and at night, when the store is locked, they will deliver an electrical shock of 2d12 damage to any but [[people/florina|Florina]] who happens to touch them.

## Interior

Inside, the store is equally as beautiful, but slightly more functional. The showroom occupies most of the ground floor, and consists mainly of racks displaying the merchandise. In the rear of the room is a small counter at which [[people/florina|Florina]] sits, playing a flute. The remainder of the ground floor is taken up with a small workshop where many of the flutes are made, and the staircase leading to the second floor. On the second floor are [[people/florina|Florina]]'s apartments, and a small bar at which [[people/florina|Florina]] entertains occasionally.

## Hours

10:00am - 6:00pm

## Price Range

Low/Costly

## Quality

Fair/Excellent

## Cash Box

[[people/florina|Florina]] keeps the cashbox secreted in a secret compartment behind her counter. She keeps most of her cash in gems for a better rate of foreign exchange with Elves. 4d10 in small gems, 2d8 in coins.

## Staff

Only [[people/florina|Florina]]

## Customers

65% chance of 2d3 customers at any time, and also a 25% chance of a hopeful suitor to [[people/florina|Florina]] hanging around making (less than subtle. [[people/florina|Florina]] doesn't like shyness) advances towards her.

## NPC Encounters

[[people/largo-profundo|Largo Profundo]]

## Outstanding Items

The majority of the flutes that [[people/florina|Florina]] sells are wooden and bamboo, but she usually has some very fine silver flutes which she imports from the Wold and other more distant lands. These flutes run at least 20% more than the regular cost as they are extremely well made.

## Special Options

The flute which [[people/florina|Florina]] plays is enchanted, and with it she causes customers to feel happy, and very interested in the merchandise. She realizes how dangerous this is, and only uses it occasionally (15% chance).
