# Lohisian Place

````col
```col-md
flexGrow=2.5
===
[[antheros/religion/lohi|Lohi]], Goddess of Healing isn't worshipped in the traditional manner but rather through acts of succor for the ill and injured and healing. This site at the east end of [[settlement/believers-lane|Believer's Lane]] is therefore not a temple per se but rather three buildings that house varrying specialties.

## Campaign NPCs 
- **Herman:**  Senior Healer. Looked after their case in [[plots/the-city-watch/tcw_-the-curse|The Curse]].
- **Raven:**  Half-elf acolyte; was working on intake when the party first met her
- **Shi'rin:**  High Elf acoltye; works intake
- **unnamed:**  male tiefling acolyte, working intake
- **unnamed:**  concierge where they stayed the night in [[plots/the-city-watch/tcw_-the-curse|The Curse]]
````
