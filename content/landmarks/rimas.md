# Description
## One of the smaller brothels on the street. 

## Description 
Dressed in open, loose satin or taffeta gowns or belted tunics, Rima's sexy ladies are very playful. All sizes and shapes, all colors of skin or fur, some have dirks or daggers. The house usually has a cash flow problem as they are often paid in gems, jewelry, clothes, pets or livestock. A short whistle from any girl will bring Rima to the rescue.
