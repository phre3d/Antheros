## Description 
[[people/browin-treebeard|Browin Treebeard]] is the owner of this shop, which makes miniature pewter figurines, depicting dwarves in a variety of labors (including a number of fighting poses), and a number of real and legendary beasts. Though Browin has the physical appearance of a dwarf, he is actually an alien being from another sphere which has a portal that opens onto this world on rare occasions. The shop is the only two-story aboveground structure in [[settlements/the-dwarrow|The Dwarrow]], and most of its neighbors view Browin and his employees as almost dangerously eccentric. Strange rumors abound that these figures sometimes move by themselves, acting in character with the form they depict (these rumors are, of course, unconfirmed, but anyone trying a figure of a dwarven thief or assassin is just asking for trouble, in my opinion). The shop advertises regularly in [[people/a.-gori-greataxe|A. Gori Greataxe]]'s newsletter, and there are also rumors that Browin is financing A. Gori's proposed expedition to find the bearded dwarven women (see Adventure Possibilities - The Dwarrow).
