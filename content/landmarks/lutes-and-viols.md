# Description

## [[people/alfio-credenza|Alfio Credenza]], prop.

## Description

The front room is small, and used for display of fine lutes, viols and psalteries. A bell on the door alerts Alfio when anyone enters. The shop employs eight journeymen and four apprentices. Only one of them, Nikklas, lives with Credenza's family upstairs.  

 Alfio has had a running feud with [[people/luciano-clarino|Luciano Clarino]] for 30 years, and will not abide even the mention of his name. He plans to marry his beautiful daughter Zofia to Nikklas in a few months, blissfully unaware that Zofia loves Derek Clarino, the son of his enemy, and Nikklas loves a different female every week. Nikklas, however, has no objection to the marriage because it will assure his position as heir.
