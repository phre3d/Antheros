# Description

Vorikooreis maintains an excellent stock of various medicinal herbs and spices. He can, and will, compound a healing draught, emetic, purgative, or minor potion for his customers on the spot (about 10 minutes). The potions work 10% of the time, the healing draughts 15% of the time - the purgatives and emetics always work.

For each additional 15 minutes he spends working on the compound, add 10% to the chance it will work (there is always a 20% chance that a given mixture will work in some totally unexpected fashion - GM's choice).

He will buy relatively scarce herbs and spices from outside sources, even if they are not the freshest available, and may commission a group to go in search of various plants.
