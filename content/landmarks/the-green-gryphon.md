# Description

## A quiet pub

## Description

The Gryphon is in a little better shape than neighboring buildings. It has a stone first floor and stucco and half-timber on the overhanging second floor with green shuttered windows. A gryphon is painted on the door in green paint.

 The proprietor of the Gryphon is [[people/marco|Marco]] d'Emil, a lean, sinister man garbed in black, dark haired and bearded, who has never been known to smile except after killing someone (rumor has it that killing is something that [[people/marco|Marco]] is very good at). Violence is very rare at the Gryphon, however, as [[people/marco|Marco]] has been known to have brawlers quieted - permanently.

 [[people/marco|Marco]] does no serving of customers, leaving that task to three attractive "barmaids", who also entertain customers upstairs - after clearing it with [[people/marco|Marco]] first, of course.
