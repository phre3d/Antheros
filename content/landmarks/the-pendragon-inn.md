## Description

The Pendragon Inn is one of several good quality inns located along [[settlements/the-south-corridor|The South Corridor]], for travelers journeying on the Long Road, and is a favorite of merchants. The Inn is large and well-kept. The food is good, the prices Fair (Quality Fair to Good), and the rooms are clean.

The innkeeper is a tall, brawny man called [[people/arthur-emrys|Arthur Emrys]], a former caravan drover. He retains good relations with old friends who travel the Long Road, and is an excellent source of the most recent news (8 weeks old at most) from other cities and towns along the trade routes. Time spent talking to the guests of the inn can reveal information on everything to the safety of the North Pass to the best places to stay in Mandalai, to the price of tea in Huy Lankh. There is a 30% chance of a room, or space in one of the common rooms. During holidays or Haven's summer trade fairs, this drops to 2%.  

 At present, the inn plays host to a number of interesting guests for the players to notice:

- [[people/nain-arn-dolbekki|Nain arn Dolbekki]] and Lady [[people/sigrid|Sigrid]], dwarves on a vital mission that is further described in  [[plots/free-city-of-haven/haven-scenarios|Haven Scenarios]]: [[plots/free-city-of-haven/scenarios/the-stoned-maiden|plots/Free City of Haven/Scenarios/The Stoned Maiden]].
- [[people/myrddin|Myrddin]], an itinerant mage whose real talents lie in clairvoyance. His ability with Tarot cards is truly amazing, but he is a reluctant prophet, and must be persuaded. If appealed to, [[people/myrddin|Myrddin]] will be able to give them valuable clues regarding the disappearance of Lady [[people/sigrid|Sigrid]].
- [[people/jelaine|Jelaine]] of Belakor, merchant, a tall, lean woman with short dark hair beginning to grey. She is a dealer in pearls and semi-precious stones. Her store of samples is cached in the Inn's safe. She will be looking for trustworthy guards in a few days to accompany her in a caravan going west to her home.
