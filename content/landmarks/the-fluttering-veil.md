## Description

The Fluttering Veil (known as the Tattered Veil to many of the locals) is a dirty, low class bordello, a sleazy imitation of the more elaborate establishments on [[settlements/the-street-of-silk-veils|The Street of Silk Veils]]. The bordello is run by [[people/rabon|Rabon]], a lecherous, sour faced man who cares little for his "girls", save for their bodies and the money they bring in. Not surprisingly, the Fluttering Veil has a high turnover, and [[people/rabon|Rabon]] is always on the alert for new "girls". Nor does it matter much to him how he gets them. He has been known to buy indentures, or use other illegal methods, such as kidnapping, to keep his bordello well-stocked.

 Most of the girls are human, with some chances of a tiefling or half-orcish girl (see Street of Veils for details on designing courtesans and bordellos. The Fluttering Veil is a LOW house).

 The bordello is in two adjoining row houses that have been made into one building. There is only one door, and all other doors and windows on the first floor have been boarded up. The first floor has a kitchen, a private "viewing" room, and a dimly lit pub that serves beer and cheap wine, where the girls are on display. Stairs lead up to girl's [[people/tiny|Tiny]] cubby-holes on the second floor, payment in advance, please. A bouncer is usually on hand to deal with unruly customers or uncooperative employees.
