# Description
## [[people/millar-lightfingers|Millar Lightfingers]], Prop. 

## Description 
The brewery, which sits at the edge of the cliff above the river Dorian, manufactures Starkerb beer, which is served on draft in many of the city's inns and drinking establishments. Millar is a chubby, extremely red-faced dwarf who samples his wares perhaps a bit more than would be politic. Although [[people/bethli|Bethli]]'s iron and copper smelter lies downstream from the brewery, many who have sampled Starkerb beer swear that Millar must somehow get his water directly from [[people/bethli|Bethli]]'s drain pipe.
