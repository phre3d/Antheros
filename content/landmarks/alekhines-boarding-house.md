# Description
## Cheap rooms, no questions 

## Description 
The three story tenement is frequented by sailors on shore leave in Haven, or between voyages. The rooms are sparsely furnished, and are far from luxury accommodations, but they are reasonably clean and cheap. No meals served. The landlord, [[people/alekhine|Alekhine]], is a shriveled, bald-headed old codger who attributes his longevity to his lack of curiosity about his boarders. As long as the rents are paid (in advance, please), he cares nothing for the doings of the residents. 

## Staff 
The only other employee is a sullen half-orc named [[people/kradoc|Kradoc]], who makes a half-hearted attempt to keep the place clean. He is nosier than his boss, and will not hesitate to eavesdrop on an interesting conversation, if the opportunity arises. Nor is he above petty theft, if he thinks he can get away from it.
