# Description

Tiko carries a respectable collection of second hand furniture. A fair portion of his stock is rejects made by the apprentices at Dortmund's and a couple of other furniture houses on the South Bank. Most of his stock is sturdy and usable, but suffers from minor design or manufacturing flaws.
