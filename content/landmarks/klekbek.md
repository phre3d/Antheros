# Description

## 'The Silver Mine' - Fine Metalworking

## Description

[[people/oin|Oin]] is an irascible old dwarf with wispy grey eyebrows and beard, and stormy dark eyes. His fingers are gnarled with age, but his hands retain much of their metalworking skill. [[people/oin|Oin]] conducts business dealings solely in the dwarven tongue (a fact hinted at by the dwarven store name), and non-dwarves must get along as best they can. His tone is fearsomely stern, and may give the impression that he is greatly insulted when a customer attempts to bargain. In fact, he loves to haggle, and a buyer will get a much better price if he perseveres. His works are classical in styling, and finely detailed- in his shop, one will find bowls, trays, ceremonial masks, rings and belt buckles. All of beaten silver and gold. [[people/oin|Oin]] also has a shop along the Arcade, where some of his most unique works are on display.
