# Description

## Workshop of the Artist

## Description

A tiny hole-in-the-wall, with dirty windows. Yet [[people/jasmine|Jasmine]] is a tattoo artist of renown, able to charge considerable amounts for her talents. She is a largish woman of indeterminate middle-age, with stringy brown hair, and is usually found in her shop, seated on a worn couch in front of a low table covered with needles, dye pots, etc. She pays local urchins to keep her apprised of the arrival of new ships in both the North and South docks. Her clientele consists primarily of officers, and the occasional seaman who has managed to save his meager earnings for a piece of her artistry.

 [[people/jasmine|Jasmine]] uses potent liquor as a painkiller when she works, and occasionally hears things not intended for her ears from her drugged clients. She rarely acts on this information, even though she is opposed to [[organizations/criminal/the-black-hand|The Black Hand]] because of their excessive brutality, which she considers bad for business.
