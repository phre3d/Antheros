# Description
The [[landmarks/hall-of-the-council-of-boroughs|Hall of the Council of Boroughs]] houses the main council chamber an many related offices.  Together with the [[landmarks/mayors-palace|Mayor's Palace]] next door, this complex is the core of Haven's government. 

> [!read-aloud] **Read Aloud** 
 >  
 > 
 > As you step into the grand Council Chamber, you're immediately struck by the air of authority that fills the space. Tall windows allow sunlight to pour in, casting a warm glow on the polished hardwood furniture and gleaming marble floors. The room resonates with a palpable sense of power, reflecting the weight of decisions that shape the community.
 >
