## Description 
Miranda is a woman of the Veil who left the houses of the Street of Silk Veils because she felt the life in the bordellos too restrictive. Now she has a tent on the Island where she plies the same trade, as she feels like it. Sometimes playing hostess to a particular man she favors for several weeks, or refusing all men when she wishes to be alone.
