# Description

## Where [[settlements/the-dell|The Dell]]'s most prominent citizens entertain the city's elite

## Exterior

A flagstone pathway leads off from Hillandale Street, and splits in two at the base of the hill. The segments lead to two elaborately carved cherry wood doors, 80 feet apart. The left hand door is engraved with the emblem of a mace held in a black leather glove, and the right hand door with a mercantile insignia. Only the face of the smial, and its living room, can be seen from street level.

## Interior

The house is organized into two completely and elaborately finished apartments occupied by Paladin and Rigaletto, respectively. At the center of the houses, the inner doors can be swung back to form a communal party room.

## Cash Box

Paladin sleeps with his money under his pillow. Rigaletto has a wall safe in his study with a complexity level 7 lock.

## Staff

In addition to the Brandybucks, an elderly halfling woman named Lissa Glass serves as housekeeper for the two men and lives on the premises. A chef named Ambo is called in to prepare the food for their frequent parties, Paladin, because of his Guard duties, will be absent from home several nights each week.

## NPC Encounters

[[people/rigaletto-brandybuck|Rigaletto Brandybuck]] & [[people/paladin-brandybuck|Paladin Brandybuck]] hold fairly large (12-30 person) dinner parties about once a month. The guest lists at these affairs are comprised almost totally (80-95%) of influential merchants from the Corridor, members of the Guild leadership, or key figures in the elven and dwarven communities.

## Outstanding Items

Paladin has, among his personal effects, the stuffed heads of several wild beasts (bagged in the dark wilds of the local taxidermist), a collection of cloth-bound volumes on military history, and a huge bronze statue of a halfling warrior in full fighting pose. Rigaletto of course has an expensive wardrobe of silks and velvets, and a number of beautiful masterworks culled from his shop over the years. He also owns an obsidian and jade chess set, and has a map of Haven and its surrounding territories that fills an entire wall of his study.
