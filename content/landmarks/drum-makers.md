# Description

## [[people/artemis-guisto|Artemis Guisto]], prop.

## Description

Drums of various sizes can be acquired from this store. Most of the work is done by the senior Journeyman, Artemis' son Gemmon, and two apprentices.

 Artemis is a pleasant, white-haired old man. He is well-loved by the children of the neighborhood for his storytelling. Children are in and out of the shop and the yard of the house next door, including some of the d'Larette and Profundo children. All call Artemis, and his wife Glory, their "grandparents".
