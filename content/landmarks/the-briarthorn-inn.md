# Description

## A rooming house

## Description

This three story house has been added to at numerous times, and is a conglomerate of [[people/brick|Brick]], stucco, and wood that badly needs painting. A sign in front proclaims, in faded paint, the name of the place. Beneath it, in newer paint, it reads: "Rooms - Cheap".

 The Briarthorn is run by a tall, broad-shouldered man with a scarred face known as [[people/calder-the-whip|Calder the Whip]]. He is a former highwayman, and is an expert with the bullwhip he wears at his hip. Calder does little of the work of the inn, except lounge behind his desk and collect rents. He employs four women to cook and serve mediocre meals in the common room, clean the place and entertain the guests as required. There is a 60% chance of a vacancy, and an 85% chance of rats, bedbugs, and other vermin. The thieves [[people/doc|Doc]] and Snake-eye, have rented a room here (see [[plots/free-city-of-haven/scenarios/the-stoned-maiden|plots/Free City of Haven/Scenarios/The Stoned Maiden]], [[plots/free-city-of-haven/haven-scenarios|Haven Scenarios]], Caravan Street) where they will bring [[people/sigrid|Sigrid]], keeping her wrapped in a blanket. They are hoping to find some way to use her power to enrich themselves (like renting her out as a guard dog).
