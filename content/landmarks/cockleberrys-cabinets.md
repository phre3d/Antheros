## Description 
The proprietor is [[people/willum-cockleberry|Willum Cockleberry]], a portly halfling with red hair and spectacles, with a taste for fine ale and bad puns (he's known as "Cackle-berry" behind his back). His sense of humor occasionally appears even in his woodcarving, such as flowers with [[people/tiny|Tiny]] impish faces or knot designs that appear hopelessly snarled. Commissions only.
