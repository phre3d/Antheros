# Chronosmith''s Timepiece
  Source: Penny Blood Adventures

**Item Type:** Wondrous Item<br>
**Rarity:** Rare<br>
**Price:** <br>
**Weight:** <br>
**Attunement:** Yes

---

# Properties
Once per long rest, the bearer can use an action to turn the timepiece's hands backward, allowing them to take back the last turn they made. This means they can redo their last action, movement, and bonus action. The state of everything else remains as it was at the end of their last turn.

Provides the bearer with an innate sense of time, always knowing the exact hour, minute, and second.

---

# Description
This pendant contains a miniature clock, its face protected by a transparent crystal. The hands move, but not in tune with the actual passage of time. Instead, they seem to dance erratically, yet somehow purposefully.
