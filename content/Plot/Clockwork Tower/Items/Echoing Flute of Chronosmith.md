# Properties
As an action, the user can play a melody to send a brief message (25 words or less) to their past self, giving them a hint or warning about a choice they're about to make. This gives the user advantage on one ability check, saving throw, or attack roll they make in the next hour. Once this property has been used, it can't be used again until the next dawn.

Bards can use the flute as a spellcasting focus.

---
# Description
This slender flute seems to shimmer in and out of existence, reflecting its bond with the echoes of time. Playing it releases hauntingly beautiful notes that linger longer than they should.
