# Special Abilities
**Temporal Phasing.**  As a bonus action, the Temporal Titan can phase out of time, allowing it to move through other creatures and objects as if they were difficult terrain. It takes 5  force damage <u>if it ends its turn inside an object</u>. It remains phased out until the end of its next turn.   **Immutable Form.**  The Temporal Titan is immune to any spell or effect that would alter its form.

### Temporal Disruption (Recharge 5-6) 
 The Temporal Titan emits a <u>wave of temporal energy in a 30-foot radius</u>. Each creature in that area must succeed on a <u>DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be affected by the slow spell for 1 minute</u>. Affected creatures can make another Wisdom saving throw at the end of each of their turns, ending the effect on a success.

# Description
The Temporal Titan stands as a testament to both the prowess of artifice and the mysteries of time. Standing at <u>over 15 feet tall</u>, this construct is crafted from a blend of sturdy metals and delicate clockwork components. Its body pulses with the soft glow of  *Earthheart Emeralds* , and ghostly gears are visible, phasing in and out of existence within its frame. As it moves, faint echoes of ticking, like a colossal clock, accompany its steps.   The most impressive feature is its capability to manipulate time in its vicinity, a feature embedded within its very mechanics. Some say looking upon the titan is like staring into the past and future simultaneously, a bridge between what was, what is, and what might yet be.
