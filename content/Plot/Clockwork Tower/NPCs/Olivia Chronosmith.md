# General Info
Born into the illustrious Constantine lineage, Olivia has always been acutely aware of her family's grand history. From a young age, the tales of her ancestor, [[person/Constantine Chronosmith|Constantine Chronosmith]], sparked a fire of curiosity and fascination within her. An aspiring artificer herself, Olivia sees her ancestor not only as a role model but also as a challenge, an unreachable apex of skill and genius she is determined to match.

Olivia is young and relatively inexperienced, but she possesses a keen intellect and a natural aptitude for understanding complex mechanisms and arcane formulae. She has turned the ground floor of her family's ancestral home into a workshop where she works tirelessly, inspired by her ancestor's creations.

Despite her obsession with her work, Olivia is far from a recluse. She's kind-hearted, outgoing, and fiercely loyal to her friends. She's also deeply proud of her family history and is eager to aid anyone seeking to understand the Clockwork Tower, not only for the good of Haven but also in the hope that the tower's secrets might help her emulate her revered ancestor's feats.

## [[plot/Plot: Clockwork Tower|Plot: Clockwork Tower]] 
Olivia is a helpful NPC, her artificer abilities and inherited knowledge of the tower make her an invaluable ally. She's best used as a source of information, a facilitator for acquiring rare components, or a helper in puzzle-solving rather than as a combatant.
