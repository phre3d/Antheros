# Description
Buttercup is a large breed dog (think Bernese Mountain dog) that was found wandering alone  by [[Plot/Mimic Mayhem/npcs/Rosy Rumblegarden|Rosy Rumblegarden]] and [[Plot/Mimic Mayhem/npcs/Mazlo Merryshadow|Mazlo Merryshadow]] on their journey. They quickly took her in and named he Buttercup. 

She is friendly one she gets to know you but wary of strangers.
