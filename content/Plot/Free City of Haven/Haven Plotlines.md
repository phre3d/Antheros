# Haven Plotlines

The are two types of plot element in this section:

- **[[Plot/Free City of Haven/Street Encounters|Street Encounters]]:**  Small random encounters on the streets of Haven
- **[[Plot/Free City of Haven/Haven Scenarios|Scenarios]]:**  Larger encounters that take longer to play out and can be mini adventures in themselves

These have been take from the original The Free City of Haven setting and modified as appropriate for this world.
