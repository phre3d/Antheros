# Description
While the details and size may vary, these items are all obsidian pendants that depict a unicorn in ivory. Richer members of the sisterhood will generally have spent more on their pendant's creation while others will posses one more simple. No matter the provenance, they will all be blessed by the local Mother, which ibues the item with its magical ability. 

These are usually only worn by members of the Sisters of the Unicorn, more specifically, ones who are sexually chaste.  One of the pair of pendants was commissioned and worn by [[person/Adrienne Geronde|Adrienne Geronde]] and [[person/Marita Maggia|Marita Maggia]].
