# Description

Driver, [[Plot/The Gypsy Train/NPCs/Scordraal Pakmydis|Scordraal Pakmydis']] brother

Ambrosis, age 40, is the father of twin sons [[Plot/The Gypsy Train/NPCs/Aldor Pakmydis|Aldor]] and [[Plot/The Gypsy Train/NPCs/Albar Pakmydis|Albar]]. His wife died four years ago during an attack on the caravan, and he still mourns her loss.

Ambrosis is a 3rd-level human fighter, chaotic neutral, with Str 15, Int 9, Wis 13, Dex 13, Con 13, Cha 10, AC 5, and 18 hp. He wears chain mail and carries a _short sword_, a _short bow_ with 12 _arrows_, and a _dagger_.
