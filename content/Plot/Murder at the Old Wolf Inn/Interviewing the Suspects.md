# Interviewing the Suspects

A key part of figuring out who murdered [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Artemis Nahanna|Artemis Nahanna]] is gathering useful information by talking to the NPCs. Almost everyone at the Old Wolf Inn has something to hide – for one reason or another. 

The statistics of all the NPCs are listed in the core rulebooks, but the DM can modify these as they wish. 

The suspects are:
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Cael Thraelive|Cael Thraelive]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Enoch the Birdwhisperer|Enoch the Birdwhisperer]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Hugo von Silverton|Hugo von Silverton]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Inga Broadhammer|Inga Broadhammer]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Jon Callion|Jon Callion]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Kara Tanner|Kara Tanner]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/Vittra Northdaughter|Vittra Northdaughter]]
- [[Plot/Murder at the Old Wolf Inn/NPCs/William Fastfinger|William Fastfinger]]
