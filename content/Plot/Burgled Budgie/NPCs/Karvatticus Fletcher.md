# Karvatticus Fletcher
Gnome Ranger, CG

>[!read aloud]
>This little brown gnome is small and wiry, with a lithe build. His dark brown skin shines with rusty-red iridescence when the light hits at the right angle. His face is framed by a light brown hair, and his golden eyes crinkle with a friendly  smile. He wears a dark green tunic and brown vest with black trousers. A bow and quiver full of arrows are slung on his back and a shortsword is belted to his waist.

Karvatticus is friendly and affable, especially when talking about animals. He loves them and is skilled at convincing them to come back to town with him. He often doesn’t know their proper names, so he gives them fanciful ones based on their coloration and habits. He has a crush on [[Plot/Burgled Budgie/NPCs/Pemma Stoutfoot|Pemma Stoutfoot]].
