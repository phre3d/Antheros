# Tibbles
Cat Familiar, N

>[!read aloud]
>This little orange tabby has white paws and green eyes that sparkle with uncanny intelligence. A black collar of tooled leather is fastened around her neck.

Tibbles is friendly and intelligent, with a childlike innocence. She can’t tell differences between humanoids easily. She enjoys being pet, playing, and rubbing on people’s legs. She runs away if the PCs act hostile towards her.

She can speak aloud thanks to an enchantment on the collar. She won’t willingly let anyone touch the collar, as it’s her only way to communicate with people. Tibbles is a normal cat who has been made into a familiar using this spell a couple of years ago. She doesn’t remember much about her “normal” life before she had the collar and a master, and she can’t offer any details on the process that granted her sentience.

Her collar is a [[Plot/Burgled Budgie/Items/Collar of Communication|Collar of Communication]].
