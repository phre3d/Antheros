# Thomas (farmer) 
![[Thomas.webp]]Thomas, a farmer, is a straightforward and warm-hearted individual. He is terrified that his children might never wake up again, and he is willing to do whatever it takes to save them. In his desperation, he even considers facing the Hag alone to secure a cure for his kids. He earnestly pleads with the players to lend their assistance in this dire situation.

Thomas is a tall, broad-shouldered man, and the constant field sunlight weathered his skin. He wears a small hat, brown trousers with a suspender and a shirt, complemented by a modest grey moustache.



[anchor|lanita]

# Lanita (priestess of Ariel) 
![[Lanita.webp]]Lanita, a priestess of Ariel, is a warm and smilingwoman in her thirties. Her skin tone suggests she comes from the south, perhaps the Endless Dunes. Sold as a slave in her youth, she grew up in a noble's house. There, she encountered a priestess of Felicida who noticed Lanita's ever-bright nature. Recognizing her potential, the priestess took Lanita to the temple, where she devoted herself to spreading joy and love to everyone she meets.

Standing at around 5 feet 5 inches tall, Lanita has darkish skin and long black hair with streaks of grey. She willingly heals the party for free if they ask, using her powers as a priestess.





[anchor|rico]

# Rico (inkeeper) 
![[Rico.webp]]Rico, the innkeeper, is a sizable human who delights in eating, talking, and drinking. He's cheerful and always ready for a lively conversation or a drinking challenge. An intriguing offer hangs on the inn's wall: if anyone can devour a hefty 4-pound steak accompanied by a loaf of bread within an hour, they can enjoy a complimentary day's stay on Rico's tab. (Constitution roll DC 15) .
