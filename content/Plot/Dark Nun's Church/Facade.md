# Outline
The Facade is a small room with no lights. Any member of the team with dark vision will be able to see that there is a D6 number of Blind Rats hiding in the shadows. Each rat has had its eyes burned from its skull. They are feral and will attack anyone coming into the Facade. There are three barrels 
filled with sweet wine in the Facade.

> [!read-aloud] **Read Aloud** 
 >  
 > A foul urine smell hangs in the air as you enter the Facade. There are no lights. You hear tiny squeaks.


# Monster 
Five [roll:1d6+2] ![[1422317]]|Blind Rats

# Treasure 
## Barrels filled with wine 
The wine in the barrels is delicious. The Dark Nuns make it themselves and use it as an offering to their dark gods. One glass of wine is the equivalent of <u>1 hour of rest</u>. 

The wine <u>is addictive</u>. Each person must make a <u>Wisdom Saving Throw or become drunk</u>, causing them to sing loudly and dance in the darkness. Use the Random Encounter table to determine who hears the singer and comes to investigate.
