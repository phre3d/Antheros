# Outline
The right Transept contains two of the magic books used to reanimate bodies. These books should be destroyed or taken away from the church's sisters. Be careful when handling the books. There are two books, and the person picking up the books will need to roll a 1D4. If the number rolled is 4, the trap, a Close Shave, will be activated. The trap can only be activated once.

# Magic Item 
>[!Item]- Volumes 1 and 3 of Books of the New Living 
>![[Plot/Dark Nun's Church/Items/Books of the New Living|Books of the New Living]]

# Trap 
## Close Shave 
**Trigger:**  When someone picks up either one of the “Books of the New Living” without leather gloves.

**Effect:**  +11 to hit against all targets within a 5 ft. arc, 4d10 slashing damage

**Countermeasures:**  A successful <u>DC 15 Intelligence (Investigation) check</u> will know that the books are possessed. Wearing leather gloves will protect the player from activating the trap when handling the books.
