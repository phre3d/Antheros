# Description
A liquid formed but the fermentation of various plants. Two common forms are wine, made from various fruits (primarily grapes) and beer, made from different grains.

---

# Properties
## Physical Characteristics
Colour depends on the fermentation inputs. Strong scent.

## Properties
Highly flammable

## Origin
Created by [[Profession/Vintner|Vintners]], [[Profession/Brewer|Brewers]], and [[Profession/Alchemist|Alchemists]], depending on the intended use.

---

# Usage
## Common Use
The most common use is as an intoxicant to aid in having a good time or drowning one's sorrows. 
Also used by [[Profession/Herbalist|Herbalists]] as a base for making  *common*  potions.

## Manufacturing / Products
Can be distilled to further increase the concentration, producing various products such as whisky, gin, vodka etc.
